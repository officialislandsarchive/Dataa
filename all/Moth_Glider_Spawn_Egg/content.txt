Moth Glider Spawn Egg A moth glider spawn egg is a spawn egg that can be used to spawn the Moth Glider Pet. It cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Harcore Mode [ ]

Getting the Plant Protection Agency achievement by killing 300 void crop eaters in hardcore mode will award the player with a moth glider spawn egg.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

To spawn a moth glider, the player must be the owner of the island they are currently on. While holding the egg, clicking or tapping anywhere on the player's screen will spawn a moth glider.
