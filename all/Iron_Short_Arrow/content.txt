Usage [ ]

Ammunition [ ]

Iron short arrows are used as ammunition in shortbows , as at least one is required to be in the player's inventory in order for the shortbow to fire. Shooting an iron short arrow deals an extra 13 - 18 HP to mobs .

Iron short arrows are a one-time use item , meaning they cannot be retrieved after they have been shot.
