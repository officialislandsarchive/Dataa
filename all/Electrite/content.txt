Electrite Electrite Rock Spawns Buffalkor Island Description A conductive material used to craft electrical equipment. Found in the caves of Buffalkor Island. Electrite is an item used to craft electrical items primarily in the electrical workbench .

Obtaining [ ]

Mining [ ]

Electrite rocks naturally generate on Buffalkor Island in the ravine . Mining an electrite rock yields 1 - 2 electrite after each stage and 3 Mining XP (15 with the 4x daily bonus), resulting in a total of 2 - 4 electrite and 9 Mining XP (30 with the 4x daily bonus) per rock.

Electrite can be used to upgrade the electrical workbench to levels 2 and 3.
