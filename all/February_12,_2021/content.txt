February 12, 2021 Basic Information Day released February 12, 2021 Version number 34 Version Navigator Previous version Next version February 5, 2021 February 19, 2021 The February 12, 2021 update, also known as the Diamonds update, is a major update to Islands. It introduced a new hostile boss, Kor , along with new ores, tools, weapons, and lots more.
