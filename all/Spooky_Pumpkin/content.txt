Spooky Pumpkin Type of block Solid block; Luminous Best tool Luminous Transparent A spooky pumpkin is a furniture block that has a chance to be obtained from cauldrons .

Obtaining [ ]

Cauldrons [ ]

Opening a cauldron has a 7% chance of rewarding a spooky pumpkin, having a rarity of 'uncommon'.
