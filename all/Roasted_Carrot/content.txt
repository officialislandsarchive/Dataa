Roasted Carrot Recipe 1 Carrot Crafted with Campfire Small Furnace Desert Furnace Industrial Oven Heals 20 HP Sell price 24 - 36 coins Hot sell price 36 - 54 coins A roasted carrot is a food item obtained by cooking carrots .

Obtaining [ ]

Cooking [ ]

Cooking a carrot in a campfire , small furnace , desert furnace , or industrial oven will create a roasted carrot.

Input Output 1 Carrot 1 Roasted Carrot

Usage [ ]

Crafting ingredient [ ]

Image Name Recipe Crafting station Skill required Roasted Honey Carrot 4 Roasted Carrot 4 Jar of Honey Cooking Table Food Processor Level 28 Cooking

Eating [ ]

Eating a roasted carrot restores 20 HP.

Selling [ ]

Roasted carrots can be sold to Petur for 24 - 36 coins each, or 36 - 54 coins if roasted carrots are a hot offer.
