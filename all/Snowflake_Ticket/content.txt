Snowflake Ticket Dropped by Evil Gingerbread Description A ticket obtained from the Christmas 2022 Event. Use to claim rewards from the Christmas merchant Snowflake tickets are the currency of the Christmas 2022 event, granting the player the ability to purchase various event items. It cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Evil gingerbreads are guaranteed to drop 3 - 5 snowflake tickets upon defeat.

Snowball fights [ ]

x-x snowflake tickets will be dropped per round. The amount increases the more kills you have, and if you won.

Usage [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Players can purchase various items from Eddie using snowflake tickets.
