Sweet Rice Cakes Recipe 3 Rice 1 Maple Syrup Bottle Crafted with Cooking Table Food Processor Heals 25 HP Sweet rice cakes are a food item that heals 25 HP when consumed. It can be crafted without any Cooking level requirement.

It currently cannot be sold to Petur .
