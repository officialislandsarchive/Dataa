Seaweed Seeds Planted on Pond Planter Description Placed in a pond instead of soil. Seaweed seeds are seeds used to grow seaweed .

Obtaining [ ]

Fishing [ ]

Seaweed seeds can be obtained from fishing using any fishing rod with the rarity of "rare".

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting seaweed will give 1 - 4 seaweed and 1 seaweed seed.

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Seaweed seeds can be planted in pond planters to be grown into seaweed.

Trivia [ ]

Despite showing that seaweed seeds are a merchant offer unlocked at level 39 Farming in the Farming skill roadmap, seaweed seeds can only be obtained from fishing . They are the only seeds that can be obtained from fishing. In real life, seaweeds do not produce seeds. When holding seaweed seeds, one fourth of the green icon turns white.
