Yak Spawn Egg Buy cost Tier 1: 15,000 coins Required skill Tier 1: Level 49 Animal Care (to buy) Description Spawn a yak with tier 1/2/3 trait maximums. Eats radishes. The yak spawn egg is a spawn egg used to spawn yaks on player islands . There are 3 tiers of the egg; one can be bought while the other two can only be obtained through breeding yaks.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Tier 1 yak spawn eggs can be purchased from Taliyah for 17,500 coins each after reaching level 49 Animal Care .

Breeding [ ]

Any tier of this egg can be obtained by breeding two yaks. To breed yaks, the player must feed them each a potion of fertility during the day (in-game). The traits of the two animals will determine the tier of the spawn egg.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

Players can only spawn yaks if they have not reached the large animal limit and are the owner of the island they are on. While holding the egg, they can click/tap anywhere on their screen to spawn a yak.
