Sparkler A sparkler is an item that could have been obtained during Islands' Third Anniversary .

Obtaining [ ]

Quests [ ]

The player can obtain 20 sparklers by completing the "Let the Sparks fly!" quest that was available from April 14, 2023 to April 28, 2023 .

Usage [ ]

Spark-making [ ]

The player, while having a sparkler selected in their inventory, can click (on PC) or tap (on mobile) to create spark particles.

History [ ]

April 14, 2023 Added sparklers. April 28, 2023 Sparklers are made unobtainable.
