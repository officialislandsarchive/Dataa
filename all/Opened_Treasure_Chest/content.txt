Opened Treasure Chest Type of block Solid block; Interactable Best tool Luminous Transparent Description An empty treasure chest The opened treasure chest is a storage block that is obtained by opening a treasure chest .

Obtaining [ ]

Treasure chest [ ]

After opening and collecting the rewarded item from a treasure chest , the chest will automatically become an opened treasure chest.

Breaking [ ]

Breaking any type of opened treasure chest will reward the normal opened treasure chest, regardless of island variation.

Usage [ ]

Storage [ ]

An opened treasure chest can hold 9 stacks of 60 items, adding up to a maximum of 540 items total.
