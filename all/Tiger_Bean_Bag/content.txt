Tiger Bean Bag Type of block Solid block; Seat Buy cost 10,000 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent The tiger bean bag is a furniture block that can be purchased from Yuzi and opening red envelopes .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

The tiger bean bag can be purchased from Yuzi for 10,000 coins .

Red envelope [ ]

Opening a red envelope has a 7.14% chance of rewarding a tiger bean bag.

Sitting [ ]

The tiger bean bag has a seat, which means players, mobs , and villagers are able to sit on the bean bag.
