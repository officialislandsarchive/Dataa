Spring Build Trophy Type of block Solid block Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Limited Spring 2022 Trophy celebrating amazing builders! The spring build trophy is a block obtained by winning the Spring Build Competition, an event hosted in the official Islands Discord server from March 1, 2022 - March 21, 2022. Only a total of three copies were given out to the winners.

It cannot be dropped, traded , placed on another player's island , or put in vending machines and chests .

Obtaining [ ]

2021 Spring Build Competition [ ]

The spring build trophy was awarded to the top 3 builders in the Spring Build Competition in the Islands Discord server. The three final winners were:

Makramee11111 (1st place) mlsoh (2nd place) EAGLEEYE9010 (3rd place)
