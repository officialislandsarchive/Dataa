Dirt Pile Type of block Solid block Spawns Near trees Best tool Drops Grass Luminous Transparent

A dirt pile is a block created when pigs dig for truffles .

Obtaining [ ]

Dirt piles cannot be obtained as an inventory item. Pigs dig close to trees and create dirt piles at the digging location.

Breaking [ ]

Breaking a dirt pile will give the player a piece of grass and not the block itself.
