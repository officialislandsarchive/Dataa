Truffle Avocado Toast Recipe 1 Avocado Toast 1 Black Truffle Crafted with Cooking Table Food Processor Effect +20% farming crop yield Required skill Level 70 Cooking Description Consume for +20% farming crop yield. A truffle avocado toast is a food item that grants +20% farming crop yield when consumed.

It currently cannot be sold to Petur .

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating a truffle avocado toast grants +20% farming crop yield for 15 minutes. The effect only affects the drops of crops harvested by hand or sickle and not their seeds .
