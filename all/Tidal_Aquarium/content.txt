Tidal Aquarium Type of block Solid block Buy cost 64 Tickets Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Rare decoration prize from a fishing festival!

The tidal aquarium was a block purchasable during the Fishing Festival 2021 event. It could be bought from Oscar for 64 Tickets , Oscar's special currency.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

The tidal aquarium could be purchased from Oscar for 64 tickets. Tickets were obtained by participating in the Fishing Contest on Fishing Festival Island , during the Fishing Festival 2021 event.
