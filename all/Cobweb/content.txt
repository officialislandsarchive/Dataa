Cobweb Type of block Solid block Best tool Luminous Transparent A cobweb is a furniture block that has a chance to be obtained from cauldrons .

Obtaining [ ]

Cauldron [ ]

Opening a cauldron has a 10% chance of rewarding 6 cobwebs, having a rarity of 'common'.
