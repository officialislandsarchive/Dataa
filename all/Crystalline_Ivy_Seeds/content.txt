Crystalline Ivy Seeds Planted on Farmland Buy cost 3,500 Coins Required skill Level 55 Combat Crystalline ivy seeds are seeds that can be grown into crystallines .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Crystalline ivy seeds can be purchased from Maria for 3,500 coins each after reaching level 55 Combat .

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a crystalline crop will give 1 - 3 crystallines and 1 crystalline seed, with a 5% chance of getting an additional or extra seed. This chance may be increased for owners of the PRO Gamepass .

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Crystalline ivy seeds can be planted on farmland to grow into crystallines.
