Islands Anniversary Cake Type of block Solid block Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Celebrate 1 year of islands with a delicious birthday cake! The Islands anniversary cake is a decorative block given out to players on April 10, 2021 .

Obtaining [ ]

Taliyah [ ]

Taliyah visited players' islands during Islands' 1 year anniversary . After talking to her, the player would receive the Islands anniversary cake.
