Jerry Can The jerry can is an unimplemented item in Islands .

Information [ ]

Jerry cans were added on June 15, 2020 , but were left unused. It is unknown what its purpose would've been, but it was likely to store a new kind of fuel. The jerry can uses the same model as water jugs .
