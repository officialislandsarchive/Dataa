----------------------------------------

Blocks are placeable objects that exist outside the player's inventory . Players can use blocks to build up their island .

List of blocks

Technical blocks

Technical blocks are blocks which serve various purposes during events within the game, or are unreleased/unused in-game. These blocks cannot be obtained in the player's inventory through normal gameplay and can only be spawned in through the use of exploits or by a developer.
