Usage [ ]

Transporting maple sap [ ]

Wood tubes can be connected from tree taps to a sap boiler and from the latter to a syrup bottler . Wood tubes will only appear full if they are connected on both sides. Two wood tube pathways can be merged into one using a wood tube merger .

Trivia [ ]

There is a visual bug where fluid within wood tubes will appear black instead of their usual color when a wood tube merger is connected to another wood tube merger.
