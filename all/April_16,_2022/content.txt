April 16, 2022 Basic Information Day released April 16, 2022 Version number 78 Version Navigator Previous version Next version April 9, 2022 April 22, 2022 The April 16, 2022 update, also known as the orb update, is an update in Islands that added personal orbs , and boss orbs .

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Ruby Skorp Claws Added Infernal Flames Added Personal Orbs Black Personal Orb Green Personal Orb Yellow Personal Orb Blue Personal Orb Lime Personal Orb Purple Personal Orb Pink Personal Orb Cyan Personal Orb Orange Personal Orb Red Personal Orb Added Boss Orbs Slime King Orb Kor Orb Wizard Boss Orb Bhaa Orb Fhanhorn Orb Infernal Dragon Orb Added new treasure chests Slime Island Treasure Maple Isles Treasure Spirit Island Treasure Buffalkor Island Treasure Wizard Island Treasure Desert Island Treasure

Modifications [ ]

Regular treasure chests can now be found on Pirate Island Treasure maps may now locate treasure chests on Slime Island , the Maple Isles , Spirit Island , Pirate Island , Buffalkor Island , Wizard Island , or Desert Island Fixed the appearances of andesite , diorite , and granite rocks Reduced the damage dealt by Fhanhorn's pull attack by 25% and increased its periodic healing time from 1.6 seconds to 2.3 seconds

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed a bug causing some treasure chests to be too deep within the ground to be dug up Fixed Xbox controls Fixed a bug causing pink rabbits to crash Roblox Fixed a bug preventing players from traveling to Fhanhorn's spawn area . Fixed tai nigiri showing up twice in the Cooking skill roadmap Fixed an issue with Kor’s stomp attack Infernal fish are now able to be placed into fish tanks Infernal fish, spirit fish , and puffer fish now appear in fish tanks Corrected the appearances of duck and turkey spawn eggs Fixed an area in the Hub with an incorrect collision box Fixed a visual error where lava appeared on Slime Island Fixed the incomplete lake on the Slime Island Fixed the torches in the mines not being lit Art frames are no longer affected by gravity

Removed [ ]

Taliyah no longer comes to player islands to grant an Islands second anniversary cake Removed decorations in the Hub celebrating Islands' second anniversary

April 18, 2022 [ ]

Bug fixes [ ]

Boss orbs now display the boss's name in the teleporter UI Boss locations can now be used to teleport players from their own islands Fixed orientation to face the summoning entrance for every boss when teleported to Renamed "Diamond Mine Island" to "Diamond Mines" for the Diamond Mines orb teleport display
