The May 4th, 2020 Update (Floral Update) is the fourth major update in Islands. This update includes the Floral Bench and Starter Pack gamepasses, and Crop Totems could be automated.

Changes [ ]

Added [ ]

The Floral Bench is now accessible by buying the corresponding gamepass. Crop Totem now outputs to Conveyor belts. Trees now have ambient leaves particle effects. Starter Pack can now be purchased on the store. You can now sell Slime Balls to Ivon (Adventurer).

Modified [ ]

The Wooden Sword no longer falls into the void when dropped. Exploit fixes. Increased the size of Slimes.

Trivia [ ]

Roblox Island's popularity rose on the release of this update. The most famous YouTubers known for making Roblox Islands videos released their first videos on the game after the intial release of this update.
