May 20, 2022 Basic Information Day released May 20, 2022 Version number 82 Version Navigator Previous version Next version May 13, 2022 May 27, 2022 The May 20, 2022 update is an update in Islands that added the blast furnace , other cut fish , and various other changes based on community opinions.

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Blast Furnaces . Added Cut Catfish . Added Cut Carp . Added Cut Trout . Added Fish and Chips .

Modifications [ ]

Fish now spawn in water wherever the fishing rod's bobber is. Players can now walk around while fishing. Casting a fishing rod onto a block that is not a body of water will now retract it. Improved fishing performances. Changed Catherine 's model. Tier 1 bait no longer requires level 15 Fishing to craft and use. Steel rods now require 20 steel ingots to craft, down from 25. The industrial smelter now requires 1 pink sticky gears to craft, down from 3. Increased the sell price of carrots from 21 coins to 25 coins. Increased the sell price of dragon fruits from 45 coins to 52 coins. Updated the sound when mining flowers .

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed a bug that prevented the minigame from ending when unequipping a fishing rod. Fixed the upside-down bobber and reworked the fishing line physics. Fixed other bugs related to fishing . Fixed a UI issue when looking in industrial chests . Fixed entities getting stuck in blocks on player islands . Fixed the Return to Home button not appearing when visiting other player islands. Fixed a bug that prevented fishing from working in private servers.
