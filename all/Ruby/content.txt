Ruby Dropped by Azarathian Serpent Bhaa Description A rare find from defeating Bhaa , the desert boss. A ruby is an item used to craft ruby-themed items.

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

The Azarathian Serpent has a 5% chance dropping a ruby upon defeat.

Bhaa has a 10% chance of dropping a ruby upon defeat.

Trivia [ ]

This and gold ingots are the only minerals that can be dropped by a boss . In the "Desert Heat" quest , rubies are referred to as "Rubys" instead of "Rubies."
