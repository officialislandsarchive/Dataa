February 19, 2021 Basic Information Day released February 19, 2021 Version number 35 Version Navigator Previous version Next version February 12, 2021 February 26, 2021 The February 19, 2021 update, or the Animal Mounting update, is an update in Islands. It introduced a new sheep skin and a new feature.

Changes [ ]

Additions: [ ]

Animals can now be mounted after reaching level 8 Animal Care . Added the "Birthday" sheep skin costing 399 Robux .

Modifications [ ]

Added confirmation prompt when selling animal. Cutlasses and cactus spikes can now be placed in the weapon stand . Fixed a bug that caused the cactus to grow too fast. Increased server performance when there are many animals. Improved the FPS of low-end devices on large islands. Steel presses and copper presses no longer dispose copper or steel when switching molds. Fixed neon items glowing too brightly. The "Lunar New Year" cow skin can no longer be purchased.
