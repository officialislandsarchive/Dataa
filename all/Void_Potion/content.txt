Void Potion Recipe 3 Amethyst Crystal 1 Empty Potion Bottle 5 Void Parasite Crafted with Brewing Stand Effect Smoke Resistance Description Consume for Smoke Resistance. A void potion is an item that grants Smoke Resistance when consumed. Drinking the potion will not return the potion bottle .

Crafting [ ]

Name Recipe Crafting station Void Potion 3 Amethyst Crystal 1 Empty Potion Bottle 5 Void Parasite Brewing Stand

Usage [ ]

Drinking [ ]

Drinking a void potion grants the player 30 seconds of Smoke Resistance . Smoke Resistance grants 50% damage resistance to void smoke .

History [ ]

August 13, 2022 Added void potions. August 20, 2022 Void potions now require 5 void parasites to craft.

Trivia [ ]

Despite giving 50% damage resistance to void smoke, its true damage resistance is actually 72%. Before the August 20, 2022 update, there was a glitch where the void potion would heal 2 HP every second.
