Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Small and tall braziers can be purchased from Ivon for 100 and 150 dungeon coins each respectively.

Dungeon chest [ ]

Opening a dungeon chest has a 3% chance of rewarding a gold small brazier or gold tall brazier, having a rarity of 'rare'.
