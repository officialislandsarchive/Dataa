Wool Vacuum Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe 1 Medium Chest 1 Pink Sticky Gear 1 Gearbox Crafted with Animal Bench Required skill Level 47 Animal Care Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Automatically pulls wool off of sheep . Outputs wool onto conveyor after 60 seconds. The wool vacuum is an industrial machine that automates the process of shearing sheep .
