DV Trophy Type of block Solid block Best tool Luminous Transparent Description DV Trophy The DV trophy is a trophy of DV Plays , a youtuber who plays games made by Easy.gg among other Roblox games.

Obtaining [ ]

DV trophies are not obtainable through normal gameplay and is instead handed out by DV Plays during certain activities such as build contests. On August 21, 2021 , DV was given around 11k copies of the trophy [ 1 ] .

Trivia [ ]

On December 29, 2021 , any player who had more than 5 DV trophies in their inventory , including DV himself, was reduced to only have 5 DV trophies due to a patch on a duplication exploit . This and the DV plushie are the two DV-themed items in the game.
