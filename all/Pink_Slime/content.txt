Pink Slime Health 100 HP Behavior Hostile Attack strength 5 HP Drops Pink Slime Ball Pink Sticky Gear Ancient Slime String Slime King Token Slime Queen Token Jukebox Disk Location Slime Island A pink slime is a hostile mob and the rarest variant of slimes .

Spawning [ ]

Pink slimes have a 5% chance of spawning on Slime Island , which is accessible through the portal in the Hub located next to Ivon or through a Slime Portal .

Drops [ ]

A pink slime rewards 3 Combat XP (9 with the 4x daily bonus) and 3 Light Melee , Heavy Melee , Magic , or Archery XP (9 with the 4x daily bonus) depending on the weapon the player used to defeat it.
