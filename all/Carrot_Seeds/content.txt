Carrot Seeds Planted on Farmland Buy cost 500 coins Required skill Level 18 Farming Carrot seeds are seeds that can be grown into carrots .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Carrot seeds can be purchased from Cletus in the Hub for 500 coins each after reaching level 18 Farming .

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a carrot crop will give 1 - 3 carrots and 1 carrot seed, with a 5% chance of getting an additional seed. This chance is increased to owners of the pro gamepass .

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Carrot seeds can be planted on farmland to be grown into carrots.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of carrots can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer . If watered and fertilized, the growth time will be shortened by 30%.
