Empty Potion Bottle Dropped by Wizard Lizard Wizard Boss Sell price 20 coins Required skill (to sell) Level 20 Combat An empty potion bottle is an item dropped by the wizard lizard and Wizard Boss .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Wizard lizards are guaranteed to drop 1 empty potion bottle upon defeat and has a 50% chance of dropping 2, and the Wizard Boss is guaranteed to drop 15 empty potion bottles upon defeat.
