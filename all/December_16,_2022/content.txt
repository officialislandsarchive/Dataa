December 16, 2022 Basic Information Day released December 16, 2022 Version number 111 Version Navigator Previous version Next version December 2, 2022 December 23, 2022 The December 16, 2022 update, also known as the Christmas Event 2022 Update I, is a major update in Islands that added content for the Christmas 2022 update.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Party menu. Added Eddie . Added new furniture blocks: Added Santa plushies . Added gnome plushies . Added elf plushies . Added penguin plushies . Added reindeer plushies . Added white reindeer plushies . Added Christmas Event 2022 Trophy . Added gnomes . Added Santa Stockings. Added Elf Stockings. Added Snowman Stockings. Added Snowflake Stockings. Added Cookie Stockings. Added candy cane fences . Added candy cane light fences . Added candy cane scepters . Added penguin pet eggs . Added evil gingerbreads . Added Christmas present 2022s . Added ATMs .

Modifications [ ]

Projectiles can now be shot in any direction from the player through ranged weapons . Increased the sell price of carrots from 21 coins to 27 coins. Decreased the sell price of onions from 30 coins to 25 coins.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed bug causing collision issues with whiteboards and blackboards .

December 20, 2022 [ ]

Added a free present drop for the winning team in snowball fight . Added an "Assists" stat to snowball fight. Made changes to the reward tickets to account for eliminations + assists in snowball fight. Reduced the required eliminations to win in a snowball fight from 30 to 25. Buffed evil gingerbread mob damage from 12 to 16. Buffed evil gingerbread movement speed. Reduced evil gingerbread jump height. Added death markers for enemy and teammates. Fixed bug causing players to move after death.
