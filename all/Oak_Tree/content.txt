Oak Tree Oak Tree 1 Oak Tree 2 Oak Tree 3 Type Oak Tree Sapling Cost 50 coins Growth Duration 90 seconds The oak tree is a type of tree in game. Each player starts with 3 of these trees when they start the game. It drops Oak Wood and an Oak Sapling when chopped down or 2 leaves when harvested with leaf clippers .

Like any other species of trees, it must be placed 7 or more blocks away from another tree to grow.

Drops [ ]

When chopped, it drops:

3 - 4 Oak Wood 1 Sapling

When harvested by leaf clippers , it drops:

2 Leaves

Trivia [ ]

Unlike most trees, the oak tree can grow into different shapes. The oak tree was originally known as the starter tree.
