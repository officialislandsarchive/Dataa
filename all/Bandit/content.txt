Bandit Health 170 HP Behavior Hostile Attack strength 12 HP Drops Bone Bone Block Gold Ingot Crystallized Obsidian Jukebox Disk Location Pirate Island Jungle A bandit is a hostile mob that resemble small skeletal pirates wielding a cutlass and a pirate hat.

Spawning [ ]

Pirate Island [ ]

Bandits spawn in the jungle on Pirate Island .

Infernal Dragon [ ]

Bandits used to be able to be summoned by the Infernal Dragon before September 12, 2021.

History [ ]

July 17, 2021 Added bandits. September 11, 2021 Bandits can be summoned by the Infernal Dragon in-battle. September 12, 2021 The Infernal Dragon can no longer summon bandits in-battle. June 3, 2022 Bandits now have a chance of dropping doubloons upon defeat.

Trivia [ ]

Bandits resemble the skeleton mob from the Halloween 2020 event but with different accessories. A bandit can be seen as the icon for a save slot . It is referred to as "Skeleton Pirate" in the combat catalogue . Bandits share the same magical resistances and weaknesses as Zombie Cletus .
