Crystalline Seed cost 3,500 coins Planted on Farmland Sell price 35 coins Edible A crystalline is a crop that grows on farmland from crystalline ivy seeds .

It has various uses, some of them are listed below!

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a crystalline ivy yields 1 - 3 crystalline, 1 crystal line ivy seed , and 12 Farming XP (48 XP with the 4x daily bonus).

Growth [ ]

The growth time of crystallines can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer . If watered and fertilized, the growth time will be shortened by 30%.
