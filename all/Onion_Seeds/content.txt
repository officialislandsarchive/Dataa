Onion Seeds Planted on Farmland Buy cost 2000 coins Required skill Level 31 Farming Onion seeds are seeds that can be grown into onions .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Onion seeds can be purchased from Cletus in the Hub for 2,000 coins each after reaching level 31 Farming .

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting an onion crop will give 1 - 3 onion and 1 onion seed, with a 5% chance of getting an additional seed. This chance is increased with higher Farming levels and to owners of the pro gamepass .

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Onion seeds can be planted on farmland to be grown into onions.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of onions can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer . If watered and fertilized, the growth time will be shortened by 30%.
