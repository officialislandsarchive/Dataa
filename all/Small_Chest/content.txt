Small Chest Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe 25 Oak Wood Crafted with Workbench Tier 1 Workbench Tier 2 Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4 Best tool Luminous Transparent Description A small storage container The small chest is a storage block that can be used to hold up to 180 items . The small chest is the cheapest and smallest storage block in the game.

Usage [ ]

Storage [ ]

If a player interacts with a small chest a UI will appear along with their inventory . If that player clicks/taps on an item in the chest, those items will added to their inventory. Likewise if they click/tap on an item in their inventory thirty of them will be added to the chest (If there is less than 30 of that item all of them will be moved). Any player can interact with a small chest and see its contents, but the player must have build permission to take or add items.
