Usage [ ]

Animal bed [ ]

When placed down, chickens and ducks will lay eggs and duck eggs respectively in the nest, while vultures will drop feathers inside after being fed carp or their favorite food . They may sleep on the nest during nighttime. The nest can hold up to 3 eggs at once. Eggs can be manually placed into nests, but duck eggs and feathers cannot.
