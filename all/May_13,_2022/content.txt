May 13, 2022 Basic Information Day released May 13, 2022 Version number 81 Version Navigator Previous version Next version April 29, 2022 May 20, 2022 The May 13, 2022 update, also known as the fishing update, is an update in Islands that revamped many fishing mechanics.

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Tier 2 Bait .

Modifications [ ]

New animations and sounds now play when fishing . Added a new mechanic when fishing. Fish now spawn on Buffalkor Island and Wizard Island . Added fish and treasure rarities. Changed fish and spirit fish shadows in bodies of water . Fishing rods now have additional properties: Decay Slow: Better fishing rods preserve the player's fishing progress. Renamed bait to tier 1 bait . Modified various skill requirements: Level 25 Fishing now unlocks the crafting recipe for fish tank shipwrecks . Level 30 Fishing now unlocks the crafting recipe for fish tank anchors . Level 35 Fishing now unlocks the crafting recipe for fish tank castles . Level 40 Fishing now unlocks the crafting recipe for fish tank treasure chests . Leveling up the Fishing skill now increases the chance of finding treasure by 0.1% per level. Owners of the pro gamepass are granted a bonus 3% chance of fishing treasure. Tier 1 bait now increases the player's fishing speed by 15% on average. Candy apples now increase the player's fishing speed by 25% on average. Orange honeycombs now increase the player's fishing speed by 15% on average. Fish tank blueprints can no longer be obtained. Renamed talapia to "Tilapia." Changed the recipe of the aquamarine sword to require 2 crystallized aquamarine instead of 2 pearls . Changed the recipe of the washing station to require 1 crystallized iron instead of 3 pearls . Players may now reset their player any island . Reduced the sell price of starfruit from 67 coins to 60 coins. Reduced the sell price of watermelon from 80 coins to 75 coins. Increased the sell price of kiwis from 24 coins to 32 coins. Greatly improved the performance in the Hub . Re-added Day/Night cycles to the Hub. Other performance improvements.

Fish price changes [ ]

Fish Old price New price Trout 3 coins ➡ 20 coins Blue tang 20 coins ➡ 35 coins Spirit fish 100 coins ➡ 200 coins Tuna 900 coins ➡ 1,000 coins Shark 450 coins ➡ 1,000 coins

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed a bug causing the bobber of fishing rods to apply centripetal force to the player. Fixed the collision box of the lights in the Hub. Melody can no longer appear inside the ground. Fixed visiting friends' islands on mob islands.

May 14, 2022 [ ]

Fixed a bug preventing fish from being fished. Fixed issues with missing audio. Fixed a bug preventing the Infernal Dragon from spawning and not showing its spawn cost. Fixed a bug causing players who jump off the Underworld not to teleport back to the Underworld. Fixed basic fertilizer not going to the inventory when fished. Fixed a bug causing fishing related quests not to save.

May 15, 2022 [ ]

Fisherman Thomas has been returned to the Hub. Fixed issues with fishing up fertilizers . Improved fishing rarities. Fixed an issue with recognizing highest tier bait effect. Fixed a bug causing fishing quest progress to not get recorded.
