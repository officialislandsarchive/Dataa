An opened cauldron is a storage block that is obtained by opening a cauldron .

Obtaining [ ]

Cauldrons [ ]

After opening and collecting the rewarded item from any cauldron , the cauldron will automatically become an opened cauldron.

Storage [ ]

A used cauldron has 9 slots that can fit up to 90 items per slot, adding up to a total of 810 items per cauldron. This applies to both cauldrons.
