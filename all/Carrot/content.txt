Carrot Carrot Oily Seed cost 500 coins Planted on Farmland Sell price 21 coins Required skill Level 18 Farming (to sell) Oily description A crop that is covered in motor oil. Must be washed before selling. Edible A carrot is a crop that grows on farmland from carrot seeds .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a carrot plant yields 1 - 3 carrots, 1 - 2 carrot seeds , and 12 Farming XP (51 with the 4x daily bonus). The chance of obtaining more than one carrot per plant is increased by the player's Farming level and for owners of the VIP gamepass . The 5% chance of obtaining an extra carrot seed is increased for owners of the pro gamepass .

Totems [ ]

A carrot totem will collect and replant carrots in a 3-block radius, which can then be collected by interacting it. A carrot totem placed next to a conveyor belt will drop the carrots onto the conveyor, but doing so will make them oily. This process cannot yield extra carrot seeds.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of carrots can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer . If watered and fertilized, the growth time will be shortened by 30%.

Cooking [ ]

Carrots can be cooked into roasted carrots in any heating device except for the industrial smelter .

Input Output 1 Carrot 1 Roasted Carrot

Eating [ ]

Carrots can be eaten to restore 10 HP.

Animals [ ]

Holding a carrot will attract nearby pigs and horses to the player. They are also the pig's and horse's favorite food and can be used to feed either of them.

Selling [ ]

Carrots can be sold to Cletus for 21 coins each after reaching level 18 Farming .
