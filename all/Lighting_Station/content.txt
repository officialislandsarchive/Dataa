Gamepass [ ]

The gamepass can be purchased from the in-game Robux shop UI for 149 Robux . Or via the Islands' Roblox game page under the store tab. Upon purchasing the gamepass, the player will be given one free lighting station, the ability to craft in lighting stations, and the ability to craft the station itself.
