Andesite Andesite Rock Type of block Solid block Spawns Hub Best tool Luminous Transparent Andesite is a block that can be mined from an andesite rock .

Obtaining [ ]

Mining [ ]

Andesite rocks naturally generate in the mining zone in the Hub . Mining an andesite rock yields 2 - 4 andesite after each stage and 3 Mining XP (6 with the 4x daily bonus), resulting in a total of 4 - 8 andesite and 9 Mining XP (18 with the 4x daily bonus) per rock.

Totem [ ]

Andesite totems can be purchased from Arius in the Hub for 8,000 coins . An andesite totem will spawn andesite rocks around it when placed, or if placed next to a conveyor belt , the totem will drop andesite directly onto the conveyor.

Transforming [ ]

Andesite can be transformed into andesite bricks in a stonecutter or an industrial stonecutter or smooth andesite in a polishing station or a industrial polishing station .
