Pumpkin Bed Type of block Solid block Best tool Luminous Transparent The pumpkin bed is a furniture block that has a chance to be obtained from cauldrons .

Obtaining [ ]

Cauldron [ ]

Opening a cauldron has a 4% chance of rewarding a pumpkin bed, having a rarity of 'rare'.
