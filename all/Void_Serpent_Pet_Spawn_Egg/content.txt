Void Serpent Pet Spawn Egg The Void Serpent pet spawn egg is a spawn egg used to spawn a Void Serpent pet . It cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Serpent eggs [ ]

Opening a serpent egg has a 1% chance of rewarding a Void Serpent pet spawn egg, having a rarity of 'mythic'.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

To spawn a Void Serpent pet , the player must be the owner of the island they are currently on. While holding the egg, clicking or tapping anywhere on their screen will spawn a Void Serpent pet.

History [ ]

August 20, 2022 Added Void Serpent pet spawn eggs.

Trivia [ ]

The Void Serpent Pet Spawn Egg has the highest drop rate out of all pet spawn eggs at 1%
