Aquamarine Sword Type Light Melee Physical class Slashing Attack damage 18 HP (Base) +9 HP (Critical) Recipe 200 Aquamarine Shard 2 Crystallized Aquamarine Crafted with Anvil The aquamarine sword is a light melee weapon in the slashing class.

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

Name Recipe Crafting station Aquamarine Sword 200 Aquamarine Shard 2 Crystallized Aquamarine Anvil

Attacking [ ]

At level 1 Light Melee , the aquamarine sword deals a base damage of 18 HP and can be swung every 0.5 seconds. It has the ability to damage multiple mobs in one swing, leaving a blue trail with its large attack range. There's also a small chance of landing a critical hit when using this weapon, meaning the attack will deal an extra 9 HP of damage. Its damage can be increased by leveling up the Light Melee skill and it is in the slashing class.

Trivia [ ]

This weapon has the greatest range out of all melee weapons . Prior to October 1, 2022 , the aquamarine swords in a tool stand lay flat instead of standing upright. There is currently a bug that causes the aquamarine sword to deal extreme amounts of knockback towards mobs when hit many times in a short time span while frozen from the frost swords or frost hammers .
