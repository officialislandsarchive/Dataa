Witch's Broom The witch's broom is a mount that was added in the October 15, 2022 update. Players can ride it to receive a 65% speed boost.

Obtaining [ ]

Quest [ ]

Completing the "The Wicked Witch" quest will unlock the witch's broom for usage.

Usage [ ]

Mounting [ ]

Players can mount the witch's broom by selecting the bicycle button next to the backpack button. Once mounted, players will receive a 65% speed boost whilst on it. The speed boost can stack with other items that provide speed boost as well. Players can manually dismount the witch's broom or when they receive damage. Dismounting it will lose the speed boost, but the witch's broom will linger around the player so they can mount it again.

History [ ]

October 15, 2022 Added witch's brooms. October 16, 2022 Removed the 5 second cooldown on mounting when receiving damage.

Trivia [ ]

The witch's broom is the second-fastest mount, with the fastest being horses .
