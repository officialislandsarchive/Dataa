August 13, 2022 Basic Information Day released August 13, 2022 Version number 94 Version Navigator Previous version Next version August 6, 2022 August 20, 2022 The August 13, 2022 update, also known as the Void Isles Update, is a major update in Islands that added the Void Isles , the Void Serpent , new weapons , and new materials.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added the Void Isles . Added the Void Serpent . Added void hounds . Added Larry . Added Bobalok . Added amethyst rocks . Added amethyst crystals . Added amethyst blocks . Added void stone rocks . Added void stone blocks . Added piles of void sand . Added void sand blocks . Added piles of void grass . Added void grass . Added void potions . Added serpent's fangs . Added serpent's scales . Added serpent's hooks . Added serpent's banes . Added void serpent tokens . Added Void Isles orbs . Added Void Serpent orbs .

Modifications [ ]

Slightly modified the Desert Island terrain to fit the Void Isles portal . Desert Island now has a more sandy visual atmosphere. The Underworld now has a reddish atmosphere. Added a new Void Isles-themed questline . Updated the island loading screen UI. Teleporting to different islands now indicates the targeted destination. Quests are now searchable in the quest log . Increased the player's maximum health from 296 to 300 if their Combat level is 99. Lowered the health of the Infernal Dragon from 30,000 HP to 17,000 HP. Renamed void blocks to "Respawn Block". Increased the sell price of dragon fruits from 45 coins to 50 coins. Decreased the sell price of oranges from 20 coins to 16 coins.

August 14, 2022 [ ]

Fixed a bug that broke animations for many entities. Fixed a bug that caused the Void Serpent to get stuck when being summoned. Corrected the orientation of ore rocks and piles in the Void Isles .

August 15, 2022 [ ]

Fixed a bug that caused mobs to die multiple times. Fixed a bug that allowed Kor to spawn extra diamond rocks upon defeat. Reduced the damage reduction scale in boss fights. Increased the Void Serpent's health to 50,000. Reduced the drop rate of serpent's fangs and serpent's scales from the Void Serpent. Fixed server memory issues.
