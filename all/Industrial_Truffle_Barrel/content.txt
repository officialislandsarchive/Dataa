Usage [ ]

Truffle [ ]

The industrial truffle barrel can be used to automatically collect truffles from pigs when ready. It has x slots able to hold x truffles each, and when a pig has gathered truffles, it will automatically go towards the industrial truffle barrel to collect truffles, provided that its maximum capacity has not yet been reached. The player will receive x Animal Care XP (x with the 4x daily bonus).
