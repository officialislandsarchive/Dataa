Ore rocks (or unofficially, nodes ) are resources in Islands that can be broken to drop minerals or stone depending on the type of rock mined. Some rocks have a chance to drop rare items when fully mined.

Trivia [ ]

If any small rock has been left untouched for a long period of time, it grants more drops upon being broken. Electrite rocks used to spawn on the Hub Mountains , but this was changed in a later update so that they only spawn on Buffalkor Island . Electrite and enchanted diamonds are the only rocks that have a special glowing effect. Stone , coal , iron , and gold rocks used to spawn on Wizard Island before the April 4, 2021 update. The textures of the diorite , andesite , and granite ore rocks appear to have been swapped and do not match that of the actual block. Prior to the April 29, 2022 update, ore rocks would sometimes spawn inside of blocks .
