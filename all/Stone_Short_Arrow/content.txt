Usage [ ]

Ammunition [ ]

Stone short arrows are used as ammunition in shortbows , as at least one is required to be in the player's inventory in order for the shortbow to fire. Shooting a stone short arrow deals an extra 8 - 11 HP to mobs .

Stone short arrows are a one-time use item , meaning they cannot be retrieved after they have been shot.
