Industrial Merchant Teleseller Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe 200 Red Bronze Ingot 10 Steel Plate 1 Pink Sticky Gear Crafted with Workbench Tier 4 Best tool Luminous Transparent The industrial merchant teleseller is an industrial machine that automatically sells items to villagers at 90% of the original price without needing to go to the Hub .

Usage [ ]

Shop [ ]

To automatically sell items to villagers , the player must select which villager they would like to sell to and the item they would like to sell. Once selected, the player can input the selected type of item through the conveyor belt at the front, and it will begin to sell the items at a rate of one per second. The coins earned can be withdrawn manually by clicking "Withdraw." The amount earned is 90% of the original profit after rounding.

Trivia [ ]

The industrial merchant teleseller is referred to as the "merchant teller seller" in its interface. The villagers in the teleseller are referred to their tag instead of their names. The only villager players are unable to sell items to in the teleseller is Petur due to his prices and offers changing everyday. Prior to January 8, 2022 , the industrial merchant teleseller was the only way to sell oily crops. It is also the only way to sell watermelons and pineapples off-season without turning it into crates .
