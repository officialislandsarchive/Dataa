June 10, 2022 Basic Information Day released June 10, 2022 Version number 85 Version Navigator Previous version Next version June 3, 2022 June 18, 2022 The June 10, 2022 update, also known as the spirit spellbook update, is an update that added spirit spellbooks and other small changes.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Spirit Spellbooks . Owners of the VIP or pro gamepass may now toggle Free Cam mode. Added many new Info Booklets . Added an "Unban All" option to the Visitor Permissions UI . Re-added Green Spirit Essence

Modifications [ ]

Quest rewards are now displayed in the quest details. Quests can now be tracked by clicking on the star button next to it. A doorbell sound now plays when another player joins the player's island . Improved mobile controls for Magic weapons. Moved Jade to an entrance to the Hub Mines near the Art Museum , Jade also has a new model, separate from Jed . Resource islands of any type are now capped to 15. Increased the sell price of watermelons from 80 coins to 85 coins. Decreased the sell price of carrots from 25 coins to 17 coins. Decreased the sell price of blueberries from 24 coins to 20 coins. Increased the volume of the "honk" command. Decreased the volume of the "arf" command.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed slime blocks not being bouncy. Fixed animal favorite foods not showing all foods. Fixed text in info booklets aligning off-screen. Fixed an issue where a spell charge would cancel if another spell was already cast. Fixed a bug where having a painting or statue in the inventory would prevent chests from functioning.

June 11, 2022 [ ]

Fixed green spirits not giving green spirit essence when caught. Spirits now drop spirit jars based on rarity.

June 15, 2022 [ ]

Fixed an issue regarding the Spirit Island orb's recipe.
