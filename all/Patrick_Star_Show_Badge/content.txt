Patrick Star Show Badge Obtained when Completing all quests in the Patrick Star Event Description Complete all quests during the Patrick Star Show event! The Patrick Star Show Badge is a badge that can be obtained by completing all of the event quests during the Patrick Star Event .

Obtaining [ ]

To obtain the badge, the player must complete Patrick's quest , which requires the completion of Bunny's , Cecil's , GrandPat's , and Squidina's quests and speaking to Patrick afterwards. The badge is awarded along with the Patrick Star trophy when the player has helped all members of the Star family.
