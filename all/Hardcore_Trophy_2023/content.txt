Hardcore Trophy 2023 Type of block Solid block Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Hardcore Trophy earned through achievements The hardcore trophy 2023 is a trophy awarded for defeating all bosses in hardcore mode .

Obtaining [ ]

Achievements [ ]

Completing the "Master Hardcore Killer" achievement by obtaining all Boss-Killer Hardcore achievements rewards a hardcore trophy 2023.

Trivia [ ]

Despite being a tradable item, trading is disabled in hardcore mode , effectively making the trophy untradable. In order to obtain this item, a minimum of 139 bosses must be defeated: 50 Slime Kings ; 30 Wizard Bosses ; 20 Bhaa ; 20 Kor ; 5 Fhanhorns ; 2 Infernal Dragons ; 10 Slime Queens ; and 2 Void Serpents .
