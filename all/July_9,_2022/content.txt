July 9, 2022 Basic Information Day released July 9, 2022 Version number 89 Version Navigator Previous version Next version July 2, 2022 July 16, 2022 The July 9, 2022 update, also known as the the chrysanthemum update, is an update in Islands that added chrysanthemum flowers and other quality of life changes.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Chrysanthemum flowers . Added Chrome Glass Blocks .

Modifications [ ]

Updated the anti-cheat system. Mundane daffodils and mundane tulips can now be bred. Moved chat bubble behind the inventory UI. Treasure maps are now labeled with the island name. Increased the health and damage of the Slime King . Changed the texture of snow in the Hub . Reduced the number of maximum players in each server. Tool stands can now hold ruby staffs and static scepters . Treasure maps can now be discarded in trash cans . Empty vending machines now show up as orange. Updated the road signs in the Hub. Moved the desert in the Hub to be closer to the town. Increased sell price of starfruit from 60 coins to 62 coins. Decreased sell price of spinach from 18 coins to 14 coins.

Bug fixes [ ]

Made fixes to reduce published servers from frequently crashing. Fixed a bug that prevented speed boosts from mounting ducks , yaks , and horses to function in the Hub . Fixed a bug that prevented the save slots menu from opening. Fixed an invincibility glitch using lava . Fixed a bug that caused a large number of decimal places to appear when receiving coins . Fixed the slime portal spawning players in an incorrect location. Fixed a bug that caused Slime Queen orbs to crash servers. Fixed the Slime Queen arena rendering the lighting from Spirit Island . Fixed floating diamond rocks in the Diamond Mines .

July 15, 2022 [ ]

Fixed a bug preventing flower breeding quests from working. Uncompleted non-repeatable questlines will now reset to become "available" after one hour.
