The March 5, 2021 update is a minor update in Islands .

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Changed the starter island for new players. Revamped and expanded the tutorial . Now villagers have a proper name, and Taliyah now has a title that says ''Animals''. Added a "Home" teleport button to the right sidebar while away from the player's island.

Modifications [ ]

Increased pickaxe block breaking damage. Reduced health/hardness of grass . The right sidebar is now positioned higher on mobile devices. Fixed a bug that sometimes caused the hotbar to appear empty on join.

Previous changes [ ]

March 4, 2021 [ ]

Removed the portal and teleport button to Fishing Festival Island . Improved portal teleporting speed to prevent duping.
