Glow Stick

A glow stick is a item that can be obtained during Islands' Third Anniversary .

Obtaining [ ]

Quests [ ]

The player can obtain 20 glow sticks by completing the "Glow With the Flow!" quest that was available from April 14, 2023 to April 28, 2023 .

Usage [ ]

Glowing [ ]

While holding the glow stick, the stick will change colors and have a fading trail effect of the stick's color when the player holding is moving.

History [ ]

April 14, 2023 Added glow sticks. April 28, 2023 Sparklers are made unobtainable.
