Usage [ ]

Transforming [ ]

Oil barrels or petroleum barrels can be inputted through the conveyor belt at the front. It has 3 slots that can hold one barrel per slot and takes 4-5 seconds to convert one oil/petroleum barrel into six oil / petroleum fuel . It does not require any fuel source to be powered and the oil/petroleum will be outputted through the pipe at the back once the process is complete.

Input Output 1 Oil Barrel ➡ 6 Oil Fuel 1 Petroleum Barrel ➡ 6 Petroleum Fuel
