Stone long arrows are large arrows that can be shot from longbows .

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

Name Recipe Crafting station 2 Stone Long Arrow 4 Oak Wood 2 Stone Anvil

Usage [ ]

Ammunition [ ]

Stone long arrows are used as ammunition in longbows , as at least one is required to be in the player's inventory in order for the bow to fire. Shooting a stone long arrow deals an extra 13 - 18 HP to mobs .

Stone long arrows are a one-time use item , meaning they cannot be retrieved after they have been shot.
