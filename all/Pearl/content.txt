Pearl Sell price 777 coins A pearl is an item obtained from fishing .

Obtaining [ ]

Fishing [ ]

Pearls can be obtained as a treasure item from fishing , having a rarity of 'rare'. The drop chances for pearls can be increased by leveling up the Fishing skill.

Usage [ ]

Crafting ingredient [ ]

Image Name Recipe Crafting station Pearl Block 10 Pearl Workbench Tier 2 Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4

Selling [ ]

Pearls can be sold to Thomas for 777 coins .

History [ ]

June 15, 2020 Added pearls. February 26, 2021 Reduced the chance obtaining a pearl from fishing . May 13, 2022 Wooden fishing rods can now fish pearls. Pearls can no longer be used to craft washing stations and aquamarine swords . June 18, 2022 Pearls can now be used to craft pearl blocks . Pearls can now be sold to Thomas for 777 coins .
