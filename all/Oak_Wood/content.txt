Oak Wood Current Old Type of block Solid block Sell price 2 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent Oak wood is a block that can be obtained from cutting down a tree . It is widely used in many basic crafting recipes.

Obtaining [ ]

Cutting [ ]

Cutting down an oak tree or any fruit tree will drop 3 - 4 oak wood along with its respective sapling .

Fueling [ ]

Wood can be used as fuel for campfires , small furnaces , and desert furnaces . Each wood fuels the heating device for 20 seconds.

Selling [ ]

Wood can be sold to John for 2 coins , who can be found in the Hub .
