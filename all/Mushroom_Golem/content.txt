Mushroom Golem Health 365 HP Behavior Hostile Attack strength 9HP Drops Jukebox Disk Location Mushroom Obby A mushroom golem is a hostile mob that is found on the third island of the Mushroom Obby .

Spawning [ ]

Mushroom golems are found on the third island of the Mushroom Obby during the "Defeat 10 Mushroom Golems" quest.

Drops [ ]

A mushroom golem rewards 5 (18 with the 4x daily bonus) Light Melee, Heavy Melee, Magic, or Archery XP depending on the weapon the player used to defeat it.
