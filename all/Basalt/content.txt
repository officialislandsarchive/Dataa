Basalt Basalt Rock Type of block Solid block Dropped by Coal Rock Mimic Iron Rock Mimic Gold Rock Mimic Best tool Luminous Transparent Basalt is a block obtained by defeating rock mimics and mining basalt rocks .

Obtaining [ ]

Mining [ ]

Basalt rocks naturally generate on Buffalkor Island . Mining a basalt rock yields 2 - 4 basalt after each stage and 3 Mining XP (6 with the 4x daily bonus), resulting in a total of 4 - 8 basalt and 9 Mining XP (18 with the 4x daily bonus) per rock.

Mob drop [ ]

Rock mimics of any kind are guaranteed to drop 4 - 7 basalt upon defeat.

Basalt can be transformed into basalt bricks in a stonecutter or an industrial stonecutter or smooth basalt in a polishing station or an industrial polishing station .
