Berry Pie Recipe 1 Berry Dough Crafted with Campfire Small Furnace Desert Furnace Industrial Oven Heals 40 HP Sell price 168 - 252 coins Hot sell price 252 - 378 coins Berry pie is a food item .

Obtaining [ ]

Cooking [ ]

Baking berry dough in a campfire , small furnace , desert furnace , or industrial oven will create a berry pie.

Input Output 1 Berry Dough 1 Berry Pie

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating a berry pie restores 40 HP.

Selling [ ]

Berry pie can be sold to Petur for 168 - 252 coins each, or 252 - 378 coins if berry pie is a hot offer.
