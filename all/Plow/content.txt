A plow is a farming tool that is used to turn grass into farmland and vice versa.

Obtaining [ ]

Natural generation [ ]

When the player first joins Islands , a plow will appear next to patches of wheat on their island , which can be picked up and used.

Crafting [ ]

Name Recipe Crafting station Plow 5 Oak Wood 2 Stone Workbench Tier 1 Workbench Tier 2 Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4

Purchasing [ ]

Cletus' lucky plow could have been purchased from Cletus on Farming Festival Island for 7,500 crops .

Usage [ ]

The plow is used to till grass into farmland to allow the growth of most crops . To use the plow, simply click on a block of grass and the block will turn into farmland . Using the same method, farmland can also be turned back into grass.

Trivia [ ]

The plow that spawns upon the creation of an island cannot despawn. This is because of a block called the item port , which holds it in place.
