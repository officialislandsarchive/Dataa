Bedrock Type of block Solid block; Unbreakable Spawns Player's Island Luminous Transparent Bedrock is an indestructible block located at the bottom of every player island upon island creation.

Obtaining [ ]

Natural generation [ ]

Bedrock naturally spawns on the player's island when the island is first generated.

Usage [ ]

Bedrock's primary usage is as a solid unbreakable block to prevent players from respawning above a hole, which would create an infinite falling loop.
