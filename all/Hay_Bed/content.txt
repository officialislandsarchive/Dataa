Usage [ ]

Animal bed [ ]

When placed down, cows , sheep , turkeys , yaks , , and horses will sleep on hay beds during the night. Animals that sleep on a hay bed will have increased happiness levels.

Hay beds are an effective way of getting animals to return to a specific location without action from the player, though it is not certain that they will sleep on it.
