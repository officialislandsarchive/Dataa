The golden slime mural is a mural that can be obtained by opening a dungeon chest . Not to be confused with normal slime murals which are obtained by buying them directly from Ivon for 1,200 dungeon coins each.

Obtaining [ ]

Dungeon Chest [ ]

Opening a dungeon chest has a 7% chance of rewarding the golden slime mural, having a rarity of 'rare'.
