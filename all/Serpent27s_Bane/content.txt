Attacking [ ]

At level 1 Heavy Melee , the serpent's bane deals a base damage of 65 HP and can be swung every 0.5 seconds. It has the ability to damage multiple mobs in one swing. There's also a small chance of landing a critical hit when using this weapon, meaning the attack will deal an extra 33 HP of damage. Its damage can be increased by leveling up the Heavy Melee skill and it is in the slashing class.

Like other melee weapon , only one player can hit damage in each hostile mob. Multiple players cannot hit damage together.

Trivia [ ]

The serpent's bane is the second strongest melee weapon , with the reaper's scythe being the first. Unlike serpent's hooks and the reaper's scythes, it is not in the void class despite having void attributes.
