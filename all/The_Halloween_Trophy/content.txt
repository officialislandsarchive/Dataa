The Halloween Trophy Type of block Solid block Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Won by defeating the Pumpkin Boss in the Halloween 2021 Event [sic] The Halloween trophy is a trophy that has a chance to be obtained from cauldrons .

Obtaining [ ]

Cauldron [ ]

Opening a cauldron has a 2% chance of rewarding the Halloween trophy, having a rarity of 'legendary'.
