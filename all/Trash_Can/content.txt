Usage [ ]

Discarding [ ]

Trash cans can be used to permanently discard all items in the inventory except for the wooden axe , wooden pickaxe , and workbench . A trash can has 12 slots that can fit up to 150 items per slot, adding up to a total of 1,800 items per can. After placing items in the slots and clicking "Clear Trash", a warning will pop up, prompting the player to confirm that they are willing to permanently discard the items in the can.
