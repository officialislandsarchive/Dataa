Desert Furnace Type of block Solid block; Interactable Best tool None Luminous Transparent Description A desert themed small furnace. The desert furnace is an unobtainable heating device used to smelt, heat, or cook items and blocks . Its stats are identical to that of the small furnace .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Desert furnaces could have been purchased from Azur for 50 sand dollars each before the June 3, 2022, update.

History [ ]

November 20, 2020 Added desert furnaces. July 23, 2021 The desert furnace can now hold up to 5 fuel items per slot, up from 1 per slot. November 27, 2021 Desert furnaces can now cook pancake dough into pancakes . January 8, 2022 Desert furnaces can now cook uncooked pineapple pizzas into pineapple pizzas . April 1, 2022 Updated the GUI to have one slot for the unheated item, one for the final product, and one for the fuel instead of two for items and one for fuel. 50 items can now queued to be heated in a slot, up from 5. June 3, 2022 Desert furnaces can no longer be purchased from Azur .
