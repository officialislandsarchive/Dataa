Void Hound Health 200 HP Behavior Hostile Attack strength 15 HP Drops Void Serpent Token Amethyst Crystal Jukebox Disk Location Void Isles The void hound is a hostile mob that spawn in the Void Isles .

Spawning [ ]

Void Isles [ ]

Void hounds spawn throughout the Void Isles , having 200 HP and dealing 15 HP of damage.

Void Serpent [ ]

The Void Serpent may summon 2 - 15 void hounds to aid it in battle, with the number summoned in proportion to the number of players fighting it.

Behavior [ ]

Void hounds attack with quick close combat hits and deal 15 HP. Void hounds are also immune to the attritional damage dealt when being in the presence of void smoke .

Drops [ ]

A void hound rewards 6 Combat XP (24 XP with the 4x daily bonus) and 6 Light Melee , Heavy Melee , Magic , or Archery XP (24 with the 4x daily bonus) depending on the weapon the player used to defeat it.
