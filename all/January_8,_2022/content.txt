January 8, 2022 Basic Information Day released January 8, 2022 Version number 67 Version Navigator Previous version Next version December 29, 2021 January 15, 2022 The January 8, 2022 update, also known as the pineapple update, is a minor update in Islands that introduced a new crop , small modifications, and a teaser for the next update.

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Pineapples Added Oily Pineapples Added Pineapple Seeds Added Pineapple Slice Added Uncooked Pineapple Pizza Added Pineapple Pizza Added Pineapple Totem Added Islands Painting Petur's prices are now increased by 1.5x

Modifications [ ]

Changed pizza base price from 80 to 100 coins Changed watermelon sell price from 95 to 80 coins Changed watermelon seeds price from 2,150 to 1,850 coins Changed crate price multipler Crate multipler changed from 1.25x to 1.20x Large crate multiplier changed from 1.46x to 1.35x Changed the texture of leaves

Other [ ]

Fixed duplication exploits Extended the Christmas 2021 event by a week
