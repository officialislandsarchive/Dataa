Iron Skorp Scale Dropped by Iron Skorp Sell price 75 coins Required skill (to sell) Level 36 Combat Description A rare drop from killing Skorps. The iron skorp scale is an item that is dropped from iron skorps .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Iron skorps are guaranteed to drop 1 iron skorp scale upon defeat and a 50% chance of dropping 2.

Usage [ ]

Selling [ ]

Iron skorp scales can be sold to Ivon for 75 coins each after reaching level 36 Combat .

History [ ]

August 9, 2020 Added iron skorp scales. January 22, 2021 Level 36 Combat is now required to sell iron skorp scales to Ivon .
