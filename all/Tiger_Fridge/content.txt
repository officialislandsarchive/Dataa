Tiger Fridge Type of block Solid block; Interactable Buy cost 20,000 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent The tiger fridge is a furniture block that could have been purchased from Yuzi .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

The tiger fridge could have been purchased from Yuzi for 20,000 coins during the Lunar New Year event .

Red envelope [ ]

Opening a red envelope has a 7.14% chance of rewarding a tiger fridge.

Breaking [ ]

Tiger Fridge Breaking Data Hardness 5 Best Tool None Max Hits Needed Material/Tool Axe Pickaxe Stone Shovel 8 Wooden 15 15 Stone 8 8 Iron 5 5 Gilded Steel 4 4 Diamond 3 3 Opal 2 2 Void Mattock 1

Usage [ ]

Storage [ ]

A tiger fridge has 6 slots that can fit up to 10 consumable items per slot, adding up to a total of 60 items per fridge.

History [ ]

January 30, 2022 Added tiger fridges. February 19, 2022 Tiger fridges can no longer be bought from Yuzi . April 12, 2022 Fixed a bug causing items inside tiger fridges to disappear.

Trivia [ ]

There was a bug where the tiger fridge would not save stored items , causing them to be removed upon rejoining the game. This was fixed on April 12, 2022 .
