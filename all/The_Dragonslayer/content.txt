Attacking [ ]

At level 1 Heavy Melee , the dragonslayer deals a base damage of 45 HP and can be swung every 0.5 seconds. It has the ability to damage multiple mobs in one swing. There's also a small chance of landing a critical hit when using this weapon, meaning the attack will deal an extra 23 HP of damage. Its damage can be increased by leveling up the Heavy Melee skill and it is in the slashing class.

Like other melee weapon , only one player can hit damage in each hostile mob. Multiple players cannot hit damage together.
