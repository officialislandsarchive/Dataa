Maple Syrup Recipe 1 Maple Sap Crafted with Sap Boiler Maple syrup is a liquid item that can be transported using wood tubes . It cannot be collected into the player's inventory .

Obtaining [ ]

Transforming [ ]

Maple syrup can be obtained by boiling maple sap in a sap boiler . It takes 15 seconds to fully boil the sap into maple syrup.

Input Item transformer Output 1 Maple Sap ➡ Sap Boiler ➡ 1 Maple Syrup

Usage [ ]

Transforming [ ]

Maple syrup can be bottled into maple syrup bottles in a syrup bottler . It takes 5 seconds to bottle maple syrup into a maple syrup bottle. Up to 6 maple syrup can be bottled in a syrup bottler at once.

Input Item transformer Output 1 Maple Syrup ➡ Syrup Bottler ➡ 1 Maple Syrup Bottle
