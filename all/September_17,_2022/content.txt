September 17, 2022 Basic Information Day released September 17, 2022 Version number 99 Version Navigator Previous version Next version September 10, 2022 September 24, 2022 The September 17, 2022 update, also known as the combat update part I, is a major update in Islands that revamped the combat system, adding many more mechanics, categories, and new weapons .

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added new weapon categories: Melee: Slashing. Striking. Piercing. Magic: Inferno. Hydro. Storm. Quake. Ethereal. Void. Added the Combat Catalogue . Added new idle, draw, and fire bow animations. Added golden shortbows . Added iron shortbows . Added stone short arrows . Added stone long arrows .

Modifications [ ]

Mobs are now weak, neutral, or resistant to certain weapon types. Revamped bows : The trajectory of arrows are now smoother. Bows now require specific types of arrows to use. Charging shots no longer increase the base power of projectiles of all magic weapons but the Slime Queen's scepter . Modified the base damage of many weapons: Static scepter : 2 HP/s → 8 HP/s. Spellbook : 45 HP → 22 HP. Tidal spellbook : 42 HP → 36 HP. Ruby staff : 42 HP → 36 HP. Staff of Godzilla : 25 HP → 20 HP. Spirit spellbook : White projectiles: 40 HP → 30 HP. Blue projectiles: 45 HP → 37 HP. Pink projectiles: 48 HP → 42 HP. Green projectiles: 55 HP → 46 HP. Slime Queen's scepter: 50 HP → 40 HP. Ranged weapons shoot in the direction of the player's cursor (PC only). The damage indicator changes color based on weapon type. Renamed the basic bow to "Wooden Shortbow". Renamed the artifact bow renamed to "Ancient Longbow". Renamed the vamp bow renamed to "Azarathian Longbow". Increased the sell price of potatoes from 11 coins to 17 coins. Decreased the sell price of radishes from 64 coins to 59 coins.
