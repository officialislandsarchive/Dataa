Spirit Crystal Sell price 23 coins Description Combine this with a dye to light up your island! A spirit crystal is an item obtained from harvesting spirit crops .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a spirit crop yields 1 - 3 spirit crystals, 1 spirit seed , and 6 Farming XP (18 with the 4x daily bonus).
