Pig Spawn Egg Buy cost Tier 1: 12,500 coins Required skill Tier 1: Level 31 Animal Care Description Spawn a pig with tier 1/2/3 trait maximums. Eats carrots. A pig spawn egg is a spawn egg used to spawn pigs on player islands . It exists in 3 tiers.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Tier 1 pig spawn eggs can be purchased from Taliyah for 12,500 coins after reaching level 31 in the Animal Care skill.

Breeding [ ]

Feeding two pigs potions of fertility during the day will create a spawn egg whose tier is based on the two "parent" animals' stats.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

To spawn a pig, the player must hatch the egg by selecting and holding anywhere on their screen if their small animal limit has not been reached.
