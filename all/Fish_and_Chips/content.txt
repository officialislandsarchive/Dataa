Fish and Chips Recipe 1 Cut Fish 5 Potato Crafted with Food Processor Heals 80 HP Required skill Level 15 Cooking Fish and chips is an unobtainable food item that heals 80 HP when consumed. It cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .
