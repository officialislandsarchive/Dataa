The Mining Event 2023 trophy is a trophy that was earned through the 2023 Mining Event .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

The Mining Event 2023 trophy could have been bought from Jed for 1000 Emerald Crystals .

Mob Drop [ ]

The Mining Event 2023 trophy could be dropped by Draven with a 3% chance.
