Tomato Seeds Planted on Farmland Buy cost 250 coins Required skill Level 5 Farming Tomato seeds are seeds used to farm for tomatoes .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Tomato seeds can be bought from Tom for 250 coins after reaching level 5 in Farming .

Harvesting [ ]

When a player harvests a tomato crop, the crop yields at least 1 tomato seed, with a 5% (1 in 20) chance of getting 2 seeds upon harvesting. This chance may be increased for owners of the PRO Gamepass .

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Tomato Seeds can be planted or placed on farmland to grow and farm tomatoes.
