Yak Butter Recipe 1 Yak Milk Bucket Crafted with Churner Heals 15 HP Yak butter is a food item obtained from transforming a yak milk bucket .

It currently cannot be sold to Petur .

Obtaining [ ]

Transforming [ ]

Yak milk buckets can be transformed into yak butter in a churner . It takes a total of 3 minutes and 30 seconds to fully transform yak milk buckets into yak butter.

Input Item transformer Output 1 Yak Milk Bucket ➡ Churner ➡ 1 Yak Butter
