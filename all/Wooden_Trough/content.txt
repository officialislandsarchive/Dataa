Usage [ ]

Feeding animals [ ]

Wooden troughs can be filled with 2 stacks of 10 food items to be fed to cows , sheep , pigs , chickens , turkeys , ducks , yaks , and horses . Animals will only eat their favorite foods or the that animal type's food.

Trivia [ ]

When the wooden trough was first released, it was called the "wheat trough" since it could only hold wheat . This was changed on January 22, 2021 , when sheep were added.
