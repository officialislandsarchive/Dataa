Orange Cake Recipe 1 Orange Cake Batter Crafted with Campfire Small Furnace Desert Furnace Industrial Oven Heals 40 HP Sell price 120 - 180 coins Hot sell price 180 - 270 coins Orange cake is a food item .

Obtaining [ ]

Cooking [ ]

Baking orange cake batter in a campfire , small furnace , desert furnace , or industrial oven will create an orange cake.

Input Output Orange Cake Batter 1 Orange Cake

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating an orange cake restores 40 HP.

Selling [ ]

Orange cakes can be sold to Petur for 120 - 180 coins each, or 180 - 270 coins if orange cakes are a hot offer.

History [ ]

June 26, 2020 Added orange cakes. January 8, 2022 Changed the base selling price from 150 coins to 180 - 270 coins. January 15, 2022 Changed the base selling price from 180 - 270 coins to 120 - 180 coins.
