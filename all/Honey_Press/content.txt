Usage [ ]

Transforming [ ]

Honeycomb can be transformed into jars of honey in a honey press. Up to ten honeycomb can be tranformed at a time, and it takes 3 minutes and 30 seconds to make one jar of honey.

Trivia [ ]

This was the first animated item transformer . Similar to the test totem , the honey press does not have a texture when held and disappears if dropped. If you spam in honeycombs just as the honey is finished processing, you can instantly transform a honeycomb or 2.
