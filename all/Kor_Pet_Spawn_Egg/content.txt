Kor Pet Spawn Egg Dropped by Kor The Kor pet spawn egg is a spawn egg used to spawn a Kor pet . It cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Kor has a 0.33% chance of dropping a Kor pet spawn egg upon defeat.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

To spawn a Kor pet , the player must be the owner of the island they are currently on. While holding the egg, clicking or tapping anywhere on their screen will spawn a Kor pet.

History [ ]

September 18, 2021 Added Kor pet spawn eggs. June 18, 2022 Buffalkors are now able to drop Kor spawn eggs upon defeat. June 25, 2022 Buffalkors are no longer able to drop Kor spawn eggs upon defeat.
