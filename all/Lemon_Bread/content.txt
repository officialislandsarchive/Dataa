Lemon Bread Recipe 1 Lemon Bread Dough Crafted with Campfire Small Furnace Desert Furnace Industrial Oven Heals 25 HP Sell price 120 - 180 coins Hot sell price 180 - 270 coins Lemon bread is a food item .

Obtaining [ ]

Cooking [ ]

Baking lemon bread dough in a campfire , small furnace , desert furnace , or industrial oven will create lemon bread.

Input Output 1 Lemon Bread Dough 1 Lemon Bread

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating lemon bread restores 25 HP.

Selling [ ]

Lemon bread can be sold to Petur for 120 - 180 coins each, or 180 - 270  coins if lemon bread is a hot offer.
