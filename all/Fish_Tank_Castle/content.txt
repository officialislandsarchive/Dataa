Fish Tank Castle Recipe 10 Iron Ingot 10 Stone Crafted with Furniture Workbench Required skill Level 35 Fishing The fish tank castle is an item that can be placed inside a small or medium fish tank .

Obtaining [ ]

Fishing [ ]

Prior to the May 13, 2022 update, the fish tank castle blueprint had a small chance to be reeled in from fishing with an iron fishing rod . Bait could have been used to increase the chances of obtaining the blueprint.

Coral [ ]

Breaking coral had a small chance of rewarding a fish tank castle blueprint.

Crafting [ ]

Name Recipe Crafting station Required skill Fish Tank Castle 10 Iron Ingot 10 Stone Furniture Workbench Level 35 Fishing

Usage [ ]

Decoration [ ]

The fish tank castle can be placed inside a small or medium fish tank as decoration, taking up 1 fish slot.

History [ ]

September 18, 2020 Added fish tank castles. May 13, 2022 Level 35 Fishing is now required to craft fish tank castles. June 3, 2022 A fish tank castle blueprint is no longer required to unlock the recipe of fish tank castles.
