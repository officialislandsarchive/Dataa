Hickory Wood Type of block Solid block Sell price 28 Coins Best tool Luminous Transparent Hickory wood is a block that can be obtained from cutting down hickory trees .

Obtaining [ ]

Cutting [ ]

Cutting down a hickory tree will drop 3 - 4 hickory wood along with a hickory sapling . If Auto Plant Saplings: On, you don't take any sapling and the sapling still on the place that the Hickory Tree being chopped.

Fueling [ ]

Hickory wood can be used to fuel campfires , small furnaces , and desert furnaces for 20 seconds per log.

Selling [ ]

Hickory wood can be sold to John for 28 coins .
