Cletus Plushie Type of block Solid block; Interactable Best tool Luminous Transparent A Cletus plushie is a plushie that produces a squeaky sound when interacted with.

Obtaining [ ]

Taliyah [ ]

From April 14, 2023 - April 28, 2023 , Taliyah visited player islands during the Islands' 3 year anniversary . Interacting with her on the player's own island will reward them the plushie.
