Fhanhorn Pet Spawn Egg Dropped by Fhanhorn The Fhanhorn pet spawn egg is a spawn egg used to spawn a Fhanhorn pet . It cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Fhanhorn has a 0.33% chance of dropping a Fhanhorn pet spawn egg upon defeat.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

To spawn a Fhanhorn pet , the player must be the owner of the island they are currently on. While holding the egg, clicking or tapping anywhere on their screen will spawn a Fhanhorn pet.
