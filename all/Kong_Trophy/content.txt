Kong Trophy Type of block Solid block Best tool Luminous Transparent The Kong trophy is a trophy that could have been obtained during the Godzilla vs. Kong event.

Obtaining [ ]

Quests [ ]

To obtain a Kong trophy, players had to complete Bosco 's first two quests: Harvest 10 coconuts and study 5 bone piles. Talking to Bosco again upon completion would reward the player with the trophy.

Trivia [ ]

This was one of the two trophies that were obtainable from the Godzilla vs. Kong event, the other being the Godzilla trophy . This trophy does not emit sparkles, just like the Godzilla trophy and Roblox Battles trophy .
