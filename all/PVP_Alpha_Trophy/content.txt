PVP Alpha Trophy Type of block Solid Block Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Limited edition trophy found only during the 2020 PvP Alpha. The PvP Alpha Trophy was a trophy available for a limited time during the PvP Alpha testing. It could be purchased from the Mercenary for 100 Skulls on the PvP Island before the event ended. This item is no longer obtainable, although it can still be traded with other players by dropping, chests , and Vending Machines .

Trivia [ ]

The PVP Alpha Trophy is the second trophy added into the game, the first being The Witches Trophy .
