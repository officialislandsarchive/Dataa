Dragon Pet Spawn Egg The dragon pet spawn egg is a spawn egg used to spawn a dragon pet . It cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Dragon eggs [ ]

Opening an infernal dragon egg has a 0.5% chance of rewarding a dragon pet spawn egg, having a rarity of 'mythic'.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

To spawn a dragon pet , the player must be the owner of the island they are currently on. While holding the egg, clicking or tapping anywhere on their screen will spawn a dragon.
