July 23, 2021 Basic Information Day released July 23, 2021 Version number 50 Version Navigator Previous version Next version July 17, 2021 July 30, 2021 The July 23, 2021 update is a minor update.

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Save Slots . Added Slime Blocks . Added passive health regeneration. Added a workbench upgrading system. Added the recipe for the Wooden Axe .

Modifications [ ]

Tree Saplings now automatically replant You can change this in the Settings . Note that Industrial Lumbermills still do not replant saplings. Decreased knockback on slimes . Fixed an issue where people would fall out of the world when traveling to the Hub . Modified level requirements for the following: Maple Sapling ( Woodcutting level 14 to level 11) Avocado Tree Sapling (Woodcutting level 12 to level 16) Net ( Animal Care level 3 to level 2) Small Display Case (Animal Care level 3 to level 2) Wooden Trough (Animal Care level 4 to level 3) Fixed an issue with Iron Ore spawning in the ground in the Hub, causing issues with the tutorial . Increased drop rate of stone from stone rocks (2 - 3 to 4 - 6). Changed Workbench Tier 3 recipe to require stone instead of stone bricks and decreased the iron requirement from 450 to 400. Renamed the Basic Workbench to Workbench Tier 1 . The Polishing Station can be crafted in the Workbench Tier 1. The Obby Kit requires 11 Wood to craft instead of 10. Fixed the issue with NPCs falling over. Re-ordered workbench items and added previews for locked items. Doubled XP drop from Oak Trees . Fixed the issue with Davey visiting the player's island twice. Fixed a large number of performance issues on hub servers. Cletus now looks different compared to Jack and Ivon .

Removed [ ]

Removed level requirements for the following: Stone Pickaxe Stone Axe Small Furnace Cow Spawn Egg Tier 1
