Fishing Festival Trophy Type of block Solid block Buy cost 450 Fishing Tickets Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Rare decoration prize from participating in the late July fishing festival of 2022 The Fishing Festival trophy is a trophy earned during the 2022 Fishing Festival event.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

The Fishing Festival trophy could have been purchased from Thomas on Fishing Festival Island for 450 fishing tickets during the 2022 Fishing Festival event.
