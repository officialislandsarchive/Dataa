Regen Potion Recipe 1 Starfruit 1 Red Mushroom 1 Empty Potion Bottle Crafted with Brewing Stand Effect Regeneration Required skill Level 40 Cooking Description Regenerate health over time. A regen potion is an item that grants Regeneration when consumed. Drinking the potion will not return the potion bottle .

Crafting [ ]

Usage [ ]

Drinking [ ]

Drinking a regen potion grants the player 10 seconds of Regeneration . Regeneration will slowly replenish the player's health by 5 HP every second, healing the player a total of 50 HP before ending. Regeneration can also be used to cure the Food Poison effect.
