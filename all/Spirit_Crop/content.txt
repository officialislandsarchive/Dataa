Spirit Crop Seed cost 50 spirit jars Planted on Farmland Edible A spirit crop is a crop that grows on farmland from spirit seeds .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a spirit crop yields 1 - 3 spirit crystals , 1 spirit seed , and 6 Farming XP (18 with the 4x daily bonus). Extra spirit seeds cannot be yielded.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of spirit crops can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer . If watered and fertilized, the growth time will be shortened by 30%.
