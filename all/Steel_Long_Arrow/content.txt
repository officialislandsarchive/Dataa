Steel long arrows are large arrows that can be shot from longbows .

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

Name Recipe Crafting station Required skill 2 Steel Long Arrow 4 Hickory Wood 2 Steel Ingot Anvil Level 37 Archery

Usage [ ]

Ammunition [ ]

Steel long arrows are used as ammunition in longbows , as at least one is required to be in the player's inventory in order for the bow to fire. Shooting a steel long arrow deals an extra 20 - 27 HP to mobs .

Steel long arrows are a one-time use item , meaning they cannot be retrieved after they have been shot.
