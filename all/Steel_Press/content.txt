Steel Press Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe 100 Copper Plate 250 Copper Rod 25 Steel Rod Crafted with Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4 Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Produces steel products using molds and steel ingots. A steel press is an industrial item transformer used to turn steel ingots into steel bolts , plates , or rods with factory molds .

Usage [ ]

Transforming [ ]

To produce bolts, plates, or rods, the machine must be fed steel ingots after being inserted with any type of mold.

Input Mold Output 10 Steel Ingot ➡ Bolt Factory Mold ➡ 1 Steel Bolt 20 Steel Ingot ➡ Rod Factory Mold ➡ 1 Steel Rod 50 Steel Ingot ➡ Plate Factory Mold ➡ 1 Steel Plate

History [ ]

September 18, 2020 Added the steel press. With the description reading "Produces high grade steel from iron." February 19, 2021 Steel pressers no longer dispose steel when switching molds. May 19, 2021 Changed the description to "Produces steel products using molds and steel ingots." December 29, 2021 Disabled manually placing items into steel presses.

Trivia [ ]

Unlike the copper press , bolts in this machine only cost 10 ingots, instead of 15.
