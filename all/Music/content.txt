Composers [ ]

All music in Islands were originally composed by Lawnreality. Royalty free tracks were also added at a later time, composed by Kevin MacLeod. On April 4, 2021 all music by Lawnreality was removed and replaced with original soundtracks composed by Micah Fischer.
