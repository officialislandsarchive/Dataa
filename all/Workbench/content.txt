Workbench Type of block Solid block; Interactable Spawns Player's Island Best tool Luminous Transparent Description "Used to construct starter island craftables."

The workbench is a crafting station that is spawned when a player's island is first generated.

Obtaining [ ]

Natural generation [ ]

A workbench naturally generates on the player's island when first playing Islands or after resetting the island.

Tutorial [ ]

Using a workbench is a part of the tutorial .

Upgrading [ ]

The workbench can be upgraded into a workbench tier 1 with 5 oak wood and 2 iron ore . Upgrading the crafting station will replace the workbench with a workbench tier 1.
