Ghost Happy Angry Surprised Type of block Solid block; Luminous Best tool Luminous Transparent Ghosts are luminous furniture blocks that can be obtained from cauldrons . They come in three variants: happy, angry, and surprised.

Obtaining [ ]

Cauldron [ ]

Opening a cauldron has a 30% chance of rewarding 5 happy, angry, or surprised ghosts, all having a rarity of 'common'.
