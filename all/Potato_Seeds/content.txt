Potato Seeds Planted on Farmland Buy cost 300 Required skill Level 9 Farming Potato seeds are seeds used to grow potatoes .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Potato seeds can be purchased from Cletus for 300 coins each after reaching level 9 in the Farming skill.

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a potato crop has a 5% chance to yield an extra potato seed. This chance can be increased by owning the VIP or Pro Gamepass .

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Potato seeds can be planted on farmland to produce potatoes.

Growing [ ]

Potato seeds have 4 growth phases, starting with a seed and then sprouting.
