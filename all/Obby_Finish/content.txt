Obby Finish Type of block Non-solid block; Interactable Recipe 10 Oak Wood 1 Red Flower Crafted with Obby Kit Best tool Luminous Transparent The obby finish is a block used in obbies . It was added in the August 16, 2020 Obby update.

Usage [ ]

Obbies [ ]

Obby finishes are used to finish or beat an obby on a Player Island . A player must first touch an obby start , after touching a obby finish the player's time will be counted and added to a nearby obby leaderboard (If the player beat one of the top 3).
