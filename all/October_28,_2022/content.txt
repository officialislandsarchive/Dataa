October 28, 2022 Basic Information Day released October 28, 2022 Version number 105 Version Navigator Previous version Next version October 22, 2022 November 4, 2022 The October 28, 2022 update, also known as the disco update, is a minor update in Islands that added new disco-themed furniture blocks, and made several changes.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added new furniture blocks: Added disco balls . Added disco floor tiles . Added lounge chairs . Added table tops . Added table top chairs . Added hanging guitars . Added short table tops . Added speaker systems .

Modifications [ ]

Island owners now have a symbol above their avatar. Players with build permissions now have a symbol above their avatars. Revamped the boss summoning UI. Tokens are now indicated on summoning UI except Fhanhorn with 1-hour cooldown due to it having no tokens . Golden nets can now be placed inside of tool stands . Fixed a bug that caused blocks to be unable to be placed near small fire chalices . Fixed a bug that caused players to be unable to retrieve trade-locked items from cauldrons . Patched another trash can trading glitch. Increased the sell price of avocados from 32 coins to 36 coins. Decreased the sell price of carrots from 21 coins to 16 coins.
