Steel Ingot Recipe 1 Iron Ingot Crafted with Steel Mill Blast Furnace Description Refined steel ingot. Used for crafting. A steel ingot is a primary industrial material made with iron ingots in a steel mill or a blast furnace .

Obtaining [ ]

Transforming [ ]

Iron ingots can be transformed into steel ingots in a steel mill or a blast furnace .

Input Item transformer Output 1 Iron Ingot ➡ Steel Mill ➡ 1 Steel Ingot ➡ Blast Furnace ➡

Upgrading [ ]

The steel ingot can be used to upgrade the electrical workbench to level 2 and 3.

Level 2 Electrical Workbench upgrade Level 3 Electrical Workbench upgrade 20 Copper Bolt 250 Electrite 150 Steel Ingot 450 Steel Ingot 50 Steel Bolt 10 Copper Plate 750 Electrite

Molding [ ]

Steel ingots can be molded into steel bolts , rods , and plates with a steel press . The respective mold must be inserted into the steel press in order to process the steel ingot.

Input Item transformer/ mold Output 10 Steel Ingot Steel Press Bolt Factory Mold 1 Steel Bolt 25 Steel Ingot Steel Press Rod Factory Mold 1 Steel Rod 50 Steel Ingot Steel Press Plate Factory Mold 1 Steel Plate
