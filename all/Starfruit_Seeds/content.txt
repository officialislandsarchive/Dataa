Starfruit Seeds Planted on Farmland Starfruit seeds are seeds that grow into starfruit once planted.

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Starfruit seeds can obtained after harvesting starfruit on Buffalkor or Wizard Island with a 4% chance. Harvesting starfruit on the Player's Island will not grant extra seeds.

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Starfruit seeds can be planted on farmland . They yield starfruit once harvested. This process can be automated with starfruit totems .

History [ ]

July 11, 2020 Added starfruit seeds. July 17, 2020 Increased chances of getting starfruit seeds.

Trivia [ ]

The drop rate for Starfruit Seeds used to be 0.1%, but this chance was increased on the July 17, 2020 update.
