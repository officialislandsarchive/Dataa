Jar of Honey Recipe 1 Honeycomb Crafted with Honey Press Heals 12 HP Sell price 20 coins Description Fresh honey from a local bee hive. A jar of honey is an item made by processing honeycomb .

Obtaining [ ]

Transforming [ ]

Honeycombs can be processed into jars of honey in a honey press . It takes about three and a half minutes to process one honeycomb into a jar of honey.

Input Item transformer Output 1 Honeycomb ➡ Honey Press ➡ 1 Jar of Honey
