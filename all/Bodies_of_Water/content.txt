----------------------------------------

Bodies of water can be found in nearly every island . Players are able to fish and collect water with buckets in most of them except for the Crystal Pool, Godzilla Lake, and Skull River. There is also a bug where players are unable to get any fish from the Hub River . Coral , Sea Grass , Aquamarine and Clay only spawn in the Hub Pond and Slime Reef .
