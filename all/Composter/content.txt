Usage [ ]

Composting [ ]

Salmon , carp , tuna , grouper , trout , and red snapper can be placed in the composter to create basic fertilizer . Up to 20 fish can be placed inside at once, with each cycle transforming 2 fish at a time every 30 seconds.

Players can only use composters after reaching level 14 Farming .

History [ ]

June 15, 2020 Added composters. January 22, 2021 Level 14 Farming is now required to craft and use the composter. April 1, 2022 Tuna , groupers , trouts , and red snappers can now be composted in composters. Two fish may now be composted at once, down from three.
