Antler Dagger Description A dagger imbued with the Might of Fhanhorn. The antler dagger is an unimplemented melee weapon in Islands .

Information [ ]

The antler dagger was added on November 27, 2021 along with the antler shield and antler hammer but was left unimplemented.

History [ ]
