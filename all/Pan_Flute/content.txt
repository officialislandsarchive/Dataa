Pan Flute Recipe 10 Iron Ingot 10 Oak Wood Crafted with Instrument Workbench The pan flute is an instrument .

Obtaining [ ]

Melody [ ]

The player will receive a pan flute and a kalimba upon first interacting with Melody .

Crafting [ ]

The instrument workbench gamepass is required to craft the pan flute.

Name Recipe Crafting station Gamepass required Pan Flute 10 Iron Ingot 10 Oak Wood Instrument Workbench

Usage [ ]

The player can play a song by using the pan flute.
