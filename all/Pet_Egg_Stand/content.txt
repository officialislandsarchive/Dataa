Pet Egg Stand White Cyan Red Blue Purple Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe See Crafting Crafted with Workbench Tier 4 Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Display your pet egg drops received from bosses! Pet egg stands are decorative blocks used for the player to display any type of pet spawn egg . There are five color variants: white, cyan, red, blue, and purple.

Usage [ ]

Displaying [ ]

Players can display any type of pet spawn egg on pet egg stands. Each stand can hold up to one item .

Trivia [ ]
