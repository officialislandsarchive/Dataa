Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

Firefly wall lamps can be crafted in the lighting station if the player owns the lighting station gamepass .

Usage [ ]

Colored light [ ]

Firefly wall lamps emit different colored light based on which color firefly is inside. It emits white light when placed, but can be changed to yellow, green, red, blue, or purple light by clicking/tapping on it while holding a firefly jar . Breaking the lamp will drop the lamp and the firefly jar inside of it.

History [ ]

Trivia [ ]
