Mud Pile Type of block Solid block Recipe 2 Tall Grass 20 Grass 1 Black Truffle Crafted with Animal Bench Required skill Level 37 Animal Care Best tool Luminous Transparent The mud pile is the pig's counterpart of a hay bed , it is unlocked at level 37 in Animal Care . It was added in the February 5, 2021 Pigs and Portals update.
