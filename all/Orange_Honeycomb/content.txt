Orange Honeycomb Recipe 8 Orange 8 Honeycomb Crafted with Cooking Table Food Processor Sell price 704 - 1,056 coins Hot sell price 1,056 - 1,584 coins Required skill Level 65 Cooking An orange honeycomb is a food item that does not heal the player or grant any additional effect when consumed.

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

Name Recipe Crafting station Required skill Orange Honeycomb 8 Orange 8 Honeycomb Cooking Table Food Processor Level 65 Cooking

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating an orange honeycomb does not restore any HP or grant any additional effect.

Selling [ ]

Orange honeycombs can be sold to Petur for 704 - 1,056 coins each, or 1,056 - 1,584 coins if orange honeycombs are a hot offer.
