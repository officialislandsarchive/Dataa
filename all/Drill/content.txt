Usage [ ]

Drilling [ ]

To use a drill, it first must be powered with coal or petrified petroleum , either manually or via its conveyor input with a coal totem . Doing so will power the drill for approximately 30 seconds. Drills can only drill copper or gold deposits , and after placing it on top of a deposit and powering it, it will start extracting copper or gold ore respectively and output it from its output conveyor. It takes 5 seconds to extract a copper ore and 2 minutes to extract a gold ore.

Trivia [ ]

The drill animations will break if the player starts and stops it twice without leaving the game or breaking it. The drill has small meters that start to move around once the drill is fueled. Before the January 22, 2021 update, the drill used to take 50 seconds to drill out gold ore from a gold deposit .
