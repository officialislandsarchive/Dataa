Snowman Pet Spawn Egg The snowman pet spawn egg is a spawn egg used to spawn a snowman pet . It cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Christmas presents [ ]

Opening a Christmas present 2021 has a 0.33% chance of rewarding a snowman pet spawn egg.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

To spawn a snowman pet , the player must be the owner of the island they are currently on. While holding the egg, clicking or tapping anywhere on their screen will spawn a snowman pet.
