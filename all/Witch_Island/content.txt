The Witch Island is an island that was accessible during the Halloween 2020 event. It was accessible by going through the portal in front of the Halloween mansion in the Hub .

Mobs [ ]

The Witch Island had 4 hostile mobs all with relatively equal spawn chances. The island also had the Grand High Witch which was the first boss added to Islands.

Skeleton Armored Skeleton Pump Pump King Grand High Witch

Trivia [ ]

This was the first event exclusive location in Islands. This island was the first island to have a boss mob.
