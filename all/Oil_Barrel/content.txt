Oil Barrel Recipe 6 Oil Fuel Crafted with Fuel Barrel Filler Sell price 500 coins An oil barrel is an item that can be obtained by pumping oil into a fuel barrel filler .

Obtaining [ ]

Transforming [ ]

Oil barrels can be obtained by pumping oil through pipes into a fuel barrel filler . The oil barrels are outputted to the back of the industrial machine and onto a conveyor belt .

Input Item transformer Output 6 Oil Fuel ➡ Fuel Barrel Filler ➡ 1 Oil Barrel

Usage [ ]

Selling [ ]

Oil barrels can be sold to Doug for 500 coins each.
