Pine Tree Type: Pine Duration to grow: 90 seconds Pine trees are a tree introduced upon the game's release.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Pine trees are grown from pine saplings , which can be purchased from Cletus for 250 coins in the Hub . The approximate growing time for the saplings is 90 seconds from planting. There must be at least 2 unoccupied blocks to be able to grow.

Drops [ ]

When cut down, the player will receive:

3-4 Pine Wood 1 Pine Sapling

Usage [ ]

Pine trees can be harvested with leaf clippers to drop 2 leaves .

Trivia [ ]

This and the oak tree are the first 2 trees in the game.

This, the hickory tree , the cherry blossom tree , the spirit tree and the palm tree are the only trees whose color is not affected by winter.
