Infernal Dragon Egg Unopened Opened Type of block Solid block; Interactable Dropped by Infernal Dragon Best tool Luminous Transparent Description A reward from killing the Infernal Dragon! The infernal dragon egg is a roulette block that can be obtained by defeating the Infernal Dragon .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

The Infernal Dragon is guaranteed to drop one infernal dragon egg upon defeat. However, if too many players are fighting the dragon, only a set amount of players will receive an egg.

Loot [ ]

Infernal dragon eggs can be opened by holding "F" on PC, holding the button on mobile, or holding X on Xbox while hovering over it. Once opened, the infernal dragon egg will scroll through the items like a roulette wheel and reward one item, similar to treasure chests and cauldrons.

The listed reward chances are community made and may not be accurate .

Trivia [ ]

There is currently a bug where players may be unable to collect underworld tokens rewarded by an infernal dragon egg. This can be fixed by mining the egg or the block under the egg, which will automatically drop the rewarded item into the player's inventory once broken.
