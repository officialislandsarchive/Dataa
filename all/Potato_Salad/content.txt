Potato Salad Recipe 12 Potato 4 Tomato 4 Mayonnaise  Jar Crafted with Cooking Table Food Processor Effect +10% crop seed yield multiplier (Currently cannot be stacked with Gondo Datshi ) Sell price 1,136 - 1,704 coins Hot sell price 1,704 - 2,556 coins Required skill Level 13 Cooking Description Consume for +10% crop seed yield multiplier. Potato salad is a food item that grants a +10% crop seed yield multiplier when consumed.

Since the March 25, 2022 update, its crop seed yield multiplier of potato salad is currently cannot be stacked with gondo datshi 's crop seed yield multiplier.

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

Name Recipe Crafting station Required skill Potato Salad 12 Potato 4 Tomato 4 Mayonnaise Jar Cooking Table Food Processor Level 13 Cooking

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating a potato salad grants +10% crop seed yield multiplier for 15 minutes, increasing the chances of receiving an extra seed from harvesting non- fruit crops where applicable.

Its crop seed yield multiplier effect of potato salads currently cannot be stacked with gondo datshis .

Selling [ ]

Potato salads can be sold to Petur for 1,136 - 1,704 coins each, or 1,704 - 2,556 coins if potato salad is a hot offer.
