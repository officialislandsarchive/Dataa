Blueberry Seeds Planted on Grass Buy cost 1,500 coins Blueberry seeds are seeds used to grow blueberries .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Blueberry seeds can be purchased from Cletus during fall on the player's island , selling one for 1,500 coins . Players can only purchase 2 seeds before Cletus has to restock.

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a blueberry bush will give 1 - 2 blueberries and a 1% chance of getting a blueberry seed.

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Blueberry seeds can be planted on grass to be grown into a blueberry bush.

Growth [ ]

A blueberry bush grows through three stages after it is planted. Its first growth stage is a small seed. It becomes a bush in its second stage, and produces berries in its third growth stage. Its growth time is not affected by water nor basic fertilizer .
