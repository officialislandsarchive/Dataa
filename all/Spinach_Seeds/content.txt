Spinach Seeds Planted on Farmland Buy cost 550 Required skill Level 22 Farming Description Feed spinach to animals for small permanent productivity boost.

Spinach Seeds are Seeds used to grow and farm Spinach . They can be bought from Cletus for 550 Coins , after reaching level 22 in the Farming skill . There is also a small chance of getting an additional seed when harvesting spinach.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Spinach Seeds can be bought from Cletus after reaching level 22 in the Farming skill . They don't require any skill level to plant or grow.

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a Spinach crop will give at least 1 Spinach Seed, but also has a small chance of giving an additional seed. This chance can be increased by purchasing the Pro Gamepass .

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Spinach Seeds can be planted on Farmland, a.k.a. plowed Grass ; To produce Spinach.

Growing [ ]

Spinach Seeds have 4 growth phases, starting with a seed and then sprouting.

Spinach Seeds Growth Phases Phase 0 Phase 1 Phase 2 Phase 3 Time since planted 0m 0s 1m 30s 3m 5s 4m 35s Note: The durations of time above are on an unfertilized and unwatered plant

Trivia [ ]

Spinach is the only thing that can be fed to any animal. This seed looks very similar to Cactus Seeds .
