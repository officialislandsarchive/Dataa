Firefly garden lamps are decorative blocks that can be crafted in a lighting station after purchasing the gamepass . It emits different color light based on the type of firefly placed inside.

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

Firefly garden lights can only be crafted in a lighting station by players that own its gamepass.

Usage [ ]

Colored light [ ]

Firefly garden lamps can be used to light up areas or just for decoration. Clicking/tapping on it while holding a firefly jar will change the color of the light to the firefly's color. When first placed, it emits white light. If the player adds a firefly inside the lamp, and there was a firefly added already, the firefly already added will be given back to the player.

History [ ]

September 6, 2020 Added firefly garden lights.

Trivia [ ]

Despite being crafted with a yellow firefly jar it won't emit yellow light unless a yellow firefly is added inside.
