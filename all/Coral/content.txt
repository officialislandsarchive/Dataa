Coral Teal Yellow Pink Blue Type of block Non-solid block Dropped by Angry Crab Spawns Ponds in the Hub and Slime Island Best tool Luminous Transparent Coral are non-solid blocks that naturally generate in ponds and can be dropped by defeating angry crabs .

Obtaining [ ]

Natural generation [ ]

Different coral naturally generate in the ponds in the Hub and Slime Island .

Mob drop [ ]

Angry crabs are guaranteed to drop 5 of a particular coral upon defeat.

Fish tank blueprints [ ]

Prior to May 13, 2022 , breaking naturally-generated coral had a small chance of rewarding the player with a fish tank decoration blueprint.

Fish tank castle blueprint . Fish tank anchor blueprint . Fish tank shipwreck blueprint . Fish tank treasure chest blueprint .
