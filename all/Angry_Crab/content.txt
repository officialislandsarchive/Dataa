Angry Crab Health 150 HP Behavior Hostile Attack strength 10 HP Drops Coral Sea Grass Coconut Sand Jukebox Disk Location Pirate Island Angry crabs are hostile mobs that spawn on the beaches of Pirate Island .

Spawning [ ]

Angry crabs spawn on Pirate Island's beaches .

Drops [ ]

Defeating an angry crab rewards 6 Combat XP (24 with the 4x daily bonus) and 6 Light Melee , Heavy Melee , Magic , or Archery XP (24 with the 4x daily bonus) depending on the weapon the player used to defeat it.

Trivia [ ]

When an angry crab tries to move underwater, it may flip forward and get stuck in the ground. Angry crabs sometimes flee from players when their HP is low.
