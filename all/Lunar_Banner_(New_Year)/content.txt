Lunar Banner Type of block Solid block Buy cost 2,500 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent The lunar banner is a furniture block that could have been purchased from Yuzi .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

The lunar banner could have been purchased from Yuzi for 2,500 coins during the Lunar New Year event .

Red envelope [ ]

Opening a red envelope has a 7.14% chance of rewarding a lunar banner.
