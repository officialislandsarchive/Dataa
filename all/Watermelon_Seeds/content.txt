Watermelon Seeds Planted on Farmland Buy cost 1,850 coins Description Watermelon can only grow in the summer season. Watermelon seeds are seeds used to grow watermelons .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Watermelon seeds can be obtained during summer if Cletus visits the player's island , whom has a 25% chance of visiting, selling one for 1,850 coins . Players can only purchase 3 seeds before Cletus has to restock.

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a watermelon crop will give 1 watermelon seed and 1 - 3 watermelons .

Note: Harvesting watermelons does not give you any more watermelon seeds.

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Watermelon seeds can be planted on farmland and will take 5 minutes to grow. The growing process can be sped up by 20% by watering the crop with a watering can or basic sprinkler, by 20% using basic fertilizer , or by 30% when using both methods.

Growing [ ]

Watermelon seeds have 4 growth phases, starting at a seed to a full watermelon.

Trivia [ ]

The third growth stage of the watermelon will hover slightly above the dirt it's planted on. Contrary to its description, watermelon is not restricted to growing in summer and can be grown in any season .
