Spring Rolls Recipe 1 Dough 1 Carrot 2 Spinach Crafted with Cooking Table Food Processor Heals 40 HP Spring rolls are a food item that heals 40 HP when consumed. It can be crafted without any Cooking level requirement.

It currently cannot be sold to Petur .
