Apple Pie Recipe 1 Uncooked Apple Pie Crafted with Campfire Small Furnace Desert Furnace Industrial Oven Heals 30 HP Sell price 120 - 180 coins Hot sell price 180 - 270 coins Apple pie is a food item .

Obtaining [ ]

Cooking [ ]

Cooking an uncooked apple pie in a campfire , small furnace , desert furnace , or industrial oven will create an apple pie.

Input Output 1 Uncooked Apple Pie 1 Apple Pie

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating an apple pie restores 30 HP.

Selling [ ]

Apple pie can be sold to Petur for 120 - 180 coins each, or 180 - 270 coins if apple pie is a hot offer.

History [ ]

June 26, 2020 Added apple pie. January 8, 2022 Changed the base selling price from 150 coins to 180 - 270 coins. January 15, 2022 Changed the base selling price from 180 - 270 coins to 120 - 180 coins.
