Spider Pet Spawn Egg Dropped by Zombie Cletus The spider pet spawn egg is a spawn egg used to spawn a spider pet .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Defeating Zombie Cletus has a 0.33% chance of rewarding a spider pet spawn egg.

Cauldrons [ ]

Opening cauldrons have a 1% chance of rewarding a spider pet spawn egg.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

To spawn a spider pet , the player must be the owner of the island they are currently on. While holding the egg, clicking or tapping anywhere on their screen will spawn a spider pet.

History [ ]

October 22, 2022 Added spider pet spawn eggs. November 4, 2022 Spider pet spawn eggs can now be dropped, traded , and put in vending machines and chests .
