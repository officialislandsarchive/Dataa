Stacked Lunar Lantern Type of block Solid block Buy cost 2,500 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent The stacked lunar lantern is a furniture block that can be purchased from Yuzi .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

The stacked lunar lantern can be purchased from Yuzi for 2,500 coins .

Red envelope [ ]

Opening a red envelope has a 7.14% chance of rewarding a stacked lunar lantern.
