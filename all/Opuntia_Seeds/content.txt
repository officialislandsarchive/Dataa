Opuntia Seeds Planted on Farmland Buy cost 4,200 Coins Required skill Level 72 Combat Opuntia seeds are seeds that can be grown into opuntias .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Opuntia seeds can be purchased from Maria for 4,200 coins each after reaching level 72 Combat .

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting an opuntia crop will give 1 - 3 opuntias and 1 opuntia seed, with a 5% chance of getting an additional or extra seed. This chance may be increased for owners of the PRO Gamepass .

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Opuntia seeds can be planted on farmland to grow into opuntias.
