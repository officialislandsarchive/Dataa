Flower pots are decorative blocks that are crafted with flowers and bricks .

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

Flower pots can only be crafted by players who own the floral bench gamepass, which costs 149 Robux , in a floral bench. Any kind of flowers can be used in this recipe.

Name Recipe Crafting station Blue Flower Pot 1 Blue Flower 3 Brick Floral Bench Red Flower Pot 1 Red Flower 3 Brick Floral Bench Yellow Flower Pot 1 Yellow Flower 3 Brick Floral Bench
