Slime King Pet Spawn Egg Dropped by Slime King The Slime King pet spawn egg is a spawn egg used to spawn a Slime King pet . It cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

The Slime King has a 0.33% chance of dropping a Slime King pet spawn egg upon defeat.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

To spawn a Slime King pet , the player must be the owner of the island they are currently on. While holding the egg, clicking or tapping anywhere on their screen will spawn a Slime King pet.
