Spirit Statue Type of block Solid block; Luminous Buy cost 500 Spirit Jars Best tool Luminous Transparent The spirit statue is a luminous furniture block that can purchased from Catherine .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Spirit statues can be purchased from Catherine for 500 spirit jars each.

Treasure chests [ ]

Opening a Spirit Island treasure chest has a small chance of rewarding a spirit statue, having a rarity of 'mythic'.

History [ ]

June 30, 2021 Added spirit statues. April 16, 2022 Spirit statues can now be obtained from Spirit Island treasure chests . June 3, 2022 Spirit statues can now be purchased with 500 spirit jars instead of 500 spirits.

Trivia [ ]

This is the second most expensive item able to be purchased from Catherine's shop . Spirit statues were the most expensive item in Catherine's shop before the June 18, 2022 update, where spirit essence holders were released.
