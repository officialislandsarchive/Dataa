Buffalkor Portal Type of block Solid block Recipe 1 Buffalkor Portal Shard 120 Iron Ingot Crafted with Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4 Required skill Level 7 Combat (To use) Best tool Luminous Transparent Description A placeable portal that allows quick-travel to Buffalkor Island. The buffalkor portal is a placeable item that allows the player to travel to the Buffalkor Island rather than going through the portal on the Slime Island .

Usage [ ]

Traveling [ ]

Buffalkor portals can be used to transport players to Buffalkor Island from a player's island after reaching Combat level 7. If the skill prerequisite is not met, a UI element will pop up, stating that the player does not meet the required skill level.
