Desert Portal Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe 1 Desert Portal Shard 120 Red Sandstone Brick 5 Cactus Crafted with Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4 Required skill Level 36 Combat (to use) Best tool Luminous Transparent Description A placeable portal that allows quick-travel to Desert Island. A desert portal is a block that allows players to directly travel to Desert Island from a player island .

Usage [ ]

Traveling [ ]

Desert portals can be used to transport players to Desert Island from a player's island after reaching Combat level 36. If the skill prerequisite is not met, a UI element will pop up, stating that the player does not meet the required skill level.
