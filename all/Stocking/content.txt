Stocking Santa Stocking Elf Stocking Snowman Stocking Snowflake Stocking Cookie Stocking Type of block Solid block; Interactable Buy cost 35 snowflake tickets Best tool Luminous Transparent Description A stocking wall decor to put presents in! Stockings are furniture blocks that come in five different variants: Santa, elf, snowman, snowflake, and cookie. Each stocking can store up to 15 items each.

All five stocking variants can be bought from Eddie for 35 snowflake tickets each.
