We've been experiencing major server lag when multiple islands with large factories are in the same server. When too many dropped items exist in the server, the physics will slow to a crawl. This unfortunately ruins the experience for everyone on the server. We're experimenting several ways to solve this problem; for now, we need to lower the item limit to keep servers in a healthy state. VIP servers still have the 80 item limit -- this change only applies to public servers.
