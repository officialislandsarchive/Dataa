Lemon Tree Sapling Spring Summer Fall Winter Type of block Solid block Spawns Player's Island Best tool Drops 3 - 4 Oak Wood 1 Lemon Tree Sapling Luminous Transparent The lemon tree is a fruit tree added in the June 26, 2020 update.

The canopies of lemon trees change color based on the current in-game season ; dark green in spring , light green in summer , dark red in fall , and white in winter , but its sapling always has light green leaves on its canopy.

Obtaining [ ]

Natural generation [ ]

Players have a 25% chance of starting with a lemon tree on their island upon island creation.

Harvesting [ ]

Lemon tree saplings have a 3% chance of dropping whenever a lemon is harvested from the tree. It can be planted on grass to be grown into a lemon tree. It takes 1 minute and 50 seconds for a lemon tree sapling to grow into a lemon tree.

Usage [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Lemons grow every season on lemon trees and can be obtained by harvesting them from the tree. There can be up to three lemons on a tree at a time, and whenever a lemon is harvested, there is a 3% chance of getting an additional lemon tree sapling. Harvesting a lemon yields 6 Farming XP (24 XP with the 4x daily bonus). It takes x minutes for a lemon tree to grow a lemon, adding up to a total of xx minutes to grow three lemons.

Lemon trees can be harvested with leaf clippers to drop 2 leaves .

History [ ]

June 26, 2020 Added lemon trees.

Trivia [ ]

If a lemon tree is chopped down while having lemons on it, it will only drop the sapling and not any of the lemons.
