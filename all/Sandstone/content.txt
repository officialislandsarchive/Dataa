Sandstone Sandstone Rock Type of block Solid block Spawns Desert Island Best tool Luminous Transparent Sandstone is a block that can be mined from sandstone rocks .

Obtaining [ ]

Mining [ ]

Sandstone rocks naturally spawn in the Hub Desert and Desert Island . Mining a sandstone rock grants the player 1 - 2 sandstone and 8 Mining XP (33 with the 4x daily bonus) per stage, resulting in a total of 2 - 4 sandstone and 16 Mining XP (66 with the 4x daily bonus) per full rock.

Totems [ ]

A sandstone totem will spawn sandstone rocks in a 5 block radius. After the totem has spawned 6 sandstone rocks or has run out of space, the totem will begin to mine the rocks and hold rewards equivalent to a half of an ore rock, which can be collected by interacting with the totem.

A sandstone totem placed next to a conveyor belt will drop sandstone onto the conveyor belt.

Transforming [ ]

Sandstone can be transformed into sandstone bricks in a stonecutter or an industrial stonecutter , or into smooth sandstone in a polishing station or an industrial polishing station .
