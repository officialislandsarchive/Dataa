Philadelphia Roll Recipe 1 Sushi Roll 1 Cut Salmon Crafted with Cooking Table Food Processor Effect +25% tree fruit harvest yield Required skill Level 63 Cooking Description Consume for +25% tree fruit harvest yield. A philadelphia roll is a food item that grants +25% tree fruit harvest yield when consumed.

It currently cannot be sold to Petur .

Crafting [ ]

Name Recipe Crafting station Required skill Philadelphia Roll 1 Sushi Roll 1 Cut Salmon Cooking Table Food Processor Level 63 Cooking

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating a philadelphia roll grants +25% tree fruit harvest yield for 15 minutes, affecting the harvest of apples , oranges , lemons , avocados , coconuts , kiwis , and plums from their respective tree .
