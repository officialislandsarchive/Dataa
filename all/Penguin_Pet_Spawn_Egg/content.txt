Penguin Pet Spawn Egg A penguin pet spawn egg is a spawn egg used to spawn a penguin pet .

Obtaining [ ]

Christmas presents [ ]

Opening a Christmas present 2022 has a 0.1% chance of rewarding a penguin pet spawn egg.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

To spawn a penguin pet , the player must be the owner of the island they are currently on. While holding the egg, clicking or tapping anywhere on their screen will spawn a penguin pet.

History [ ]

December 16, 2022 Added penguin pet spawn eggs. January 6, 2023 Penguin pet spawn eggs can now be dropped, traded , and put in vending machines and chests .
