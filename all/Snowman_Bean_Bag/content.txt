Snowman Bean Bag Type of block Solid block; Seat Best tool Luminous Transparent The snowman bean bag is a furniture block that has a chance to be obtained from Christmas present 2021s .

Obtaining [ ]

Christmas presents [ ]

Opening a Christmas present 2021 has a 10% chance of rewarding a snowman bean bag.

The snowman bean bag has a seat, which means players, mobs , and villagers are able to sit on the bean bag.
