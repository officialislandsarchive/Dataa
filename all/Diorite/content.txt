Diorite Diorite Rock Type of block Solid block Spawns Hub Best tool Luminous Transparent Diorite is a block that can be mined from a diorite rock .

Obtaining [ ]

Mining [ ]

Diorite rocks naturally generate in the mining zone in the Hub . Mining a diorite rock yields 2 - 4 diorite after each stage and 3 Mining XP (6 with the 4x daily bonus), resulting in a total of 4 - 8 diorite and 9 Mining XP (18 with the 4x daily bonus) per rock.

Totem [ ]

Diorite totems can be purchased from Arius in the Hub for 8,000 coins . A diorite totem will spawn diorite rocks around it when placed, or if placed next to a conveyor belt , the totem will drop diorite directly onto the conveyor.

Transforming [ ]

Diorite can be transformed into diorite bricks in a stonecutter or an industrial stonecutter or smooth diorite in a polishing station or a industrial polishing station .
