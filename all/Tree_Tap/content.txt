Tree Tap Recipe 35 Iron Ingot Crafted with Maple Workbench A tree tap is an item used to tap maple sap from maple trees .

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

Name Recipe Crafting station Tree Tap 35 Iron Ingot Maple Workbench

Usage [ ]

Tapping [ ]

Tree taps can be placed on maple trees to extract maple sap . It can be connected to a wood tube to transport the sap to other areas of the player's island .
