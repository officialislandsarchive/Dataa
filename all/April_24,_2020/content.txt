April 24, 2020 Basic Information Day released April 24, 2020 Version number 2 Version Navigator Previous version Next version April 10, 2020 April 27, 2020 The April 24, 2020 update is the first update released for Islands . This update is different from the first version, removing and modifying existing items in the game. The clay totem , clay nodes , clay , and bricks were added to the game.

Mobile support is implemented. VIP Servers are now purchasable. Bricks can be smelted from clay. Tree saplings can be bought from Cletus .

Modified starter island design. Player limit is modified to 30. Modified island teleport location. Removed the natural growth of default trees.
