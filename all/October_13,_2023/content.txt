The October 13, 2023 update, or the Halloween update is an update that initiated the Halloween Event.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added The Cursed Wraith . Added The Hooded Figure . Added Haunted Mansion Event Tickets . Added The Black Cat Pet Spawn Egg . Added new furniture blocks : Added Mansion Cabinet. Added Mansion Bench. Added Mansion Desk. Added Mansion Bust Statues. Added Mansion Bed. Added Mansion Rocking Horse. Added Mansion Bed. Added Mansion Grandfather Clock. Added Wraith Boss Plushie.

Modifications [ ]

Updated the Hub . Changed the sell price of lemons from 20 coins to 26 coins. Changed the sell price of spinach from 18 coins to 14 coins.

October 20, 2023 [ ]

Added Cauldrons . Added The Cursed Grimoire . Added The Cursed Hammer . Added The Iridescent Flower . Added The 2023 Halloween Trophy.
