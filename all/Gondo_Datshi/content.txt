Gondo Datshi Recipe 1 Tomato 1 Yak Butter 2 Chili Pepper 1 Egg Crafted with Cooking Table Food Processor Heals 40 HP Effect +10% crop seed yield multiplier (Currently cannot be stacked with Potato Salad ) Required skill Level 49 Cooking Description Consume for +10% crop seed yield multiplier. A gondo datshi is a food item that heals 40 HP and grants +10% crop seed yield multiplier when consumed.

Its crop seed seed yield multiplier of gondo datshi currently cannot be stacked with potato salad 's crop seed seed yield multiplier.

It currently cannot be sold to Petur .

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating a gondo datshi restores 40 HP and grants +10% crop seed yield multiplier for 5 minutes, increasing the chances of receiving an extra seed from harvesting non- fruit crops where applicable.

Its crop seed yield multiplier effect of gondo datshis currently cannot be stacked with potato salads .
