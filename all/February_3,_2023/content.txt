February 3, 2023 Basic Information Day released February 3, 2023 Version number 115 Version Navigator Previous version Next version January 20, 2023 February 17, 2023 The February 3, 2023 update, also known as the achievement points update, is an update in Islands that new achievements , concluded the Lunar New Year 2023 event, and added medals .

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added achievements : Added Mastery of Fishing. Added Mastery of Farming. Added Mastery of Forging. Added Mastery of Combat. Added Mastery of Light Melee. Added Mastery of Heavy Melee. Added Mastery of Magic. Added Mastery of Archery. Added Mastery of Mining. Added Mastery of Woodcutting. Added Mastery of Economy. Added Mastery of Cooking. Added Mastery of Animal Husbandry. Added Medals . Added Single Medal Display Stands .

Modifications [ ]

All Lunar New Year 2023 items are now tradable. Updated block sounds for grass , wood , stone , and glass blocks . Increased skill levels' maximum cap from 99 to 100. Pirate and Maple travel merchants teleport you back to the Hub . Increased the sell price of wheat from 7 coins to 12 coins. Decreased the sell price of radishes from 64 coins to 60 coins.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed bug preventing achievement rewards to be given out correctly.

February 5, 2023 [ ]

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed bug causing some players to get stuck on "Downloading Profile..." when loading into the game.

February 6, 2023 [ ]

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed bug causing some players' profile slots to not load properly, causing them to be kicked from the game.
