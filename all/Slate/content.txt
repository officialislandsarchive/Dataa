Slate Slate Rock Spawns Buffalkor Island Best tool Luminous Transparent Slate is a block that can be mined from slate rocks .

Obtaining [ ]

Mining [ ]

Slate rocks naturally generate on Buffalkor Island in the hole in front of the Slime Portal . Mining a slate rock yields 1 - 3 slate after each stage and 6 Mining XP (24 with the 4x daily bonus), resulting in a total of 2 - 6 slate and 12 Mining XP (48 with the 4x daily bonus) per rock.

Totems [ ]

Slate totems can be purchased from Arius in the Hub for 30,000 coins after reaching level 32 Mining . A slate totem will spawn slate rocks around it when placed, or if placed next to a conveyor belt , the totem will drop slate directly onto the conveyor.

Transforming [ ]

Slate can be transformed into slate bricks in a stonecutter or an industrial stonecutter or smooth slate in a polishing station or an industrial polishing station .
