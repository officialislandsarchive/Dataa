Gold Deposit Type of block Solid block Spawns Gold Resource Island Best tool Drops 15 - 20 Gold Ore Luminous Transparent A gold deposit is a resource block which generates in gold resource islands spawned using a gold resource deed . Prior to June 3, 2022 , gold resource islands could spawn naturally if two copper resource islands were already present.

Obtaining [ ]

Resource islands [ ]

One permanent gold deposit naturally generates as part of gold resource islands spawned using gold resource deeds . Gold resource deeds will only spawn a gold resource island if the limit of 15 resource islands of any kind has not been reached. A gold resource island deposit deed can be purchased from Jade for 400,000 coins, once every 4 hours.

Breaking [ ]

Breaking a gold deposit will drop 15 - 20 gold ore , but not the deposit itself.

Usage [ ]

Drilling [ ]

Placing a drill on top of a gold deposit and fueling it with coal or petrified petroleum will produce one gold ore every 2 minutes.

History [ ]

October 3, 2020 Added gold deposits. June 3, 2022 Gold deposits no longer spawn naturally. Gold deposits now require gold resource deeds to spawn. June 10, 2022 Fixed a bug that didn't allow gold deposits to spawn when using a gold resource deed. Increased the limit of gold deposits from 2 to 15.

Trivia [ ]
