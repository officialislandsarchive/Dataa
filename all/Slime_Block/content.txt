Slime Block Green Blue Pink Type of block Solid block; Bouncy Recipe 10 respective Slime Balls Crafted with Workbench Tier 1 Workbench Tier 2 Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4 Best tool Luminous Transparent A slime block is a block that players can bounce on. There are three different types of slime blocks, each corresponding to a different slime variant. The green slime block bounces the player the least and the pink variant the most.

History [ ]

July 23, 2021 Added green slime blocks, blue slime blocks, and pink slime blocks. June 3, 2022 Removed the bounciness from slime blocks. June 10, 2022 Fixed the bounciness of slime blocks.

Trivia [ ]

Placing a slab , stair , conveyor , spawn block , or carpet on top of a slime block still causes the player to bounce on the slime block. The bounciness of slime blocks is not affected by any status effect affecting the player's speed or jump power.
