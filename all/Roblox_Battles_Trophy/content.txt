Roblox Battles Trophy Type of block Unsolid block Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Limited edition trophy found only during the 2020 Roblox Battles Event. The Roblox Battles trophy is a trophy that could have been obtained during the Roblox Battles event.

Obtaining [ ]

Scavenger list [ ]

The Roblox Battles trophy could have been obtained by completing the scavenger list given by the Scavenger on their island during the Roblox Battles event. Once completed, the player could interact with him to claim the trophy, causing him to leave after the trophy was claimed. To complete it, the following items needed to be collected:

5 stone 5 fish (any type) 2 bread 10 slime balls (any type) 5 coral (any type) 1 tomato

Trivia [ ]

It is the only trophy that does not emit a sparkling particle effect when placed or held. It is the smallest trophy, with block dimensions of 1x1x1, whereas the other trophies take up a larger space. The Roblox Battles trophy lacks collision, meaning that players can phase through it as if there was nothing there.
