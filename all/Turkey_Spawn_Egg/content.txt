Turkey Spawn Egg Buy cost Tier 1: 5,000 coins Description Spawn a turkey with tier 1/2/3 trait maximums. Eats berries. A turkey spawn egg is a spawn egg used to spawn turkeys on player islands . It exists in 3 tiers.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Tier 1 turkey spawn eggs can be purchased from Emma for 5,000 coins after completing her quest.

Breeding [ ]

Feeding two turkeys potions of fertility during the day will create a spawn egg whose tier is based on the two "parent" animals' stats.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

To spawn a turkey, the player must be the owner of the Island they are currently on. They can hatch the egg by selecting and holding anywhere on their screen if their small animal limit has not been reached.

History [ ]

November 27, 2021 Added turkey spawn eggs. April 16, 2022 Changed the model of tier 2 turkey spawn eggs.

Trivia [ ]

From February 19, 2022 to April 16, 2022 , the model of tier 2 turkey spawn eggs were the same as duck spawn eggs .
