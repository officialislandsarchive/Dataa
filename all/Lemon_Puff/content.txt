A lemon puff is a food item that grants +20% reward luck when mining when consumed.

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

Name Recipe Crafting station Required skill Lemon Puff 4 Lemon 12 Jar of Honey 4 Bread Cooking Table Food Processor Level 62 Cooking

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating a lemon puff grants +20% reward luck when mining for 15 minutes.

Selling [ ]

Lemon puffs can be sold to Petur for 768 - 1,152 coins each, or 1,152 - 1,728 coins if lemon puffs are a hot offer.

History [ ]
