Fishing Ticket Description A ticket obtained from the Fishing Festival. Use to claim rewards from the fishing merchant. Fishing tickets were the official currency of the 2022 Fishing Festival event, granting the player the ability to purchase various limited items and trades. The item cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines , chests , and trash cans .

Obtaining [ ]

2022 Fishing Festival [ ]

During the 2022 Fishing Festival event, players could have competed in 5-minute fishing contests with other players in the server they are in to earn fishing tickets. The amount of fishing tickets a player received at the end of the contest depended on the amount of points they scored on the leaderboard.

Rank Prize 1st 10 fishing tickets 2nd 5 fishing tickets 3rd and under 2 fishing tickets
