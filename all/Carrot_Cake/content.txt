Carrot Cake Recipe 1 Carrot Cake Batter Crafted with Campfire Small Furnace Desert Furnace Industrial Oven Effect +15% more output when crafting in the furniture workbench Sell price 480 - 720 coins Hot sell price 720 - 1,080 coins Description Eat for +15% more output when crafting in carpentry station. A carrot cake is a food item that grants +15% more output when crafting in the furniture workbench when consumed.

Obtaining [ ]

Cooking [ ]

Cooking carrot cake batter in a campfire , small furnace , desert furnace , or industrial oven will create carrot cake.

Input Output 1 Carrot Cake Batter 1 Carrot Cake

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating a carrot cake grants +15% more output when crafting in the furniture workbench for 15 minutes. This means that every block crafted in it has a 15% chance of granting an extra block.

Selling [ ]

Carrot cakes can be sold to Petur for 480 - 720 coins , or 720 - 1,080 coins if carrot cake is a hot offer.
