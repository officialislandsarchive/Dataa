Tiger Jacuzzi Type of block Solid block; Seat Buy cost 50,000 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent The tiger jacuzzi is a furniture block that can be purchased from Yuzi .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

The tiger jacuzzi can be purchased from Yuzi for 50,000 coins .

Red envelope [ ]

Opening a red envelope has a 7.14% chance of rewarding a tiger jacuzzi.

Sitting [ ]

The tiger jacuzzi has two seats, which means players, mobs , and villagers are able to sit in the jacuzzi.
