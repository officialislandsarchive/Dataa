November 18, 2022 Basic Information Day released November 18, 2022 Version number 108 Version Navigator Previous version Next version November 11, 2022 November 25, 2022 The November 18, 2022 update, also known as the PvP update, is a major update in Islands that added Taylor , re-added PvP, along with reworks and bug fixes.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Taylor . Added pastels . Re-added PvP.

Modifications [ ]

John no longer sells sewing buttons . PvP mode can now be toggled on or off through the Settings Menu . Wizard Boss now uses new arc effects with its charged up attacks, and attacks faster. Damage numbers when fighting mobs are now client side. Removed Hide Icons in the Permissions Menu. Added Hide Icons in the Settings Menu. Renamed gazebos . Increased the sell price of pineapple from 92 coins to 96 coins. Decreased the sell price of spinach from 18 coins to 15 coins.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed bug causing players to attack others without PvP toggled on. Fixed bug causing mobs not to take or deal damage to players.
