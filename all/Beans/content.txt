Beans Beans Seeds Planted on Trellis

Beans are an unimplemented vine crop in Islands .

Information [ ]

If beans would've been implemented on the date its models were added, June 26, 2020 , it would've been the second vine crop to exist in-game. Beans would've been planted in trellises with bean seeds . It is unknown why beans were never implemented.
