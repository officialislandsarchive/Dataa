Electronics [ ]

For the electricity from the input(s) to pass through, BOTH inputs need to have at least one power unit. This is sometimes confused with the combiner , which only needs ONE input to be on to work. It is usually accompanied with the or gate , xor gate , combiner, and/or splitter .
