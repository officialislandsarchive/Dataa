Kue Keju Recipe 1 Egg 2 Cheese 1 Dough Crafted with Cooking Table Food Processor Heals 25 HP Kue keju is a food item that heals 25 HP when consumed. It can be crafted without any Cooking level requirement.

It currently cannot be sold to Petur .
