Bat Boss Token Dropped by Crab Golem Draven The Bat Boss token is an item that was used to summon Draven in the Emerald Mines during the Mining Event 2023 .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Crab golems had a 50% of dropping a Bat Boss token, and Draven was guaranteed to drop 33 - 50 tokens upon defeat.

Participation [ ]

Completing a round in the Emerald Mines rewarded the player 0 - 50 tokens depending on the player's performance.

Usage [ ]

Summoning [ ]

250 Bat Boss tokens were required to summon Draven in its spawn area in the Emerald Mines during the Mining Event 2023 .

History [ ]

April 28, 2023 Added Bat Boss tokens. May 12, 2023 Bat Boss tokens are no longer obtainable.
