Poached Duck Egg Recipe 1 Duck Egg Crafted with Campfire Small Furnace Desert Furnace Heals 20 HP A poached duck egg is a food item obtained by cooking a duck egg .

It is currently cannot be sold to Petur .

Obtaining [ ]

Cooking [ ]

Cooking a duck egg in a campfire , small furnace , or desert furnace will create a poached duck egg.
