Ember Skull Behavior Passive Drops Zombie Cletus Token Location Player's Island An ember skull is a flying passive mob that spawned on the player's island at night during the Halloween 2022 event.

Spawning [ ]

Ember skulls spawned near trees on player islands during the night during the Halloween 2022 event. They acted similarly to fireflies , flying around aimlessly before despawning when it becomes daytime.

Catching [ ]

Ember skulls could have been caught with a net or golden net . When caught, the player received 15 - 55 Zombie Cletus tokens and 3 Animal Care XP (15 with the 4x daily bonus).

History [ ]

October 15, 2022 Added ember skulls. October 22, 2022 Decreased the number of Zombie Cletus tokens given when catching ember skulls. November 4, 2022 Ember skulls no longer spawn on player islands .
