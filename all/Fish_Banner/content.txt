The fish banner is a block that was obtainable from Oscar during the Fishing Festival event.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

The fish banner is an block that could be purchased from Oscar for 14 tickets. Tickets were obtained by competing in fishing contests on Fishing Festival Island or talking to Thomas during the Festival.
