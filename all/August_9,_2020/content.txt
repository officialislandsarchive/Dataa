The August 9, 2020 update, or more commonly known as the Skorp Islands update, is the 17th major Islands update. This update adds a new island to the game, as well as a new enemy, a new workbench, and a variety of new blocks.

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Islands [ ]

Desert Island has been added to the game The desert island key came alongside in order to enter the portal

Blocks/Items [ ]

Red sandstone has been added, available by destroying its node It can be crafted into its brick , brick slab , and stair variants. The brewing stand , a new crafting station , is now implemented in the game Grants the ability to craft regen potions and strength potions Dead bushes have been added to the game Hardened bow limbs are craftable, and are used to craft the artifact bow

Mobs [ ]

Introduced the iron skorp , ruby skorp , and gold skorp , all of which are hostile mobs located on Desert Island Obtain iron skorp scale , ruby skorp scale , and gold skorp scale by killing their skorp variety Obtain gold skorp claws by killing Gold Skorps Obtain ruby skorp stingers by killing Ruby Skorps Slimes are now able to drop ancient slime string .

Weapons [ ]

Basic bows and artifact bows , as well as arrows , have been added, and are able to be crafted in the anvil The vamp bow has been added, and requires a new blueprint to be crafted

Music Disks [ ]

Yellow jukebox disks , red jukebox disks , and blue jukebox disks can be obtained by killing hostile mobs

Modifications [ ]

Adventurer [ ]

Ivon now buys all skorp scales

Inventory [ ]

Improved inventory tooltips Selected item titles have been improved

Indicators [ ]

Taking damage now has an indicator with the damage taken

Game Translation [ ]

The game fully supports the French translation

Bug Fixes [ ]

Bows are easier to hit, and the artifact bow does more damage now Fixed an issue causing some users to not load into the game The regen potion works properly now
