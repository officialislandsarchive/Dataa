August 6, 2022 Basic Information Day released August 6, 2022 Version number 93 Version Navigator Previous version Next version July 30, 2022 August 13, 2022 The August 6, 2022 update, also known as the new totems update, is a minor update in Islands that added new crop totems and other small changes.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added new totems : Added spinach totems . Added dragonfruit totems . Added grape totems .

Modifications [ ]

Increased the damage dealt by the Slime Queen's scepter from 40 HP to 50 HP. Shots that hit mobs now heal the player's health. Changed the recipe of Slime Queen's scepter to require 10 pink slime crystals , down from 20. Slime Queen's scepter can now be placed into tool stands . Changed the rewards of a few desert quests . Reduced the interaction range of villagers Updated a few info booklets . Increased the sell price of cacti from 30 coins to 35 coins. Increased the sell price of pineapples from 72 coins to 92 coins. Decreased the sell price of spinach from 18 coins to 14 coins.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed whisper commands not registering.
