Antler Shield Type Heavy Melee/Shield Physical class Striking Attack damage 15 HP (Base) 23 HP (Critical)

The antler shield is a heavy melee weapon able to attack and defend from attacks.

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Any mob has a 0.3% chance to drop either an antler shield or antler hammer upon defeat provided that the player has the Might of Fhanhorn effect.

Usage [ ]

Attacking [ ]

At Heavy Melee level 1, the antler shield deals a base damage of 15 HP. It has the ability to damage multiple mobs in one swing. There's also a small chance of landing a critical hit when using this weapon, meaning the attack will deal 50% more damage. Its damage can be increased by leveling up the Heavy Melee skill.

Defending [ ]

Any damage taken from mobs will be reduced by 60% if the player is holding the antler shield.
