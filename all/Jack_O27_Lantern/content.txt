Jack O' Lantern Type of block Solid block; Luminous Best tool Luminous Transparent A jack o' lantern is a block that provides light.

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

Jack o' lanterns could have been crafted during the Halloween 2020 event with the recipe below:

Name Recipe Crafting station Before November 1, 2020 Jack O' Lantern 1 Pumpkin 1 Torch Workbench Tier 1 Workbench Tier 2 Workbench Tier 3

Cauldron [ ]

Opening a cauldron has a 10% chance of rewarding 5 - 10 jack o' lanterns, having a rarity of 'common'.
