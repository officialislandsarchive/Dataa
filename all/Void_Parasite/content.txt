Void Parasite Planted on Farmland Sell price 30 coins Required skill None Description Parasitic crop grown from corrupted seeds. Edible A void parasite is a crop that grows on farmland from void parasites seeds and naturally spawns in the Void Isles as foliage .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a void parasite plant yields 1 - 3 void parasites, 1 void parasite seed , and 30 Farming XP (60 with the 4x daily bonus). The chance of obtaining more than one void parasite per plant is increased by the player's Farming level and for owners of the VIP gamepass . Extra void parasite seeds cannot be yielded.

Void parasites also naturally spawn throughout the Void Isles as foliage . Void parasites harvested from the isles yield 1 void parasite and have a 3% chance to yield a void parasite seed.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of void parasites can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer .
