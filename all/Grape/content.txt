Grapes Seed cost 3,000 coins Planted on Trellis Sell price 22 coins Required skill Level 35 Farming (to sell) Edible A grape is a crop that grows on trellises from grape seeds .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a grape vine yields 1 - 3 grapes, 1 - 2 grape seeds , and 27 Farming XP (108 XP with the 4x daily bonus). The chance of obtaining more than one grape per plant is increased by the player's Farming level and for owners of the VIP gamepass . The 5% chance of obtaining an extra grape seed is increased for owners of the pro gamepass .

Totems [ ]

A grape totem will collect and replant grape in a 3-block radius, which can then be collected by interacting it. A grape totem placed next to a conveyor belt will drop the grapes onto the conveyor. This process cannot yield extra grape seeds.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of grapes can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer . If watered and fertilized, the growth time will be shortened by 30%.
