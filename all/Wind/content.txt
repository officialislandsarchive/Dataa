----------------------------------------

Wind was the first weather type added to the game, it was introduced in the March 13, 2021 update. It usually appears during Spring, but it can happen during any season .

Appearance [ ]

Wind is portrayed by translucent white lines of light blue moving horizontally. The lines of wind can curve freely.

Behavior [ ]

Wind can appear during any season. It can be seen throughout the entire map, including the Player's Island , Hub , and mob islands. Wind does not have a current use.

History [ ]

March 13, 2021 Added wind. April 4, 2021 Adjusted wind. June 30, 2021 Shifted wind above players heads.

Trivia [ ]

Wind is the first weather occurrence, the second being rain . Similar to rain , wind can be seen inside the Diamond Mines .
