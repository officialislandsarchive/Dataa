Usage [ ]

Ammunition [ ]

Iron long arrows are used as ammunition in longbows , as at least one is required to be in the player's inventory in order for the bow to fire. Shooting an iron long arrow deals an extra 15 - 20 HP to mobs .

Iron long arrows are a one-time use item , meaning they cannot be retrieved after they have been shot.
