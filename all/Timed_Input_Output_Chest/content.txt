A timed input/output chest is an automatable chest that dispenses items going through at set intervals. Crafting Timed Input/Output Chest 100 Red Bronze Ingot Workbench Tier 4 Automatic storage Timed input/output chests can hold 12 stacks of 60 items, adding up to a total of 720 items. The items can be added in either manually or through a conveyor belt at the front. The chest will begin to output items by the set interval shown in its UI screen, which can be toggled to output items immediately or up to every 60 seconds in increments of 5 seconds. If set to "Instant", the chest will output items every 0.5 seconds.
