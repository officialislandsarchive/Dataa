Cactus Seeds Planted on Sand Cost 3,000 coins Required skill Level 38 Farming Level 36 Farming Description A long term crop that takes 20 hours to grow. Planted on sand. Cactus seeds are seeds used to grow cacti .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Cactus seeds can be purchased from Cletus for 3,000 coins each after reaching level 38 Farming and level 36 Combat .

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a cactus will drop 1 - 3 cactus and 1 cactus seed. Harvesting a cactus cannot reward an extra cactus seed.

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Cactus seeds can be planted on sand to grow into cacti.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of cacti is not affected by water nor basic fertilizer .
