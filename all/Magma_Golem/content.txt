Magma Golem Health 350 HP Behavior Hostile Attack strength 35 HP (Big) 25 HP (Small) Drops Underworld Token Opal Obsidian Jukebox Disk Location Underworld A magma golem is a hostile mob that spawns in the Underworld .

Spawning [ ]

Underworld [ ]

Magma golems spawn throughout the Underworld .

Attacks [ ]

Punch [ ]

Magma Golems will throw a punch to players dealing 35 damage.

Catapult [ ]

Magma Golems, after 2 consecutive punches, will throw the player to a large distance while also dealing 35 damage.

Split [ ]

Once defeated, Magma Golems will split into 2 smaller golems who deal 25 damage instead.

Drops [ ]

A magma golem rewards 25 (99 with the 4x daily bonus) Combat XP and 25 (99 with the 4x daily bonus) Light Melee , Heavy Melee , Magic , or Archery XP depending on the weapon the player used to defeat it.
