Scarecrow Yellow Eyed Green Eyed Type of block Solid block Buy cost 1,500 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent A scarecrow is a furniture block that could have been purchased from Kontos during the Halloween 2022 event. It comes in two different variants: yellow eyed and green eyed.

Scarecrows could have been purchased for 1,500 coins each from Kontos during the Halloween 2022 event.
