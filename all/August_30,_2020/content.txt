August 30, 2020 Basic Information Day released August 30th, 2020 Version number 19 Version Navigator Previous version Next version August 16, 2020 September 6, 2020 The August 30, 2020 update, or more commonly known as the Bees update, is the 19th update in Islands. This update brought various new items and blocks , a new passive mob , and general quality of life updates.

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Bees [ ]

Added the Bee mob Collects pollen from Flowers on your island to produce Honeycomb Creates a Bee Hive if the bees like your island.

Food [ ]

Added Honeycomb Added the ability to craft Honey Toast , Roasted Honey Carrot , Candy Apple , Lemon Puff , and Orange Honeycomb Added the ability to make Roasted Carrot

Blocks & Items [ ]

Added Honey Press to the game Turn Honeycomb into Jars of Honey Added Honeycomb Block and Honey Block

Reset [ ]

Added /reset to reset your island (does not reset inventory).

Modifications [ ]

Inventory UI [ ]

Ability to search New looks Sorted into categories

Totems & Chests [ ]

Added 2 more slots to totems . (Used to be 4) Added additional slots to chests

Animal AI [ ]

Rebuilt the framework allowing them to more easily add new animals

Jars [ ]

Changed the model of grape jam and tomato sauce .
