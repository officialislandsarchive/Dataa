Slime Knight Health 250 HP Behavior Hostile Attack strength 16 Drops Jukebox Disk Location Slime Dungeon A slime knight is a hostile mob found exclusively in the Slime Dungeon .

Spawning [ ]

Slime knights are found in the Slime Dungeon during the " Clear all Slime Knights " quests or can be summoned by the Primordial Slime .

Drops [ ]

A slime knight rewards 8 (33 with the 4x daily bonus) Light Melee, Heavy Melee, Magic, or Archery XP depending on the weapon the player used to defeat it.
