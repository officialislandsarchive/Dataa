Portal Crystal The portal crystal is an unused item in Islands . It cannot be obtained through normal gameplay.

Usage [ ]

Currently the portal crystal has no use. It was most likely used to test some kind of gameplay mechanics for portals around Islands' release.

Trivia [ ]

This is one of the 4 test items, the others being the test totem , the test generator , and the test blueprint . The portal crystal has the same model as coal . [ 1 ]
