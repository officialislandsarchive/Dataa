Evil Gingerbread Health 85 HP Behavior Hostile Attack strength 16 Drops Snowflake Ticket Jukebox Disk Location Hub The evil gingerbread was a hostile mob that spawned in the Hub during the Christmas 2022 event.

Spawning [ ]

Hub [ ]

Evil gingerbreads spawned in the Hub during the Christmas 2022 event.

Behavior [ ]

The evil gingerbread is a small mob who attacks every 1.5 seconds, dealing 16 damage per hit. Their small hitbox limits the suitability in using melee weapons against them.

Drops [ ]

An evil gingerbread rewards 5 Combat XP (18 XP with the 4x daily bonus) and 5 Light Melee , Heavy Melee , Magic , or Archery XP (18 with the 4x daily bonus) depending on the weapon the player used to defeat it.
