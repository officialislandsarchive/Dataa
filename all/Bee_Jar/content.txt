Bee Jar A bee jar is a captured bee in a jar.

Obtaining [ ]

Capturing [ ]

Upon capturing a bee with a net , a bee jar will be put in the player's inventory.

Usage [ ]

Bee jars are used to move bees from one hive to another.

Bees are unable to be released if the small animal limit has been reached. The limit can be raised by leveling up the Animal Care skill.

History [ ]

September 6, 2020 Added bee jars.

Trivia [ ]

Prior to the January 22, 2021 update, it was very rare to be able to release a bee from the jar, possibly because there was no indication as to how many bees are currently on the player's island . Bee jars cannot be used to put bees on display in display cases .
