Forge Hammer Type Tool Recipe 2 Enchanted Diamond 30 Steel Plate 20 Pine Wood Crafted with Workbench Tier 4 A Forge hammer is a tool that builds blocks in a 7x1 grid. It cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

To get its crafting recipe, players need to complete Master islands player achievement .

Name Recipe Crafting station Required skill Forge Hammer 2 Enchanted Diamond 30 Steel Plate 20 Pine Wood Workbench Tier 4 None

Usage [ ]

Building blocks [ ]

The forge hammer can be used to build blocks in a 7x1 grid.
