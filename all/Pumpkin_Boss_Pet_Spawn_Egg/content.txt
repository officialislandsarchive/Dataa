Pumpkin Boss Pet Spawn Egg The Pumpkin Boss pet spawn egg is a spawn egg used to spawn a Pumpkin Boss pet . It cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Cauldron [ ]

Opening a cauldron has a 1% chance of rewarding a Pumpkin Boss pet spawn egg, having a rarity of 'mythic'.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

To spawn a Pumpkin Boss pet , the player must be the owner of the island they are currently on. While holding the egg, clicking or tapping anywhere on their screen will spawn a Pumpkin Boss pet.
