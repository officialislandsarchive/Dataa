Party Horn A party horn is a item that can be obtained during Islands' Third Anniversary .

Obtaining [ ]

Quests [ ]

The player can obtain 20 party horns by completing the "Toot Your own Horn!" quest that was available from April 14, 2023 to April 28, 2023 .

Usage [ ]

Noise-making [ ]

The player, while having a party horn selected in their inventory, can click (on PC) or tap (on mobile) to make a party horn noise.

History [ ]

April 14, 2023 Added party horns. April 28, 2023 Party horns are now unobtainable.
