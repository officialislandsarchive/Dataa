Avocado Heals 10 HP Sell price 36 coins An avocado is a fruit grown on avocado trees .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Avocados grow every season on avocado trees and can be obtained by harvesting the trees. There can be up to three avocados on a tree at a time. When harvested, the player will obtain 1 avocado, 6 Farming XP (24 with the 4x daily bonus), and a 3% chance for an additional avocado tree sapling .

It takes 1 minute and 30 seconds for an avocado to grow and 4 minutes and 30 seconds for the three avocados to grow.

Selling [ ]

Avocados can be sold to Tom for 36 coins .

Trivia [ ]

If an avocado tree is chopped down while having avocados on it, it will only drop the sapling and not any of the avocados.
