Red Mushroom Type of block Non-solid block; Harvestable Spawns Wizard Island Best tool Luminous Transparent A red mushroom is a plant that naturally spawns on Wizard Island .

Obtaining [ ]

Natural generation [ ]

Red mushrooms naturally spawn on Wizard Island . It can be harvested by pressing "F" on PC, but breaking a naturally-generated red mushroom will not drop itself.

Breaking [ ]

Red mushrooms can be instantly removed by harvesting it, but will take slightly longer to remove if mining it. Only mining a red mushroom on a player island will drop it.
