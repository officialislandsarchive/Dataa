Cauldron Type of block Solid block; Interactable Dropped by Zombie Cletus Buy cost 750 Zombie Cletus Tokens Best tool Luminous Transparent The cauldron is an unobtainable roulette block that could have been obtained by defeating Zombie Cletus or purchasing it from Kontos during the Halloween 2022 event.

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Zombie Cletus was guaranteed to drop a cauldron upon defeat.

Purchasing [ ]

Cauldrons could have been purchased for 750 Zombie Cletus tokens each from Kontos during the Halloween 2022 event.

Loot [ ]

Cauldrons can be opened by holding "F" on PC, holding the button on mobile, or holding X on Xbox while hovering over it. Once opened, the cauldron will scroll through the items like a roulette wheel and reward one item. After collecting the rewarded item, the cauldron will act as a storage block with 9 slots able to hold 90 items per slot.

Storage [ ]

A used cauldron has 9 slots that can fit up to 90 items per slot, adding up to a total of 810 items per cauldron.
