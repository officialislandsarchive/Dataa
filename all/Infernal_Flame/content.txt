Infernal Flame Description A single yet rare flame from the infernal dragon. The infernal flame is an item that has a chance to be obtained from infernal dragon eggs .

Obtaining [ ]

Dragon eggs [ ]

Opening an infernal dragon egg has a 9% chance of rewarding an infernal flame, having a rarity of 'rare'.
