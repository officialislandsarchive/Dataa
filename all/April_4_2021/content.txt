April 4, 2021 Basic Information Day released April 4, 2021 Version number 40 Version Navigator Previous version Next version March 19, 2021 April 10, 2021 The April 4, 2021 update, also known as the Wizard Boss update, added the new hostile boss , the Wizard Boss , and revamped Buffalkor Island and Wizard Island . Other additions to the game include frogs , new blocks , and more.

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Rain [ ]

Rain has a chance of occurring; it is more likely to occur during spring . Added frogs that spawn during rain. Added frog jars that can be obtained by catching a frog with a net .

Revamped Buffalkor Island [ ]

Added slate and marble blocks, slabs and stairs . Added slate bricks and marble columns .

Revamped Wizard Island [ ]

Added Wizard Boss . Removed ores spawning.

Other [ ]

Added slab and stair variants to aquamarine blocks and red sandstones . Added spawn blocks that can purchased from Jack . Added industrial lumbermills . Added steam generators . Added sickles . Added watermelons . Cletus has a chance of visiting the Player's Island in the summer to sell watermelon seeds and buy watermelons. Added roses . Fulco can now visit the Player's Island. Fulco sells fertile red or blue roses during the summer, and has a small chance of appearing during the winter to sell fertile white roses. Added palm trees . Players now have a 3% chance of harvesting a palm tree saplings along with a coconut when harvesting a coconut from a palm tree on their island. Players may start with a palm tree as their starter fruit tree .

Modifications [ ]

Bug Fixes [ ]

Fixed xor gates Fixed bug with pricing of hot items sold to the Baker . Stone , coal , iron and gold rocks no longer spawn on Wizard Island.

Other [ ]

Wheat can now be placed in metal troughs . Players can ask villagers to leave their island. Aligned bedrock to the Portal . Adjusted wind . Updated background music . Added an anti-cheat system that disables cheaters and exploiters.
