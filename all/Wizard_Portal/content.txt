Wizard Portal Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe 1 Wizard Portal Shard 120 Stone Brick 30 Red Carpet Crafted with Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4 Required skill Level 20 Combat (To use) Best tool Luminous Transparent Description A placeable portal that allows quick-travel to Wizard Island.

The wizard portal is a portal block that allows the player to travel to the Wizard Island directly from their Player Island , rather than going through the portal on the Buffalkor Island .

Usage [ ]

Traveling [ ]

Wizard portals can be used to transport players to Wizard island from a player's island after reaching Combat level 20. If the skill prerequisite is not met, a UI element will pop up, stating that the player does not meet the required skill level.

Trivia [ ]

The inscription across the top of the portal reads "Islands" in the galactic alphabet . Around the portal you can find several potion bottles, several of them look quite similar to formula 86 .
