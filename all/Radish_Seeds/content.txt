Radish Seeds Planted on Farmland Buy cost 1,450 coins Description This bitter radish can only grow in the spring season Radish seeds are seeds used to grow radishes .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Radish seeds can be purchased from Cletus during spring on the player's island , selling one for 1,450 coins . Players can only purchase 8 seeds before Cletus has to restock.

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a radish will give 1 radish seed and 1 - 3 radishes.

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Radish seeds can be planted on farmland to be grown into a radish.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of radishes can be shortened by 33% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler and by 50% when fertilized with basic fertilizer .
