Ruby Skorp Scale Dropped by Ruby Skorp Sell price 1,000 coins Required skill (to sell) Level 36 Combat Description A rare drop from killing Skorps. A ruby skorp scale is a mob drop dropped from ruby skorps .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Ruby skorps are guaranteed to drop 1 ruby skorp scale upon defeat and a 50% chance of dropping 2.

Selling [ ]

Ruby skorp scales can be sold to Ivon for 1,000 coins each after reaching level 36 Combat .

Buying [ ]

Ruby skorp scales can be used to buy a T-Rex fossil from the Desert Nomad for 35 scales.
