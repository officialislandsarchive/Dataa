Usage [ ]

Detecting [ ]

Conveyor sensors sends a short power signal when an item or block passes through it, allowing for other electrical components like LED lights or split conveyors to be powered momentarily but it uses power itself. It could also be used to activate the totem disabler when an autofarm is overflowing.
