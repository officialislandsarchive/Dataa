Usage [ ]

Splitting power [ ]

When a splitter is powered , it splits the power into two outputs. The player can configure the amount of power each output sends through its UI; the available options are 10% and 90%, 20% and 80%, 30% and 70%, 40% and 60%, and vice versa or 50% and 50% by default.

Trivia [ ]

The combiner does the opposite of the splitter, combing two power signals instead of splitting two signals.
