The Iridescent Flower is an item obtainable from the Cauldron (2023)

It's description reads:

"A forever living flower"

It is also an item in the Haunted Mansion Event where you have to mine rocks with a pickaxe to obtain to fight the Cursed Wraith.

It can be obtained by opening a Cauldron (2023) which you get from killing The Cursed Wraith .
