January 22, 2021 Basic Information Day released January 22nd, 2021 Version number 31 Version Navigator Previous version Next version January 4, 2021 January 29, 2021

The January 22 2021 update, more commonly known as the XP + Animals update, featured an economy wipe, added a new animal, and introduced a skill level system.

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Economy wipe [ ]

Removed Items based on a specific limit with an Economy Wipe . Players who accumulated over 100 million coins pre-wipe got rewarded with a golden portal color.

Skill levels [ ]

Added skill levels which are required for certain actions such as crafting, buying and selling items to villagers, fishing luck, etc. Skills are required in various ways related to their title. Farming Woodcutting Mining Economy Combat Light Melee Heavy Melee Archery Magic Cooking Animal Care Fishing Forging

Animal expansion [ ]

Added Sheep . Added animal spawn eggs which can be purchased from Taliyah . Cow Spawn Egg Sheep Spawn Egg Added animal breeding. Added baby animals. Animals now have unique personalities and traits. Players can now sell animals for coins and XP based on the animal's traits, personality, happiness and age. New happiness algorithm for animals. Animals now wag their tails, blink, and can look at the player. Animals are now able to eat grass blocks if no troughs are nearby. Added Shears . Added Cloth . Added Industrial Milker . Added Wool Vacuum .

Other [ ]

Added blocks of Wool that can be colored. Added Scratching Post . Added Spinach crop that can be fed to animals up to fifty times. New sidebar. Disabled "M" camera mode. Added Resistance Potion . Added Potion of Fertility . UI that tells the player when their friends join the game.

Modifications [ ]

Pink slime spawn chance increased to 5%. Island keys are replaced with the combat skill level and are no longer obtainable. Removed the Banker villager. Removed Taliyah's leveling system Removed Coin Bags . Redesigned the animal inspection menu on the Animal Well-being Kit . Improved block break particles. Changed the sapling growth speed to make it harder to afk macro. Increased tree health to make axes and woodcutting stats more useful. Updated the cow 's model and animations. Renamed wheat troughs into wooden troughs .

Fixes [ ]

Fixed all known dupes. Fixed crops instantly growing after rejoining the game.

Previous changes [ ]

January 11, 2021 [ ]

Removed portal to PvP Island . The Cutlass sword can no longer be purchased and is only obtainable through trading with other players.
