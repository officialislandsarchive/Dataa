Must be washed before selling. Edible A tomato is a crop that grows on farmland from tomato seeds and naturally spawns on Buffalkor Island as foliage .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a tomato plant yields 1 - 3 tomatoes, 1 - 2 tomato seeds , and 9 Farming XP (36 XP with the 4x daily bonus). The chance of obtaining more than one tomato per plant is increased by the player's Farming level and for owners of the VIP gamepass . The 5% chance of obtaining an extra tomato seed is increased for owners of the pro gamepass .

Tomatoes also naturally spawn throughout Buffalkor Island as foliage , but harvesting these cannot yield tomato seeds.

Totems [ ]

A tomato totem will collect and replant tomatoes in a 3-block radius, which can then be collected by interacting it. A tomato totem placed next to a conveyor belt will drop the tomatoes onto the conveyor, but doing so will make them oily. This process cannot yield extra tomato seeds.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of tomatoes can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer . If watered and fertilized, the growth time will be shortened by 30%.

Eating [ ]

Eating a tomato restores 10 HP.

Selling [ ]

Tomatoes can be sold to Cletus for 9 coins each after reaching level 3 Farming .
