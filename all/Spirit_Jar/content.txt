----------------------------------------

Spirit jars are the currency choice on Spirit Island , granting the player the ability to trade with Catherine for spirit-themed items. The item cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Catching [ ]

Catching white, blue, pink, and green spirits drop a varying amount of spirit jars when caught.
