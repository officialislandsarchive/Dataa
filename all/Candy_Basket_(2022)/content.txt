Candy Basket (2022) Type of block Solid block Best tool Luminous Transparent The candy basket is a furniture block that has a chance to be obtained from cauldrons .

Obtaining [ ]

Cauldron [ ]

Opening a cauldron has a 7% chance of rewarding 8 candy baskets, having a rarity of 'uncommon'.
