Steam Generator Type of block Solid block; Interactable; Electrical Recipe 1x Gearbox 20x Copper Bolt 150 Electrite Crafted with Electrical Workbench (Level 3) Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Burn coal for power. The steam generator is a block that turns coal into electrical power . It has 60 power output.

Usage [ ]

Power source [ ]

Steam generators make electrical power by burning coal , it can hold up to 10 coal at a time. It is currently the only infinite power source that is not a bug since it can be fueled with a coal totem , when placed next to a conveyor belt .
