Dumpling Recipe 1 Dough 1 Egg 2 Spinach Crafted with Cooking Table Food Processor Heals 10 HP A dumpling is a food item that heals 10 HP when consumed. It can be crafted without any Cooking level requirement.

It currently cannot be sold to Petur .
