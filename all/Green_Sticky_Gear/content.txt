Green Sticky Gear Dropped by Green Slime Slime King Description Valuable advanced crafting component A green sticky gear is a mob drop uncommonly dropped from green slimes and a guaranteed drop from the Slime King .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Green slimes have a 10% chance of dropping a green sticky gear and the Slime King is guaranteed to drop a green sticky gear upon defeat.
