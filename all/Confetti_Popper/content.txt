Confetti Popper Description Pop colorful confetti with this fun party item! A confetti popper is a one-time use item that can be obtained during Islands' Third Anniversary .

Obtaining [ ]

Quests [ ]

The player will receive 20 confetti poppers by completing the "Glitter Gala Extravaganza!" quest that was available from April 14, 2023 to April 28, 2023 .

Usage [ ]

Glitter-making [ ]

The player, while having a confetti popper selected in their inventory, can click (on PC) or tap (on mobile) to to create confetti particles.

History [ ]

April 14, 2023 Added confetti poppers. April 28, 2023 Confetti poppers are made unobtainable.
