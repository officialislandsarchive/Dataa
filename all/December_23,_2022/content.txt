December 23, 2022 Basic Information Day released December 23, 2022 Version number 112 Version Navigator Previous version Next version December 16, 2022 January 6, 2023 The December 23, 2022 update, also known as the Christmas Event 2022 Update II, is a major update in Islands that added Christmas quests , reworked Snowball Fight , and other event-related content.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added jolly daggers . Added new furniture blocks: Added Christmas lanterns . Added candy clusters . Added Christmas street lights . Added Christmas fence lights . Added Frost . Added “A Very Merry Day” quests : Added “Breezy Battles”. Added “Snow For The Gold”.

Modifications [ ]

Removed Slowness . Increased the sell price of watermelon from 80 coins to 85 coins. Decreased the sell price of lemon from 20 coins to 16 coins.

Snowball Fight reworks [ ]

Teams now spawn in caves that have 2 ways to exit Teams cannot enter other team's spawn or throw snowballs into their spawn Added a 5 minute timer to end the match Players without any kills and assists will not receive any rewards.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed a bug causing rewards to double in notifications. Fixed a bug causing players completing a new task to count for 5 instead of 1.
