Attacking [ ]

At level 1 Heavy Melee , the infernal hammer deals a base damage of 48 HP and can be swung every 0.5 seconds. It has the ability to damage multiple mobs in one swing. There's also a small chance of landing a critical hit when using this weapon, meaning the attack will deal an extra 24 HP of damage and its damage can be increased by leveling up the Heavy Melee skill and it is in the striking and inferno classes.

Like other melee weapon , only one player can hit damage in each hostile mob. Multiple players cannot hit damage together.

Trivia [ ]

The infernal hammer is one of the only weapons with a physical and magical classification, with the others being the frost hammer , the frost sword , the trout's fury , the serpent's hook , the reaper's scythe , jolly dagger , and the noxious stinger . If the mob has a weakness or resistance to both of its classification, then the damage multiplier will be either higher or lower than normal. If the mob has a weakness and resistance to both classifications, then neutral damage will apply.
