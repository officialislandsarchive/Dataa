October 22, 2022 Basic Information Day released October 22, 2022 Version number 104 Version Navigator Previous version Next version October 15, 2022 October 28, 2022 The October 22, 2022 update, also known as the Halloween Event Update II, is a minor update in Islands added the spider pet , the spider candle mob, and many quality of life changes.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added spider pets. Added spider pet spawn eggs . Added spider candles . Added scarecrows .

Modifications [ ]

Modified arrows. Arrows can now be purchased from Ivon . Arrows now have a chance to be returned after usage. Arrows that didn't hit an enemy after usage will be returned. Added a hotkey for mounts (C). Added icons next to regular cauldrons to differentiate from opened cauldrons . Coffins now have seats. Ghosts now float if the block beneath them was broken. Reduced the amount of Zombie Cletus tokens given from ember skulls . Zombie Cletus can now summon spider candles as one of his attacks. Other players can no longer break cauldrons that have rewarded trade-locked items displayed. Modified the collisions for butterfly event archways , skull gates , and fire chalices . Increased the sell price of cactus from 30 coins to 35 coins. Decreased the sell price of plums from 50 coins to 46 coins. Improved UI and SFX.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed a bug that caused higher-rarity fish to be unable to fished. Fixed a bug that caused players to access Fhanhorn 's battle arena after jumping into the void. Fixed a bug that allowed players to reach infinite damage with weapons. Fixed a bug that caused wooden mallets and granite hammers to not swing properly. Fixed a bug were mobs wouldn't drop items after being defeated. Fixed quests not advancing after completing "Defeat mobs" tasks. Fixed a bug that caused mounts to break multiple equipping and idle animations for weapons.

October 23, 2022 [ ]

Cauldrons now allow players to collect trade-locked items. Untradeable items can now be stored in storage blocks. Added a red padlock icon next to untradeable blocks that are inside storage blocks. Fixed a bug that caused players to input trade-locked items inside trash cans . Fixed a bug that allowed other players to take out items that are inside of the trash can.
