November 4, 2022 Basic Information Day released November 4, 2022 Version number 106 Version Navigator Previous version Next version October 28, 2022 November 11, 2022 The November 4, 2022 update, also known as the pet egg stand update, is a minor update in Islands that concluded the Halloween 2022 event, added pet egg stands , and other small changes.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added pet egg stands . Added large diamond chests . Added expanded diamond chests . Added DV plushies . Re-added Cletus .

Modifications [ ]

Concluded the Halloween 2022 event. Halloween event items are now tradable . Players will be teleported when the last island owner leaves their island, instead of after their island unloads. Island owners can now toggle off builder/owner icon renders in the permissions menu. Vending machines now retain the purchase amount without resetting back to one. Increased load time for owner icons when loading in. Increased the sell price of pumpkins from 52 coins to 58 coins. Decreased the sell price of cacti from 30 coins to 25 coins. Removed Tom .

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed bug causing visitors to lose items when island owners disconnect.

November 6, 2022 [ ]

Players now instantaneously teleport back to their island if the owner of the island they're on leaves the game or teleports away.
