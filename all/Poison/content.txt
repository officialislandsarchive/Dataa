Poison Causes Poisons and damages mobs over time Source Weapons whose magical class is toxin Type Attack

Poison is an effect that affects hostile mobs when attacked by weapons whose magical class is toxin .

Causes [ ]

The Poison Effect can be obtained by attacking mobs with a weapon whose magical class is toxin such as a noxious stinger , the divine dao , or poison long arrows .

Information [ ]

Poison will deal damage over time, dealing 15% of the attacks initial damage that inflicted it. It does not stack. Enemies that resist toxin will only take a maximum of 1 damage per tick over time.
