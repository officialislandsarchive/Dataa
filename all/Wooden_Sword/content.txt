Attacking [ ]

At level 1 Light Melee , the wooden sword deals a base damage of 10 HP and can be swung every 0.5 seconds. It has the ability to damage multiple mobs in one swing and moderate knockback. There's also a small chance of landing a critical hit when using this weapon, meaning the attack will deal an extra 5 HP of damage. Its damage can be increased by leveling up the Light Melee skill and it is in the slashing class.

Like other melee weapon , only one player can hit damage in each hostile mob. Multiple players cannot hit damage together.

Trivia [ ]

This was the first ever weapon to be released in Islands . This is the first craftable weapon not crafted in the anvil , the second being the stone sword . The wooden sword deals the second-lowest damage per attack, with the lowest being a tie between the lightning scepter and the maple shield . The weapon's current texture was taken from Easy.gg's other game, Bedwars .
