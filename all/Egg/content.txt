Egg Required skill (to sell) Level 12 Animal Care Description Infertile egg laid by chickens , can make a great meal. An egg is an item laid by chickens .

Obtaining [ ]

Chickens [ ]

Adult and baby chickens will lay eggs shortly after being fed with their favorite food or red berries . Chickens will lay their eggs in a nest if it is accessible and hasn't reached its max capacity of 3.

If there is no nest that is able to accommodate an extra egg, the chicken will lay eggs on a random block on the player's island . Each block is only able to hold one egg.

Transforming [ ]

Eggs can be transformed into mayonnaise jars in a churner . It takes 3 minutes and 30 seconds to transform an egg into a mayonnaise jar.

Input Item transformer Output 1 Egg Churner 1 Mayonnaise Jar

Selling [ ]

Egg can be sold to Taliyah for 145 coins each after reaching level 12 Animal Care .
