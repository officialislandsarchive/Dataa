Opal Opal Rock Dropped by Magma Blob Magma Golem Spawns Underworld Description A beautiful and rare rock. An opal is an item obtained by breaking its respective rock or by defeating magma blobs and magma golems . It is used to craft various opal tools .

Obtaining [ ]

Natural generation [ ]

Opal rocks naturally generate throughout the Underworld . Mining an opal rock yields 1 - 2 opals after each stage and 8 Mining XP (15 with the 4x daily bonus), resulting in a total of 2 - 4 opals and 16 Mining XP (30 with the 4x daily bonus) per rock. 1 - 5 underworld tokens will also be rewarded after mining the entire rock.

Mob drop [ ]

Magma blobs and magma golems have a 10% chance of dropping an opal upon defeat.

Dragon eggs [ ]

Opening an infernal dragon egg has a 23% chance of rewarding 10 - 50 opals, having a rarity of 'common'.
