A potato and duck egg scramble is a food item that heals 40 HP and grants +40% movement speed when consumed.

It currently cannot be sold to Petur .

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating a potato and duck egg scramble restores 40 HP and grants +40% movement speed for 90 seconds; 1 minute and 30 seconds.

Its movement speed boost can be stacked with other movement speed boosts.
