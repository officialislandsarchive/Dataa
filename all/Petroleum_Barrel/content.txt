Petroleum Barrel Recipe 6 Petroleum Fuel Crafted with Fuel Barrel Filler Sell price 1,000 coins A petroleum barrel is an item that can be obtained by pumping petroleum into a fuel barrel filler .

Obtaining [ ]

Transforming [ ]

Petroleum barrels can be obtained by pumping petroleum through pipes into a fuel barrel filler . The petroleum barrels are outputted to the back of the industrial machine and onto a conveyor belt .

Input Item transformer Output 6 Petroleum Fuel ➡ Fuel Barrel Filler ➡ 1 Petroleum Barrel

Usage [ ]

Selling [ ]

Petroleum barrels can be sold to Doug for 1,000 coins each.
