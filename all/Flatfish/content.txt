Flatfish The flatfish is an unused item in Islands .

Information [ ]

The flatfish model and name are from Farm Life , a game which SnickTrix used to develop. It is unknown why the flatfish is in Islands . Flatfish cannot be obtained through normal gameplay and can only be spawned in with developer-only commands.
