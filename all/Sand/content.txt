Sand Type of block Solid block Dropped by Angry Crab Buy cost 8 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent Sand is a block that can be obtained by buying it from John or dropped by angry crabs .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Sand can be bought from John for 8 coins each.

Mob drop [ ]

Angry crabs are guaranteed to drop sand upon defeat.

Smelting [ ]

Smelting sand in a campfire , small furnace , or desert furnace will turn it into a glass block . Sand cannot be heated in an industrial smelter .

Input Output 1 Sand 1 Glass Block

Farming [ ]

Cactus seeds can be planted on sand to be grown into cacti.

Palm trees [ ]

Palm trees can grow on sand.
