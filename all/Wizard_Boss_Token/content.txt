Wizard Boss Token Dropped by Wizard Lizard Description A token obtained from the Wizard Island! The Wizard Boss token is an item used to bypass the summon cooldown of the Wizard Boss .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Wizard lizards are guaranteed to drop 5 Wizard Boss tokens upon defeat.

Usage [ ]

Summoning [ ]

At least 500 Wizard Boss tokens can be used to bypass the summon cooldown needed to summon the Wizard Boss .

History [ ]

September 18, 2021 Added Wizard Boss tokens. February 7, 2022 Wizard Boss tokens can now be dropped, traded , and put into vending machines and chests .
