Pirate Ship Type of block Solid block; Seat Buy cost 100 Fishing Tickets Best tool Luminous Transparent Description A pirate's ship!

The pirate ship is a furniture block that has a chance to be obtained from treasure chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Pirate ships could have been purchased from Odlum on Fishing Festival Island for 100 fishing tickets during the 2022 Fishing Festival event.

Treasure chest [ ]

Opening a treasure chest has a 1% chance of rewarding a pirate ship, having a rarity of 'mythic'.

Sitting [ ]

The pirate ship has a seat, which means players, mobs , and villagers are able to sit on the ship.
