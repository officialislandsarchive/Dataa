The July 23, 2022 update, also known as the Fishing Festival 2022 update, is an update in Islands that readded the Fishing Festival , magma golems , new tools , and new food items.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Started the Fishing Festival . Added Fishing Tickets . Added Fishing Festival Trophies . Added Thomas' Lucky Fishing Rod . Added Trout's Fury . Added new quests relating to the Fishing Festival. Added Slime Queen's Scepter . Added Magma Golems . Added Blueberry Dough . Added Blueberry Cookies . Added Glasses of Milk .

Modifications [ ]

Added decor farm animals in Taliyah 's barn in the Hub . Players are now able to sit on the hub statue and benches. Moved Melody , John , and Jack to a different location in the Hub. Cleaned up excess models in the Hub Desert . Reduced the interaction radius with villagers. Increased sell price of grapes from 18 coins to 22 coins. Increased sell price of coconuts from 45 coins to 48 coins. Decreased the sell price of potatoes from 17 coins to 11 coins. Decreased the sell price of radishes from 64 coins to 60 coins.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed the chat box covering the publish island settings . Fixed a bug that prevented players from inspecting their animals. Using item transformers while another menu is open no longer locks the menu on the screen. Fixed the Slime Queen orb to function correctly and corrected its teleport location.
