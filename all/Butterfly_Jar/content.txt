Butterfly Jar White Red Yellow Green Blue Silver A butterfly jar is an item that can be obtained by capturing butterflies with a net or golden net . Capturing a butterfly will give the respective color of the jar.

Obtaining [ ]

Catching [ ]

Butterfly jars can be obtained by capturing butterflies with a net or a golden net . Once captured, the color of the butterfly jar will be according to the color of the butterfly. Players can release butterflies from their jars by using the item. Once released, the butterflies will not despawn.

History [ ]

September 3, 2022 Added white, red, yellow, green, blue, and silver butterfly jars.
