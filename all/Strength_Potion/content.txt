Strength Potion Recipe 1 Gold Skorp Claw 1 Red Mushroom 1 Empty Potion Bottle Crafted with Brewing Stand Effect Strength A strength potion is an item that grants strength when consumed. Drinking the potion will not return the potion bottle .

Usage [ ]

Drinking [ ]

Drinking a strength potion grants the player 90 seconds of Strength . Strength gives the player a 30% damage boost for all melee weapons . Damage dealt by ranged weapons is not affected.
