Gold Ingot Dropped by Bhaa Bandit Magma Blob Gold Rock Mimic A gold ingot is a mineral obtained by smelting gold ore or defeating certain mobs .

Obtaining [ ]

Smelting [ ]

Gold ore can be smelted into gold in a campfire , small furnace , desert furnace , or industrial smelter .

Input Output 1 Gold Ore 1 Gold Ingot

Mob drop [ ]

Upon defeat, Bhaa is guaranteed to drop 15 gold, bandits have a 30% chance of dropping 1 gold, magma blobs have a 60% chance of dropping 1 gold, and gold rock mimics are guaranteed to drop 5 - 8 gold.

Transforming [ ]

Gold ingots and copper ingots can be transformed together to create red bronze ingots in a red bronze refinery .

Input Item transformer Output 30 Copper Ingot 2 Gold Ingot Red Bronze Refinery 1 Red Bronze Ingot

Trivia [ ]

Gold ingots and rubies are the only minerals that can be dropped by a boss .
