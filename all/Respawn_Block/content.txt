Respawn Block Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe 10 Magma Block 5 Crystallized Obsidian 20 Obsidian Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Block that instantly resets a player back to spawn when making contact. The respawn block is a block that resets a player back to the spawn point of a player island when touched.
