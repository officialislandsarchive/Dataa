Pink Slime Crystal Dropped by Slime Queen Draven A pink slime crystal is a rare mob drop from the Slime Queen and a common drop from Draven . It cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .

Description [ ]

The Pink Slime Crystal is an in-game item that you can obtain as a rare drop from defeating the Slime Queen or as a guaranteed drop from Draven. The drop rate from the Slime Queen is 14.29%. You can't trade it, drop it, or place it in vending machines or chests.

This crystal is a crucial crafting ingredient for high-level items like the Slime Queen Orb and the Slime Queen's Scepter. The Orb requires a Level 60 Combat skill and is crafted at a Workbench Tier 4, while the Scepter has no Combat Level requirement and is crafted at an Anvil.

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

The Slime Queen has a 14.29% chance of dropping a pink slime crystal upon defeat.

Draven is guaranteed to drop 1-3 pink slime crystals upon defeat.
