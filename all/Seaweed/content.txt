Seaweed Planted on Pond Planter Edible Seaweed is a crop that grows in pond planters from seaweed seeds . Seaweed cannot be watered nor fertilized.

It currently cannot be sold to Cletus .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a seaweed plant yields 1 - 4 seaweed, 1 seaweed seed , and 27 Farming XP (108 XP with the 4x daily bonus). The chance of obtaining more than one seaweed per plant is increased by the player's Farming level and for owners of the VIP gamepass . Extra seaweed seeds cannot be yielded.
