Kalimba Recipe 10 Iron Ingot 10 Oak Wood Crafted with Instrument Workbench The kalimba is an instrument .

Obtaining [ ]

Melody [ ]

The player will receive a kalimba and a pan flute upon first interacting with Melody in the Hub .

Crafting [ ]

The instrument workbench gamepass is required to craft the kalimba.

Name Recipe Crafting station Gamepass required Kalimba 10 Iron Ingot 10 Oak Wood Instrument Workbench

Usage [ ]

The player can play a song by using the kalimba by clicking, and just like all the other instruments it emits a multicolored effect of music notes when played. Using the kalimba also causes an animation to play on the player.
