History [ ]

May 2, 2020 Added steel rods. May 17, 2020 Steel rods can now be used to craft industrial stonecutters , gilded steel rods , and iron war axes . June 15, 2020 Steel rods can now be used to craft water storage barrels and industrial washing stations . June 26, 2020 Steel rods can now be used to craft food processors . September 18, 2020 Changed the texture for steel rods. Changed the crafting recipe from 25 iron and 10 coal to 25 steel ingots in the anvil . Steel rods can now be molded with 25 steel ingots using a steel press and rod factory mold . Steel rods can now be used to craft steel presses, copper presses , drills , and red bronze refineries . Steel rods can no longer be bulk crafted in an anvil . November 7, 2020 Steel rods can now be used to craft randomizers . December 11, 2020 Steel rods can now be used to craft split conveyors . January 29, 2021 Steel rods can be bulk crafted in an anvil . April 4, 2021 Steel rods can now be used to craft industrial lumbermills . June 4, 2021 Steel rods can now be used to craft industrial polishing stations . August 15, 2021 Steel rods can now be used to craft pipes , pumpjacks , oil refineries , petroleum petrifiers , tree fruit shakers , and mergers . August 21, 2021 Steel rods can now be used to craft filter conveyors and pallet packers . October 9, 2021 Steel rods can now be used to craft conveyor ramp downs . November 27, 2021 Steel rods can now be used to craft sap boilers . May 20, 2022 20 steel ingots are now required to craft steel rods, down from 25.

Trivia [ ]

Before the September 18, 2020 update, the steel rod and gilded steel rod shared the same model before the steel rod was updated to a newer one. The steel rod is the only moldable item that can be crafted outside the steel or copper press .
