Flower Pot V1 V2 V3 V4 Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe 20 Brick 1 Grass Crafted with Pottery Bench Best tool Luminous Transparent A flower pot is a block used for players to display a flower . There are four variants of flower pots that can be crafted in the pottery bench .

Usage [ ]

Displaying [ ]

Flower pots can be used to display one flower each as decoration. Interacting with a flower pot will allow the player to insert any flower inside. It will appear in the soil of the pot.
