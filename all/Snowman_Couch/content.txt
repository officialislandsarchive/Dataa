Snowman Couch Type of block Solid block; Seat Best tool Luminous Transparent The snowman couch is a furniture block that has a chance to be obtained from Christmas present 2021s .

Obtaining [ ]

Christmas presents [ ]

Opening a Christmas present 2021 has a 2% chance of rewarding a snowman couch.
