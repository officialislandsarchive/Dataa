Obby Checkpoint Type of block Non-solid block; Interactable Recipe 10 Oak Wood 1 Blue Flower Crafted with Obby Kit Best tool Luminous Transparent

The Obby Checkpoint was added in the August 16, 2020 update. It is used to add checkpoints to in-game obbies, built on Player Islands .

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

Obby checkpoints can be crafted with 1 blue flower and 10 oak wood after the player buys the obby kit gamepass for 399 Robux

Usage [ ]

Obbies [ ]

An obby checkpoint is used as a checkpoint so a player would not have to start from the obby start when they fall. Once a player touches this and after they fall into the void or restart, they will respawn on this instead of the obby start or the previous checkpoint.
