Frosty: Remainder of a frozen slime. A slime ball is an item dropped by slimes . There are 4 variations dropped by their respective slime type, being the green, blue, pink, and frosty slime balls. Aside from frosty slime balls, they can be sold for coins to Ivon .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Green slimes , blue slimes , and pink slimes are guaranteed to drop 1 of their respective slime ball upon defeat and a 50% chance of dropping 2. The Slime King is guaranteed to drop 20 green slime balls upon defeat, and the Slime Queen is guaranteed to drop 4 - 8 pink slime balls upon defeat.

During the Christmas 2020 event, players could defeat frost slimes for a guaranteed chance of 1 frosty slime ball and a 50% chance of 2.

Scavenger Hunt [ ]

During the Roblox Battles event, collecting 10 slime balls was a part of the quest for obtaining the Roblox Battles trophy offered by the Scavenger .
