The industrial oven is an industrial machine used to cook food . Anything that can be cooked, such as dough and berry dough , can be put inside.

Usage [ ]

The industrial oven is used to cook food items. It has 6 slots, 3 top ones for food items, and 3 bottom ones for fuel. The oven requires a fuel such as coal as power to cook any food items.

The industrial oven takes 10 seconds to cook an item , and rewards cooking xp once cooked.
