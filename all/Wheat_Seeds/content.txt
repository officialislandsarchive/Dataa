Wheat Seeds Planted on Farmland Buy cost 125 coins Wheat seeds are seeds used to grow wheat .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Wheat seeds can be bought from Cletus for 125 coins each.

Harvesting wheat [ ]

Harvesting wheat gives 1 - 3 wheat, 1 wheat seed, and a 5% chance of getting an additional seed. This chance is increased to owners of the pro gamepass .

Growth [ ]

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Wheat seeds can be planted on farmland to produce wheat.
