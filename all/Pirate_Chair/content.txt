Pirate Chair Type of block Solid block; Seat Best tool Luminous Transparent Description A pirate's chair! The pirate chair is a furniture block that has a chance to be obtained from treasure chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Treasure chest [ ]

Opening a treasure chest has a 1% chance of rewarding a pirate chair, having a rarity of 'mythic'.
