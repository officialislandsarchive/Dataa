Usage [ ]

Transforming [ ]

Syrup bottlers are used to bottle maple syrup into maple syrup bottles . It has six slots able to hold one maple syrup per slot. It takes about 5 seconds to bottle the syrup. As maple syrup cannot be collected to the player's inventory , the syrup must be directly transported from a sap boiler to the syrup bottler via wood tubes .
