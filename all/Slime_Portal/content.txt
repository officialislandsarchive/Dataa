Slime Portal Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe 1 Slime Portal Shard 80 Stone Crafted with Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4 Best tool Luminous Transparent Description A placeable portal that allows quick-travel to Slime Island. The slime portal is a portal that allows the player to travel to the Slime Island rather than going through the portal in the Hub .
