The wreath is a decorative event item that was purchasable  from Santa during the 2020 Christmas event . They can still be obtained from Christmas Presents , which spawned on Player Islands during the event.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Wreaths were sold by Santa during the 2020 Christmas Event for 40 Snowflakes.

Christmas Presents [ ]

Wreaths are also obtainable by opening Christmas Presents , and since some players still have unopened ones these can still be obtained.
