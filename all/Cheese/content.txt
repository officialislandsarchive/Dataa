Cheese Recipe 1 Milk Bucket Crafted with Cheesemaker Heals 25 HP Sell price 100 coins Cheese is a food item obtained from transforming a milk bucket .

Obtaining [ ]

Transforming [ ]

Milk buckets can be transformed into cheese in a cheesemaker . It takes a total of 3 minutes and 30 seconds to fully transform milk buckets into cheese.

Input Item transformer Output 1 Milk Bucket ➡ Cheesemaker ➡ 1 Cheese

Usage [ ]

Crafting ingredient [ ]

Image Name Recipe Crafting station Kue Keju 1 Egg 2 Cheese 1 Dough Cooking Table Food Processor

Eating [ ]

Eating cheese restores 25 HP.

Selling [ ]

Cheese can be sold to Taliyah for 100 coins each after reaching level 10 Animal Care .
