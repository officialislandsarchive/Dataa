Magma Blob Health 400 HP Behavior Hostile Attack strength 25 HP (Head bash) 40 HP (Burrow-blast) Drops Gold Ingot Underworld Token Opal Obsidian Magma Block Jukebox Disk Location Underworld A magma blob is a hostile mob that spawns in the Underworld .

Spawning [ ]

Underworld [ ]

Magma blobs spawn in two sizes in the Underworld â€” small and large. Both sizes deal the same amount of damage to the player, although the hitbox of large magma blobs are larger than those of small magma blobs.

Infernal Dragon [ ]

The Infernal Dragon may summon 1 - 7 magma blobs to aid it in battle, with the amount summoned in proportion to the number of players fighting it.

Attacks [ ]

Head bash [ ]

Magma blobs hit players with their heads dealing 25 damage.

Burrow-blast [ ]

Magma blobs can burrow underground and follow the player, leaving behind a fiery trail signaling their location. Once an underground magma blob gets close enough to a player, it will resurface. If the player is close enough, the attack deals 40 damage to the player and knocks the player back a short distance. Magma blobs cannot be damaged when burrowed underground.

Drops [ ]

A magma blob rewards 25 (99 with the 4x daily bonus) Combat XP and 25 (99 with the 4x daily bonus) Light Melee , Heavy Melee , Magic , or Archery XP depending on the weapon the player used to defeat it.

Trivia [ ]

Magma blobs are the fastest hostile mobs in the game in terms of speed. Magma blobs shares the same magical resistances and weaknesses as evil gingerbreads and wizard lizards , and physical weakness as green slimes , blue slimes , pink slimes , and frost slimes . They are the first mobs that can immune to damage when burrowed underground.
