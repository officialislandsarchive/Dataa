Spirit Seeds Planted on Farmland Buy cost 50 Spirit Jars Spirit seeds are seeds used to grow spirit crops .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Spirit seeds can be bought from Catherine for 50 spirit jars each.

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a spirit crop will give 2 - 3 spirit crystals and 1 spirit seed.

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Spirit seeds can be planted on farmland to be grown into spirit crops.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of spirit crops can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer .
