Amethyst Crystal Amethyst Crystal Rock Dropped by Void Hound , Void Serpent , Void Crop Eater Spawns Void Isles Sell price 7 coins An amethyst crystal is a crystal that can be mined from an amethyst rock .

Obtaining [ ]

Mining [ ]

Amethyst rocks naturally generate throughout the Void Isles . Mining an amethyst rock yields 1 - 2 amethyst crystals after each stage and 9 Mining XP (36 with the 4x daily bonus), resulting in a total of 2 - 4 amethyst crystals and 18 Mining XP (72 with the 4x daily bonus) per rock.

Mob drop [ ]

Void hounds have a 1% chance of dropping an amethyst crystal upon defeat.

Serpent eggs [ ]

Opening a serpent egg has a 75% chance of rewarding 15 - 30 amethyst crystals, having a rarity of 'common'.
