Planks [ ]

Putting birch wood inside a sawmill or an industrial sawmill will turn it into 4 birch planks .

Input Item transformer Output 1 Birch Wood ➡ Sawmill ➡ 4 Birch Plank ➡ Industrial Sawmill ➡

Fueling [ ]

Birch wood can be used to fuel campfires , small furnaces , or desert furnaces . The fuel lasts for about 20 seconds.

Selling [ ]

Birch wood can be sold to John for 7 coins .

Trivia [ ]

Before the July 4, 2020 update, birch wood wasn't a placeable block ; it was just an item .
