May 26, 2020 Basic Information Version number 9 Version Navigator Previous version Next version May 17, 2020 May 31, 2020

The May 26th, 2020 Update (Xbox Update) is the eighth major update for Islands. This update introduces Xbox Support, allowing Xbox users to play on the console. This also adds controller support for PC players.
