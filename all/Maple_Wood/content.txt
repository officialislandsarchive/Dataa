Fueling [ ]

Maple wood can be used as fuel for campfires , small furnaces , or desert furnaces . Each maple wood fuels the heating device for 20 seconds.

Selling [ ]

Maple wood can be sold to John for 15 coins each.

Trivia [ ]

Before the July 4, 2020 update, maple wood wasn't a placeable block ; it was just an item .
