Rock Type of block Non-solid block Spawns On top of Grass and Stone Best tool Drops Stone Luminous Transparent

Rocks are naturally generating blocks that spawn on top of grass .

Obtaining [ ]

Natural generation [ ]

Rocks naturally spawn on top of grass at random occasions. Breaking the block will drop stone , and not the block itself.

Trivia [ ]

Breaking these rocks was previously required in a quest , but quests have since been removed. The only time when rocks can spawn on top of stone is when a resource island generates.
