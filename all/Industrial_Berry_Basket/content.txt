Industrial Berry Basket Recipe 1 Berry Basket 1 Conveyor Belt 3 Blue Sticky Gear Crafted with Maple Workbench The industrial berry basket is a block used to automatically farm any types of berries dropped by turkeys .

Obtaining [ ]

After the player completes Emma 's second (final) quest, both the industrial berry basket and the berry basket crafting recipes are unlocked for the player.
