Usage [ ]

Opening [ ]

Players can interact with trap doors to open them. Players need build permission to open both version of trap doors.

History [ ]

December 10, 2021 Added trap doors. January 20, 2023 Added trap door cherry blossoms.

Trivia [ ]

Trap doors were added because of suggestion #35496 on the official Islands suggestions website . If a trap door is placed under water or lava , it will instantly break and not be returned to the player's inventory.
