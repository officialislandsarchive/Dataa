Dragon Fruit Seed cost 5,000 coins Planted on Trellis Sell price 45 coins Required skill Level 41 Farming (to sell) Edible A dragon fruit is a crop that grows on trellises from dragon fruit seeds .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a dragon fruit vine yields 1 - 4 dragon fruits, 1 dragon fruit seed , and 27 Farming XP (108 XP with the 4x daily bonus). The chance of obtaining more than one dragon fruit per plant is increased by the player's Farming level and for owners of the VIP gamepass . Extra dragon fruit seeds cannot be yielded.

Totems [ ]

A dragon fruit totem will collect and replant dragon fruits in a 3-block radius, which can then be collected by interacting it. A dragon fruit totem placed next to a conveyor belt will drop the dragon fruits onto the conveyor. This process cannot yield extra dragon fruit seeds.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of dragon fruits can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer . If watered and fertilized, the growth time will be shortened by 30%.

Eating [ ]

Eating a dragon fruit restores 45 HP.

Selling [ ]

Dragon fruits can be sold to Tom for 45 coins each after reaching level 41 Farming .

Trivia [ ]

Dragon fruit was referred to as "Dragon Fruit Harvested" for a few minutes in-game when the February 27, 2022 update was released. Dragon fruit are also known as "Pitaya" or "Pitahaya" in countries where it's natively found. The vines from a dragon fruit are actually a type of cacti known as Selenicereus costaricensis . It name of this item in-game is "Dragon Fruit Vine".
