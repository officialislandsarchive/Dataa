Rice Seeds Planted on Pond Planter Buy cost 3,300 coins Required skill Level 39 Farming Description Placed in a pond instead of soil. Rice seeds are seeds used to grow rice .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Rice seeds can be bought from Cletus for 3,300 coins each after reaching level 39 Farming .

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting rice yields at least 1 rice seed, with a 2% chance of giving an additional seed. This chance is increased for players who own the Pro Gamepass .

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Rice seeds can be planted in pond planters to be grown into rice.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of rice can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer . If watered and fertilized, the growth time will be shortened by 30%.
