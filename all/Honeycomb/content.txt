Honeycomb Heals 10 HP A honeycomb is an item that can be harvested from bee hives .

Obtaining [ ]

Bee hives [ ]

When a bee hive is full, indicated by dripping honey, the player can collect up to 4 honeycombs from the hive.

Usage [ ]

Crafting ingredient [ ]

Image Name Recipe Crafting station Required skill Honeycomb Block 4 Honeycomb Workbench Tier 1 Workbench Tier 2 Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4 None Orange Honeycomb 8 Honeycomb 8 Orange Cooking Table Food Processor Level 65 Cooking

Honey-making [ ]

Honeycombs can be processed into jars of honey in a honey press . It takes about three and a half minutes to process one honeycomb into one jar of honey.

Input Item transformer Output 1 Honeycomb ➡ Honey Press ➡ 1 Jar of Honey

Eating [ ]

Eating a honeycomb restores 10 HP.

Animals [ ]

Honeycombs can be used to feed animals by placing some in a wooden , metal , or automated trough .
