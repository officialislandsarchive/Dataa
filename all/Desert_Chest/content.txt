Desert Chest Type of block Solid block; Interactable Best tool Luminous Transparent Description A desert themed chest. The desert chest is an unobtainable desert-themed chest that can be used to hold up to 720 items .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Desert chests could have been purchased from Azur for 30 sand dollars each before the June 3, 2022 update.

Breaking [ ]

Usage [ ]

Storage [ ]

A medium chest has 12 slots that can fit up to 60 items per slot, adding up to a total of 720 items per chest.

June 3, 2022 Desert chests can no longer be purchased from Azur .

Trivia [ ]

Despite having the exact same function as medium chests , desert chests have a slightly larger hitbox that prevents blocks from being placed directly in front of or behind it.
