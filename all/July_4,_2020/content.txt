July 4, 2020 Basic Information Day released July 4, 2020 Version number 13 Version Navigator Previous version Next version June 26, 2020 July 11, 2020 The July 4, 2020 update, also known as the Lighting update, was a major update that introduced the ability to have free VIP servers, a day and night cycle, light sources , and many new sounds.

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Lighting [ ]

Torches are now craftable All legacy torches are changed to torches The game now has a day/night cycle

Gamepass [ ]

Introducing the new premium gamepass: Firework Gamepass Be able to craft the Firework Barrel and craft Fireworks

Sounds [ ]

A speaking sound effect will play when a player sends a chat message Mining or chopping blocks/nodes will emit a variety of sounds Sound effects are now louder and much clearer

Particles [ ]

Mining or chopping blocks / rocks will emit particles

Modifications [ ]

VIP servers [ ]

VIP servers are now free for all players

Chat [ ]

Bubble chats are now visible

Lighting [ ]

The lighting system has received an overhaul

Trees [ ]

Wood , Birch Wood , Pine Wood , and Maple Wood are now placeable blocks Trees have an updated model

Textures [ ]

A few blocks like the wood variants have received tweaks to their textures

Bug Fixes [ ]

Tree rotations didn't save Performance improvements
