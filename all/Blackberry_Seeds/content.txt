Blackberry Seeds Planted on Grass Buy cost 1,500 coins Blackberry seeds are seeds used to grow blackberries .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Blackberry seeds can be purchased from Cletus during spring on the player's island , selling one for 1,500 coins . Players can only purchase 1 seed before Tom has to restock.

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a blackberry bush will give 1 - 2 blackberries and a 1% chance of getting a blackberry seed.

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Blackberry seeds can be planted on grass to be grown into a blackberry bush.

Growth [ ]

A blackberry bush grows through three stages after it is planted. Its first growth stage is a small seed. It becomes a bush in its second stage, and produces berries in its third growth stage. Its growth time is not affected by water nor basic fertilizer .
