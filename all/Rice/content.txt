Rice Seed cost 3,300 coins Planted on Pond Planter Sell price 30 coins Edible Rice is a crop that grows in pond planters from rice seeds .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a rice plant yields 1 - 3 rice, 1 - 2 rice seeds , and 27 Farming XP (108 XP with the 4x daily bonus).  The 5% chance of obtaining an extra rice seed is increased for owners of the VIP gamepass or PRO gamepass .

Growth [ ]

The growth time of rice can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer . If watered and fertilized, the growth time will be shortened by 30%.
