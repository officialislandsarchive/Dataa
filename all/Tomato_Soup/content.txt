Tomato Soup Recipe 8 Tomato Sauce 4 Onion 1 Milk Bucket Crafted with Cooking Table Food Processor Effect +20% break speed (Currently cannot be stacked with Bhutan Butter Tea ) Sell price 512 - 768 coins Hot sell price 768 - 1,152 coins Required skill Level 36 Cooking Description Consume for +20% break speed. Tomato soup is a food item that grants +20% breaking speed when consumed.

Since the March 25, 2022 update, the breaking speed boost given by tomato soup cannot be stacked with Bhutan butter tea 's breaking speed boost.

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

Name Recipe Crafting station Required skill Tomato Soup 8 Tomato Sauce 4 Onion 1 Milk Bucket Cooking Table Food Processor Level 36 Cooking

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating tomato soup grants +20% breaking speed for 15 minutes.

Its breaking speed effect currently cannot be stacked with Bhutan butter tea 's breaking speed boost.

Selling [ ]

Tomato soup can be sold to Petur for 512 - 768 coins each, or 768 - 1,152 coins if tomato soup is a hot offer.
