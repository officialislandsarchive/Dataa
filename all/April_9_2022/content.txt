April 9, 2022 Basic Information Day released April 9, 2022 Version number 77 Version Navigator Previous version Next version April 1, 2022 April 16, 2022 The April 9, 2022 update, also known as the second anniversary update, is an update that celebrates the second anniversary of Islands .

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added the Islands Second Anniversary Cake Added Teleporter Pads Added Orb Stands Added Island Orbs Slime Island Orb Island Orb Hub Island Orb Pirate Island Orb Maple Islands Orb Spirit Island Orb Buffalkor Island Orb Diamond Mines Orb Wizard Island Orb Desert Island Orb Underworld Orb

Modifications [ ]

Revamped the Hub Updated the UIs of crafting stations : Added a button to directly upgrade tiered workbenches Added a suggestion tab Added a search bar Added a toggle to display items that are currently craftable with the player's current inventory Added an option to choose the number of items to craft at once Separated the Hub and mob islands into different servers to increase performance

Bug fixes [ ]

Ducks and yaks are now able to eat from wooden , metal , and automated troughs Yaks can now be milked with a bucket for yak milk Timed input/output chests now output items after the current time frame Tooltips are no longer cut off in crafting stations Fixed a bug that made it possible to spawn multiple bosses at the same time Fixed the custom number input in the trading menu being blocked by the tooltip Fixed the bug preventing the Wizard Boss' static attack from disappearing Fixed the bug that caused players to consume two of a food item at a time instead of one

Removed [ ]

Removed the 2 songs from the jukebox that no longer work in Roblox
