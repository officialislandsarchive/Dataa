Usage [ ]

Polishing [ ]

Polishing stations can be used to polish certain blocks into their smooth variant. It has two slots able to hold one unprocessed block each to be polished in its user interface and does not require fuel to run. When polishing a block, an animation will play causing its barrel to spin. It takes 10 seconds to polish one block.
