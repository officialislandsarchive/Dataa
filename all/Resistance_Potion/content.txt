Resistance Potion Required skill Level 58 Cooking Description Reduces incoming damage. The Resistance Potion was an uncraftable item that was meant to be unlocked after reaching Level 58 in the Cooking skill. However, it wasn't a craftable item even after reaching the needed level.

It was removed from the skill reward list in the February 5, 2021 update.

Effect [ ]

From its name and description, a guess could be made that it would supposedly reduce damage from hostile mobs and players on the mob islands and the PvP island when it was available.

As of February 7, 2022 , its effect can only be obtained by eating a fortune cookie .

History [ ]

January 22, 2021 Added resistance potions. February 5, 2021 Removed the resistance potion.
