Fish Tank Shipwreck Recipe 20 Iron Ingot 10 Oak Wood Crafted with Furniture Workbench Required skill Level 25 Fishing Description A decoration item that can be placed inside fish tanks. The fish tank shipwreck is an item that can be placed inside a small or medium fish tank .

Obtaining [ ]

Fishing [ ]

Prior to the May 13, 2022 update, the fish tank shipwreck blueprint had a small chance to be reeled in from fishing with an iron fishing rod . Bait coudl have been used to increase the chances of obtaining the blueprint.

Coral [ ]

Breaking coral had a small chance of rewarding a fish tank shipwreck blueprint.

Crafting [ ]

Name Recipe Crafting station Required skill Fish Tank Shipwreck 20 Iron Ingot 10 Oak Wood Furniture Workbench Level 25 Fishing

Usage [ ]

Decoration [ ]

The fish tank shipwreck can be placed inside a small or medium fish tank as decoration, taking up 1 fish slot.

History [ ]

September 18, 2020 Added fish tank shipwrecks. May 13, 2022 Level 25 Fishing is now required to craft fish tank shipwrecks. June 3, 2022 A fish tank shipwreck blueprint is no longer required to unlock the recipe of fish tank shipwrecks.
