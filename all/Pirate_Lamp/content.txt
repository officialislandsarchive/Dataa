Pirate Lamp Type of block Solid block; Luminous Best tool Luminous Transparent Description A pirate's lamp! The pirate lamp is a luminous furniture block that has a chance to be obtained from treasure chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Treasure chest [ ]

Opening a treasure chest has a 10% chance of rewarding a pirate lamp, having a rarity of 'uncommon'.
