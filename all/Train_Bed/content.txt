Train Bed Type of block Solid block; Seat Best tool Luminous Transparent The train bed is a furniture block that has a chance to be obtained from Christmas present 2021s .

Obtaining [ ]

Christmas presents [ ]

Opening a Christmas present 2021 has a 1% chance of rewarding a train bed.
