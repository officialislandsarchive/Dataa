Potato Potato Oily Seed cost 300 coins Planted on Farmland Sell price 11 coins Required skill Level 9 Farming (to sell) Oily description A crop that is covered in motor oil. Must be washed before selling. Edible A potato is a crop that grows on farmland from potato seeds .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a potato yields 1 - 3 potatoes, 1 - 2 potato seeds , and 12 Farming XP (42 XP with the 4x daily bonus). The chance of obtaining more than one potato per plant is increased by the player's Farming level and for owners of the VIP gamepass . The 5% chance of obtaining an extra potato seed is increased for owners of the pro gamepass .

Totems [ ]

A potato totem will collect and replant potatoes in a 3-block radius, which can then be collected by interacting it. A potato totem placed next to a conveyor belt will drop the potatoes onto the conveyor, but doing so will make them oily. This process cannot yield extra potato seeds.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of potatoes can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler and by 30% when fertilized with basic fertilizer .

Cooking [ ]

Cooking a potato in a campfire , small furnace , desert furnace , or industrial oven will create a baked potato .

Input Output 1 Potato 1 Baked Potato

Sheep [ ]

Holding a potato will attract nearby sheep to the player. They are also the sheep's favorite food and can be used to feed any sheep .

Selling [ ]

Potatoes can be sold to Cletus for 11 coins each after reaching level 9 Farming .
