Christmas Present 2022 Type of block Solid block Spawns Player's Island Buy cost 450 snowflake tickets Best tool Luminous Transparent Description I wonder what's inside! The Christmas present 2022 is a roulette block added during the Christmas 2022 event.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Christmas present 2022s can be purchased from Eddie for 450 snowflake tickets each.

Spawning [ ]

Christmas present 2022s spawn every 20 minutes on a block on the player's island during the Christmas 2022 event. A festive jingle can be heard when this happens. When the player comes near one, a rustling sound can be heard occasionally to make them easier to find and locate.

Christmas present 2022s will only spawn on grass , stone , snow , ice , oak wood , and coal blocks .

Snowball fights [ ]

Winning a snowball fight with at least one kill will reward a Christmas present 2022.
