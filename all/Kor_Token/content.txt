Kor Token Dropped by Buffalkor Description A token obtained from the Buffalkor Island! The Kor token is an item used to bypass the summon cooldown of Kor .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Buffalkors are guaranteed to drop 5 Kor tokens upon defeat.

Usage [ ]

Summoning [ ]

At least 500 Kor tokens can be used to bypass the summon cooldown needed to summon Kor .

History [ ]

September 18, 2021 Added Kor tokens. February 7, 2022 Kor tokens can now be dropped, traded , and put into vending machines and chests .
