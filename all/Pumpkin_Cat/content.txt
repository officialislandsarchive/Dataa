Pumpkin Cat Type of block Solid block; Luminous Best tool Luminous Transparent The pumpkin cat is a furniture block that has a chance to be obtained from cauldrons .

Obtaining [ ]

Cauldron [ ]

Opening a cauldron has a 7% chance of rewarding a pumpkin cat, having a rarity of 'uncommon'.
