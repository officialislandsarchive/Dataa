September 18, 2021 Basic Information Day released September 18, 2021 Version number 58 Version Navigator Previous version Next version September 11, 2021 September 25, 2021 The September 18, 2021 update, also known as the Lava and Boss Pets update, is a major update adding new spawn eggs , tokens , and more.

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Lava Lava Bucket Added new pets Slime King Kor Wizard Boss Bhaa Added new spawn eggs Slime King Pet Spawn Egg Kor Pet Spawn Egg Wizard Boss Pet Spawn Egg Bhaa Pet Spawn Egg Added tokens to bypass the boss summon cooldowns Slime King Token Kor Token Wizard Boss Token Bhaa Token Added Orange, Light Green, Dark Green, Cyan, Purple, Pink, and Black Glass Blocks and Glass Panes Added sound effects to Magma Blobs

Modifications [ ]

Lava in the Underworld and in the player's island now does 10 DPS (1 damage per hit, every 0.1s) and may kill the player. Filling a Bucket next to a lava pool now gives a Lava Bucket instead of a Water Bucket

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed bug with water transparency "Fixed" bug causing projectiles from the Ruby Staff to not disappear, but still do. Fixed an issue with pets not displaying as owned
