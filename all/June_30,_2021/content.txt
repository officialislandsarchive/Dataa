June 30, 2021 June 30 - July 1 July 2 - July 17 Basic Information Day released June 30, 2021 Version number 47 Version Navigator Previous version Next version June 4, 2021 July 9, 2021 The June 30, 2021 update, also known as the Spirit Update, is the 37th major update in Islands . It added various new spirit-themed additions and the Patrick Star Show event two days later.
