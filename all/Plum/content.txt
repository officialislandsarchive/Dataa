Plum Heals 15 HP Sell price 50 coins A plum is a fruit grown on plum trees .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Plums grow every season on plum trees and can be obtained by harvesting them. There can be up to three plums on a tree at a time, and whenever a plum is harvested, there is a 3% chance of getting an additional plum tree sapling . Harvesting a plum yields 6 Farming XP (24 XP with the 4x daily bonus).

It takes 5 minutes for a plum tree to grow a plum, adding up to a total of 16 minutes and 15 seconds to grow three plums.

Plums can be eaten to restore 15 HP.

Selling [ ]

Plums can be sold to Cletus for 50 coins each.
