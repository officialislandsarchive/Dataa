July 16, 2022 Basic Information Day released July 16, 2022 Version number 90 Version Navigator Previous version Next version July 9, 2022 July 23, 2022 The July 16, 2022 update, also known as the spirit furniture update, is an update in Islands that added spirit furniture blocks, overhauled vending machines , mossy blocks , cobblestone , the world map guide , and other quality of life changes.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added spirit lava lamps . Added spirit beds . Added cobblestone blocks . Added mossy cobblestone blocks . Added cobblestone stairs . Added cobblestone slabs . Added mossy cobblestone stairs . Added mossy cobblestone slabs . Added mossy blocks . Added mossy stairs . Added mossy slabs . Added a world map guide containing information on every visited island.

Modifications [ ]

Overhauled vending machines . Revamped UI. Vending machines can now go offline. Vending machines with max or no coins will appear as red. Players can now type in numbers to insert or withdraw certain amounts of items or coins. Trash cans now return items if the player exits the menu without discarding items. Replaced tooltips with proximity prompts for all villagers . Replaced the Maple Isles portal in the Hub with Don , who will transport the player to the Maple Isles instead. Added additional details in the Hub for players with graphics setting greater than 3. Added new dialogue for Quinn and John . Increased sell price of potatoes from 11 coins to 17 coins. Decreased sell price of grapes from 22 coins to 18 coins.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed an issue with players getting stuck on the loading screen. Fixed an issue where players could not play the game if their islands were corrupted. Fixed the level stats menu not closing when opening the inventory . Villager dialogue boxes now close when opening the inventory. Fixed a bug that prevented players from being able to obtain a treasure map from Davey after discarding one in a trash can . Fixed certain treasure spots not rewarding treasure chests when dug up. Fixed the Breed Daisy quest . Fixed an issue with Quinn duplicating in the Hub.
