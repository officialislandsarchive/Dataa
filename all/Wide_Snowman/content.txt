The wide snowman is a decorative Christmas block obtained by purchasing it for 80 Snowflakes from Santa . This was only available during the Christmas 2020 event.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Wide snowmen were purchasable for 80 snowflakes from Santa during the Christmas 2020 event. Snowflakes are Santa's special currency, and were obtained by completing Santa's quests .
