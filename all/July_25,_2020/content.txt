July 25, 2020 Basic Information Day released July 25 2020 Version number 16 Version Navigator Previous version Next version July 17, 2020 August 2, 2020 The July 25, 2020 update or more commonly known as the Secret Weapon update, was a small content update to Islands . This update added a secret weapon to the game, as well as new blocks and items, in addition to new audio in-game.

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Weapons [ ]

Added the Tidal Spellbook Blueprint Unlocks the ability to craft the Tidal Spellbook

Blocks/Items [ ]

Crystallized aquamarine is now obtainable by mining aquamarine rocks . Dried grass , obtained by placing grass in a campfire or small furnace . Gravel , purchasable from John for 12 coins .

Sounds [ ]

Added two new tracks that play during night time.

Modifications [ ]

Building [ ]

Blocks can be placed deeper below the player's island

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed an issue that caused fireballs to not despawn. Fixed an issue with the rock step of the tutorial .

Trivia [ ]

The wingdings text in the update's icon translates to "Sctir Spely,"
