Pineapple Seeds Planted on Farmland Buy cost 2,850 coins Required skill Level 37 Farming Pineapple seeds are seeds used to grow pineapples .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Pineapple seeds can sometimes be purchased from Cletus during spring on the player's island after reaching level 37 Farming , selling one for 2,850 coins in place of radish seeds . Players can only purchase 6 seeds before Cletus has to restock.

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a pineapple crop will give 1 - 3 pineapples and 1 pineapple seed.

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Pineapple seeds can be planted on farmland to be grown into a pineapple.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of pineapples can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer . If watered and fertilized, the growth time will be shortened by 30%.
