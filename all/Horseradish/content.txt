Horseradish Type of block Non-solid block; Harvestable Spawns Buffalkor Island Wizard Island Sell price 2 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent Description A plant found in the wilderness.

A horseradish is a plant that naturally spawns on Buffalkor Island and Wizard Island .

Obtaining [ ]

Natural generation [ ]

Horseradishes naturally spawn on Buffalkor Island and Wizard Island . It can be harvested by pressing "F" on PC, but breaking a naturally-generated horseradish will not drop itself.

Breaking [ ]

Horseradishes can be instantly removed by harvesting it, but will take slightly longer to remove if mining it. Only mining a horseradish on a player island will drop itself.
