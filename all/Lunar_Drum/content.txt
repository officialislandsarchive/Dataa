Lunar Drum Type of block Solid block Buy cost 7,000 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent The lunar drum is a furniture block that can be purchased from Yuzi .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

The lunar drum can be purchased from Yuzi for 7,000 coins .

Red envelope [ ]

Opening a red envelope has a 7.14% chance of rewarding a lunar drum.
