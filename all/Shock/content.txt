Shock Causes Shocks and damages mobs over time Source Weapons whose magical class is storm Type Attack

Shock is an effect that affects hostile mobs when attacked by weapons whose magical class is storm .

Causes [ ]

Shock affects mobs when attacked with the lightning scepter .

Information [ ]

Shock damage pierces through the enemy multiple times and inflicts Shocked, which does 25% of the attacks initial damage over time. It does not stack and does not refresh upon hitting multiple times.
