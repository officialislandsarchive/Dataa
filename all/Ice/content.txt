Ice is a block that could be obtained by breaking ice spikes that spawned in the Hub during the Christmas 2020 and Christmas 2021 . It can also be obtained from Christmas presents .

Obtaining [ ]

Natural generation [ ]

Breaking an ice spike yielded 1 - 2 ice for each stage, resulting in total of 2 - 4 ice per spike. Ice spikes can be found in the Hub during the Christmas 2020 and Christmas 2021 events.

Christmas presents [ ]

Opening a Christmas present 2020 or Christmas present 2021 has a chance of rewarding ice. The exact amount and chance of getting ice is unknown for the former, and there is a 15% chance of receiving 5 - 6 ice from the latter.

Trivia [ ]

This is the second block in-game that has a special effect (less friction/slippery). The only other blocks that produce a special effect are luminous blocks . Although ice spikes are translucent, it is opaque when placed on player islands . Dropped items can slide on ice due to its low friction.
