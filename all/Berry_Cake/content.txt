Berry Cake Heals Unknown The berry cake is an unused food item in Islands .

Information [ ]

Berry cake is an unobtainable food item in Islands . It can be presumed that its crafting recipe would've contained eggs as they were added in the same update. It was likely scrapped due to the fact that there was already a berry themed food item ( Berry Pie ).
