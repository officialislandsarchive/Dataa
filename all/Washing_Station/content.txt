Washing Station Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe 1 Crystallized Iron 100 Maple Wood 30 Iron Ingot Crafted with Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4 Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Clean oily crops. The washing station is a crafting station that can be used to wash oily crops . Water is not actually needed to wash crops. The washing station can clean up to 100 oily crops at a time.

History [ ]

June 15, 2020 Added washing stations. January 8, 2022 Washing stations can now wash oily pineapples . May 13, 2022 Changed the recipe of washing machines to require 1 crystallized iron instead of 3 pearls .

Please Note - You do not need to run pineapples through a washing machine anymore.

Trivia [ ]

This is the fastest way to wash oily crops , but cannot be automated. This can wash oily crops as many as 100 at a time, while the industrial washing station can only wash 3. Even though oily starfruit was removed from the game, its recipe is still in the UI.
