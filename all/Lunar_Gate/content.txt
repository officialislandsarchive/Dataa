Lunar Gate Type of block Solid block Buy cost 100,000 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent The lunar gate is a furniture block that can be purchased from Yuzi .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

The lunar gate can be purchased from Yuzi for 100,000 coins .

Red envelope [ ]

Opening a red envelope has a 7.14% chance of rewarding a lunar gate.
