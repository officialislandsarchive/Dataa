Usage [ ]

Catching mobs [ ]

Nets can be used to catch catchable mobs by clicking (PC) or pressing the "Use" button (mobile). The end of the net must touch the mob to catch it. The player will receive colored firefly jars from catching fireflies, frog jars from frogs, caged rabbits from rabbits, spirit jars from spirits , Pumpkin Boss tokens from pumpkin bats , colored butterfly jars from butterflies and Zombie Cletus tokens from ember skulls .

Trivia [ ]

Fish cannot be caught with nets. There used to be a bug where swinging a net will remove the stun effect if the player is affected by it. Following the release of the June 30, 2021 update, there was a bug where nets were unable to capture mobs in private servers. This was fixed the following day so that nets properly function in private servers.
