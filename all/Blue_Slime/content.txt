Blue Slime Health 100 HP Behavior Hostile Attack strength 5 HP Drops Blue Slime Ball Blue Sticky Gear Ancient Slime String Slime Portal Shard Slime King Token Jukebox Disk Location Slime Island A blue slime a hostile mob and the second-most rarest variant of slimes . Blue slimes spawn on Slime Island along with green slimes and pink slimes .

Spawning [ ]

Blue slimes have a 25% chance of spawning on Slime Island , which is accessible though the portal in the Hub located next to Ivon or through a Slime Portal .

Drops [ ]

A blue slime rewards 3 Combat XP (9 with the 4x daily bonus) and 3 Light Melee , Heavy Melee , Magic , or Archery XP (9 with the 4x daily bonus) depending on the weapon the player used to defeat it.
