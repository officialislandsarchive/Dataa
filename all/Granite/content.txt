Granite Granite Rock Type of block Solid block Spawns Hub Best tool Luminous Transparent Granite is a block that can be mined from a granite rock .

Obtaining [ ]

Mining [ ]

Granite rocks naturally generate in the mining zone in the Hub . Mining a granite rock yields 2 - 4 granite after each stage and 3 Mining XP (6 with the 4x daily bonus), resulting in a total of 4 - 8 granite and 9 Mining XP (18 with the 4x daily bonus) per rock.

Totem [ ]

Granite totems can be purchased from Arius in the Hub for 8,000 coins . A granite totem will spawn granite rocks around it when placed, or if placed next to a conveyor belt , the totem will drop granite directly onto the conveyor.

Transforming [ ]

Granite can be transformed into granite bricks in a stonecutter or an industrial stonecutter or smooth granite in a polishing station or a industrial polishing station .
