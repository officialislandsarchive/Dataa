February 7, 2022 Basic Information Day released February 7, 2022 Version number 70 Version Navigator Previous version Next version January 30, 2022 February 19, 2022 The February 7, 2022 update, also known as the lunar food update, is an update in Islands that introduced new Lunar New Year-themed food items, the Mailman , and implemented various other minor tweaks.

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added the Mailman Added plates Wood Plate Clay Plate Golden Plate Added five new food items: Fortune Cookie Dumpling Sweet Rice Cakes Kue Keju Spring Rolls

Modifications [ ]

Tokens can now be traded Fixed the spelling of Compact Snow Items can be swapped directly from the inventory and hotbar using the number keys Improved item tooltips Added tooltips to the sidebar buttons Teleporting to the player's island from the Hub now displays a notification Improved Petur 's UI Various villager improvements Improved collision boxes in the Hub Fixed a bug where berry bushes displayed the “Harvest” prompt when not harvestable Fixed a bug that caused berry bushes to disappear when harvested with a sickle Fixed a bug that caused frogs to spawn in the ground Fixed an issue that prevented upgrades to the electrical workbench from appearing on mobile Fixed UI issues Various other bug fixes
