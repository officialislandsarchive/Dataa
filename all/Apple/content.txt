Apple Heals 10 HP Sell price 26 coins An apple is a fruit grown on apple trees .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Apples grow every season on apple trees and can be obtained by harvesting them. There can be up to three apples on a tree at a time. When harvested, the player will obtain 1 apple, 6 Farming XP (24 with the 4x daily bonus), and a 3% chance for an additional apple tree sapling .

It takes 5 minutes and 30 seconds for an apple to grow and 16 minutes and 30 seconds for the three apples to grow.

Eating [ ]

Eating an apple restores 10 HP.

Selling [ ]

Apples can be sold to Tom for 20 coins .

Animals [ ]

Apples can be a cow's , sheep's , or pig's favorite food.
