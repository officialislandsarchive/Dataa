Void Grass Void Grass Pile Type of block Solid block Spawns Void Isles Best tool Luminous Transparent A void grass is a block that can be dug from piles of void grass.

Obtaining [ ]

Digging [ ]

Piles of void grass naturally generate throughout the Void Isles . Digging up a pile yields 1 - 2 void grass.

Farming [ ]

Using a plow on void grass will create farmland . Wheat , tomatoes , potatoes , carrots , onions , starfruits , spinach , pumpkins , watermelon , radishes , spirit crops , chili peppers , pineapples , and void parasites can be planted and grown on this block. Plowing the farmland again or placing a block above it will turn the block into grass rather than void grass.

Farmland can be watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler or fertilized with basic fertilizer to turn it into watered/fertilized farmland respectively, allowing crops to grow 20% faster. Watering and fertilizing farmland allows crops to grow 30% faster.
