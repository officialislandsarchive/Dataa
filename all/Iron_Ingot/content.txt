Iron Ingot Recipe 1 Iron Ore Crafted with Campfire Small Furnace Desert Furnace Industrial Smelter Sell price 6 coins Description An ingot of iron, smelted from iron ore . An iron ingot is a widely-used crafting material in many recipes. It is obtained by smelting iron ore in a heating device and is used to craft conveyors , gilded tools , industrial machines , and more.

Obtaining [ ]

Smelting [ ]

Iron ingots can be obtained by smelting iron ore in a campfire , small furnace , desert furnace , or industrial smelter .

Input Output 1 Iron Ore 1 Iron Ingot

Christmas presents [ ]

Iron ingots have a chance to be rewarded from opening Christmas present 2020s .

Gamepasses [ ]

Purchasing the Starter Pack for 199 Robux gives 10 iron ingots among other items on every save created by the player.

Steel-making [ ]

Iron ingots can be turned into steel ingots in a steel mill or blast furnace . Both require coal or petrified petroleum as a fuel source to start processing.

Input Item transformer Output 1 Iron Ingot ➡ Steel Mill ➡ 1 Steel Ingot ➡ Blast Furnace ➡

Selling [ ]

Iron ingots can be sold to Jade for 6 coins each.

History [ ]

April 10, 2020 Added iron. March 12, 2022 Iron can now be used to craft castle shields , horse shields , king's shields , medieval chandeliers , medieval green couches , and plated wall torches . April 9, 2022 Iron can now be used to craft teleporter pads , Hub Island orbs , and orb stands . May 20, 2022 Iron can now be used to craft blast furnaces . June 18, 2022 Iron can now be sold to Jade instead of to John . June 25, 2022 Iron can now be used to craft ruby swords . July 2, 2022 Iron can now be used to craft trash cans . July 30, 2022 Renamed iron to "Iron Ingot". Changed the description from "Smelted from iron ore." to "An ingot of iron, smelted from iron ore." August 13, 2022 Iron ingots can now be used to craft serpent's hooks and serpent's banes . September 10, 2022 Iron ingots can now be used to craft silver butterfly lanterns and green butterfly lanterns . September 17, 2022 Iron ingots can now be used to craft iron shortbows . October 15, 2022 Iron ingots can now be used to craft granite hammers . October 28, 2022 Iron ingots can now be used to craft lounge chairs , table tops , table top chairs , hanging guitars , short table tops , and speaker systems . December 2, 2022 Iron ingots can now be used to craft lockers , lunch tables , lunch benches , school chairs , school desks , desks & chairs , school bells , blackboards , whiteboards , and school water fountains . January 6, 2023 Iron ingots can now be used to craft diner tables , diner booths , counter corners , counter centers , and neon light diner signs .

Trivia [ ]

Iron ingots were originally used to craft steel rods , but has been replaced by steel ingots after the September 18, 2020 update. Iron ingots were originally sold to John instead of Jade . With more than 130, iron ingots are included in the most crafting recipes.
