The cat pet is obtained at The Hooded Figure for 750 Haunted Mansion Event Tickets, or from the 2023 cauldron ( 5% ).  This is part of the 2023 Halloween Event. Like other 2023 Halloween items, this is currently tradable.

The cat appears to have a skeletal interior, glowing a soft mint color.
