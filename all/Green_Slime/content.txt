Green Slime Health 100 HP Behavior Hostile Attack strength 5 HP Drops Green Slime Ball Green Sticky Gear Ancient Slime String Fertile Yellow Daisy Fertile Red Daisy Slime Portal Shard Slime King Token Jukebox Disk Location Slime Island A green slime is a hostile mob and the most common variant of slimes .

Spawning [ ]

Green slimes spawn on Slime Island , which is accessible though the portal in the Hub located next to Ivon or through a Slime Portal .

Drops [ ]

A green slime rewards 3 (9 with the 4x daily bonus) Combat XP and 3 (9 with the 4x daily bonus) Light Melee , Heavy Melee , Magic , or Archery XP depending on the weapon the player used to defeat it.
