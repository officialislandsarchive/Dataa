Trivia [ ]

Tokens could previously not be traded. This was changed in the February 7, 2022 update. The Fishing Festival trophy , trout's fury , wooden fishing rod , iron fishing rod ,  and Thomas' lucky fishing rod could not be traded when the 2022 Fishing Festival event was active. Trade-locked items could not have been put in chests before October 24, 2022.
