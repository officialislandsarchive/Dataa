Leaves Leaves Maple Leaves Leaves are items obtained by harvesting trees with leaf clippers .

Obtaining [ ]

Clipping [ ]

To harvest leaves from trees, a leaf clipper must be used. A tree will yield 2 leaves and can only be harvested once unless replanted. Clipping a maple tree yields maple leaves, while clipping any other tree yield regular leaves.
