Shears Recipe 10 Iron Ingot 10 Oak Wood Crafted with Animal Bench Required skill Level 21 Animal Care Shears are tools used to shear sheep to get wool .

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

Name Recipe Crafting station Required skill Shears 10 Iron Ingot 10 Oak Wood Animal Bench Level 21 Animal Care

Usage [ ]

Shearing [ ]

Sheep can be sheared by holding a shear and using it on a sheep that has its wool fully grown. Shearing a sheep will yield 1 block of white wool .

History [ ]

January 22, 2021 Added shears.

Trivia [ ]

A player may accidentally yield 2 blocks of wool if they use shears too quickly.
