Ruby Gauntlet

The ruby gauntlet is an unused item in Islands .

Information [ ]

The ruby gauntlet was added to the game on August 9, 2020 along with skorps . The item cannot be obtained and does not have a use.

It was suspected to be used for the Roblox Battles event, however it was unused.
