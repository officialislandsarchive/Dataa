Candy Cane Planted on Trellis Sell price 24 coins Edible A candy cane is a crop that grows on a trellis from candy cane seeds .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a candy cane yields 1 - 3 candy canes, 1 candy cane seed , and 27 Farming XP (108 XP with the 4x daily bonus). The chance of obtaining more than one candy cane per plant is increased by the player's Farming level and for owners of the VIP gamepass . Extra candy cane seeds cannot be yielded.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of candy canes are not affected by water nor basic fertilizer .

Eating [ ]

Eating a candy cane restores 45 HP.

Selling [ ]

Candy canes can be sold to Tom for 24 coins each.

Trivia [ ]

Candy canes were the first event crop that could have dropped an extra seed . However, since December 29, 2021 , candy canes could no longer drop extra candy cane seeds . They are the only crop to have the ability to drop seeds changed. Candy canes are the first and only event crop that can be sold to Cletus .
