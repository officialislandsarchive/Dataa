Rabbit White Brown Pink A caged rabbit is an item obtained by catching a rabbit with a net .

Obtaining [ ]

Catching [ ]

A caged rabbit is obtained by catching a rabbit with a net .

Usage [ ]

Releasing [ ]

Caged rabbits can be released by holding the item and holding down the left mouse button on PC or pressing and holding the ‘open’ button on mobile.

History [ ]

June 4, 2021 Added rabbits. November 13, 2021 Fixed large lag spikes when dropping or storing rabbits into chests.
