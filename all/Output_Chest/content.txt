Usage [ ]

Automatic storage [ ]

Input/output chests can hold 9 stacks of 30 items, adding up to a total of 270 items, while input/output chest tier 2's can hold 12 stacks of 150 items, adding up to a total of 1,800 items. The items can be added in either manually or through a conveyor belt at the front. Once the chest reaches its item limit, it will begin to output any inputted items to the conveyor belt at the other side.
