A jam sandwich is a food item that grants +15% skill experience gained when consumed.

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

Name Recipe Crafting station Required skill Jam Sandwich 4 Grape Jam 8 Bread Cooking Table Food Processor Level 55 Cooking

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating a jam sandwich grants +15% skill experience gained for 15 minutes.

Selling [ ]

Jam sandwiches can be sold to Petur for 640 - 960 coins each, or 960 - 1,440 coins if jam sandwiches are a hot offer.
