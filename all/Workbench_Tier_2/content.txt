The workbench tier 2 (formerly advanced workbench ) was the second crafting station added to the game. It contains all of the recipes in the workbench tier 1 and other recipes too.

Upgrading [ ]

The workbench tier 1 can be upgraded into a workbench tier 2 with 50 oak wood , 50 stone , and 50 iron ingots . Upgrading the crafting station will replace the workbench tier 1 with a workbench tier 2.

Upgrading [ ]

The workbench tier 2 can be upgraded into a workbench tier 3 with 400 iron ingots , 50 stone , and 20 maple wood . Upgrading the crafting station will replace the workbench tier 2 with a workbench tier 3.
