Turkey Trophy Type of block Solid block Best tool Luminous Transparent The turkey trophy is a trophy that can be claimed after defeating Fhanhorn . It cannot be dropped.

Obtaining [ ]

Kontos [ ]

The player can interact with Kontos to claim a turkey trophy after defeating Fhanhorn for the first time. The trophy can only be claimed once per player and any subsequent defeats will not yield additional trophies.
