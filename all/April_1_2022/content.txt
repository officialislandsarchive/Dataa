April 1, 2022 Basic Information Day released April 1, 2022 Version number 76 Version Navigator Previous version Next version March 25, 2022 April 9, 2022 The April 1, 2022 update is an update in Islands that introduced many minor quality of life changes.

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added the Spring Build Trophy

Modifications [ ]

Manual machines now work while players are on other islands Changed the UI of all item transformers Increased the number of items that can be put in the following item transformers at a time: Campfire (1 ➡ 10) Cheesemaker (1 ➡ 10) Stonecutter (2 ➡ 50) Honey Press (1 ➡ 10) Sawmill (3 ➡ 50) Composter (3 ➡ 20) Cloth Loom (1 ➡ 10) Churner (1 ➡ 10) Small Furnace (3 ➡ 50) Adjusted the smelting time of various recipes Totems bought from Arius are now identified more clearly The Mailman can no longer spawn on newly created player islands Decreased the spawn rate of opal rocks in the Underworld Decreased the drop rate of rubies Changed drops rates from some mobs Changed the recipe of the king's shield to require 1 iron sword instead of 10 Changed composters to allow tuna , red snapper , trout , and grouper to be transformed into basic fertilizer .

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed update logs being dated as 2021 Fixed the “Back To Island” button being behind the player list Fixed the settings menu being cut off Potions now have an animation when being drunk Added a food category to the inventory Fixed chili pepper seeds' collisions Fixed combat and other animations Fixed wheat tiling issue

April 2, 2022 [ ]

Modifications [ ]

Changed the text on mobile to display "consume" instead of "eat/drink" when consuming food or potions

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed an issue where totems wouldn't stack crops when upgraded Fixed pipes giving fuel when broken Fixed an issue that prevented players from reeling fishing rods Fixed a bug that didn't allow players to write or save text on signs
