Sweet Rice Dessert Recipe 1 Pineapple Slice 1 Rice 1 Yak Butter Crafted with Cooking Table Food Processor Heals 35 HP Effect +30% movement speed Required skill Level 49 Cooking Description Consume for 30% movement speed increase. A sweet rice dessert is a food item that heals 35 HP and grants +30% movement speed when consumed.

It currently cannot be sold to Petur .

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating a sweet rice dessert restores 35 HP and grants +30% movement speed for 8 minutes.

Its movement speed boost can be stacked with other movement speed boosts.
