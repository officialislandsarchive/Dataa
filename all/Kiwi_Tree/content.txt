Kiwi Tree Type of block Solid block Best tool Drops 1 Kiwi Tree Sapling 3 - 4 Oak Wood Luminous Transparent The kiwi tree is a fruit tree naturally found on Pirate Island .

The canopies of kiwi trees never change during seasons .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Kiwi trees are grown from kiwi tree saplings , which can be purchased from Terri for 80 doubloons each on Pirate Island . It takes x minutes for a kiwi tree sapling to grow into a kiwi tree.

Harvesting [ ]

Kiwi tree saplings have a 3% chance of dropping whenever a kiwi is harvested from a kiwi tree on either the player's island or naturally-generated ones on Pirate Island's jungle . It can be planted on grass to be grown into a kiwi tree. It takes 2 minutes for an kiwi tree sapling to grow into a kiwi tree.

Usage [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Kiwis grow every season on kiwi trees and can be obtained by harvesting them from the tree. There can be up to three kiwi on a tree at a time, and whenever a kiwi is harvested, there is a 3% chance of getting an additional kiwi tree sapling. Harvesting a kiwi yields 3 Farming XP (12 XP with the 4x daily bonus). It takes x minutes for a kiwi tree to grow a kiwi, adding up to a total of xx minutes to grow three kiwis.

Kiwi trees can be harvested with leaf clippers to drop 2 leaves .

History [ ]

Trivia [ ]

If a kiwi tree is chopped down while having kiwis on it, it will only drop the sapling and not any of the kiwis.
