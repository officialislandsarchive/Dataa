Christmas Lantern Red and White Green and White Red and Green Buy cost 25 snowflake tickets Description A colorful Christmas lantern to hold A Christmas lantern is a luminous item that emits light when held. It comes in a mix of three color variants: red, white, and green.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

All three Christmas Lantern variants can be purchased from Eddie for 25 snowflake tickets each.

Usage [ ]

Light source [ ]

Equipping a Christmas lantern will emit light that can be used to guide the player. Unequipping the lantern or switching items will make the light disappear.
