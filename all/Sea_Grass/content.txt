Sea Grass Type of block Non-solid block Dropped by Angry Crab Spawns Ponds in the Hub and Slime Island Best tool Luminous Transparent Sea grass is a non-solid block that naturally generates in ponds and can be dropped by defeating angry crabs .

Obtaining [ ]

Natural generation [ ]

Sea grass naturally spawns in the ponds in the Hub and Slime Island .

Mob drop [ ]

Angry crabs are guaranteed to drop 5 sea grass upon defeat.

Fish tank blueprints [ ]
