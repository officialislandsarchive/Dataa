Usage [ ]

Electrical [ ]

A timer will, when powered, toggle on and off outputs every 2 seconds. Could possibly be editable in the future. It is good for Auto- firework launchers , Flashing LED lights , and can turn split conveyor into a randomizer . A timer has one input and one passthrough.

Trivia [ ]

Currently timers cannot be stacked (placing multiple timers next to each other and connecting them).
