Group of Ghosts Type of block Solid block; Luminous Best tool Luminous Transparent Description A small pile of ghosts in a circle Group of ghosts are luminous furniture blocks that can be obtained from cauldrons .

Obtaining [ ]

Cauldron [ ]

Opening a cauldron has a 10% chance of rewarding a group of ghosts, having a rarity of 'common'.
