Berry Pancakes Recipe 1 Sweet Pancakes 6 Raspberry 6 Blueberry Crafted with Cooking Table Food Processor ( blueprint required) Heals 55 HP Sell price 750 coins Description Very tasty pancakes made with the maple island's [sic] famous recipe. Berry pancakes are a food item that heals 55 HP when consumed.

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

The recipe for berry pancakes will automatically be unlocked after starting Emma 's second quest.
