Candy Cane Seeds Planted on Trellis Description Plant in a trellis to grow candy canes. Candy cane seeds are seeds used to grow candy canes .

Obtaining [ ]

Christmas presents [ ]

Opening a Christmas present 2021 has a 2% of rewarding a candy cane seed.

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a candy cane crop will give 1 - 3 candy canes and 1 candy cane seed.

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Candy cane seeds can be planted in trellises to be grown into candy canes.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of candy canes are not affected by water nor basic fertilizer .

History [ ]

December 18, 2021 Added candy cane seeds. December 29, 2021 Harvesting candy canes no longer has a 5% chance of rewarding an extra candy cane seed.

Trivia [ ]

Candy canes were the first event crop that could have dropped an extra seed . However, since December 29, 2021 , candy canes could no longer drop extra candy cane seeds.
