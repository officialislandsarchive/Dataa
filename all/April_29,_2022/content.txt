April 29, 2022 Basic Information Day released April 29, 2022 Version number 80 Version Navigator Previous version Next version April 22, 2022 May 13, 2022 The April 29, 2022 update, also known as the baby buffalkor pet update, is a minor update in Islands that added the baby buffalkor pets along with quality of life changes.

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Baby Buffalkor Pet Spawn Eggs Added Baby Buffalkor Pets

Modifications [ ]

Reduced the sell price of rice from 30 coins to 24 coins Reduced the sell price of carrots from 25 coins to 21 coins Increased the sell price of starfruit from 60 coins to 67 coins Reduced lag in the Hub Added directional signs in the Hub Updated the banner of the Hub Island orb in the teleporter pad GUI

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed a bug that caused maple trees summoned by Fhanhorn to float Fixed a bug that prevented red berry seeds , blackberry seeds , blueberry seeds , and raspberry seeds from being placed into chests Fixed a model issue with Hub Island orb Villagers with less than four quests no longer show four quest slots Fixed a bug where sapling automatic planters were able to place saplings on any block Fixed the animal nametag UI Certain personal orbs are no longer censored in the teleporter pad GUI Orbs no longer have a hitbox when displayed on teleporter pads and orb stands Fixed an issue that caused grass to turn grey when the season changes Ore rocks are no longer able to spawn in walls

May 1, 2022 [ ]

Fixed falling through the ground when teleporting to wild islands. Fixed Fhanhorn's icon in the teleporter pad GUI. Fixed baby buffalkor animations. Hub and wild islands no longer show up in the distance when on the Player's Island . Decreased drop chance of baby buffalkor spawn eggs from 0.33% to 0.02%.
