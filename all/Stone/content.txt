Stone Stone Rock Spawns Player's Island Hub Slime Island Best tool Luminous Transparent Stone is a block that can be mined from wild rocks or stone rocks in the Hub and Slime Island .

Obtaining [ ]

Natural generation [ ]

The player automatically starts with 3,047 blocks of stone on their island .

Mining [ ]

Stone rocks naturally spawn in the Hub and on Slime Island . Mining a stone rock grants 4 - 6 stone and 3 Mining XP (6 with the 4x daily bonus) per stage, resulting in a total of 8 - 12 stone and 6 Mining XP (12 with the 4x daily bonus) per rock.

Additionally, mining a wild rock on the player's island will drop 1 stone.

Mining a fossil rock grants the player ??-?? stone.

Totems [ ]

Stone totems can be purchased from Arius in the Hub for 2,000 coins after reaching level 10 Mining . A stone totem will spawn stone rocks around it when placed, or if placed next to a conveyor belt , the totem will drop stone directly onto the conveyor.

Transforming [ ]

Stone can be transformed into stone bricks in a stonecutter or an industrial stonecutter , or into smooth stone in a polishing station or an industrial polishing station .

Input Item Transformer Output 1 Stone ➡ Stonecutter ➡ 1 Stone Brick ➡ Industrial Stonecutter ➡ 1 Stone ➡ Polishing Station ➡ 1 Smooth Stone ➡ Industrial Polishing Station ➡

Totem upgrading [ ]

Stone can be used to upgrade mineral totems .
