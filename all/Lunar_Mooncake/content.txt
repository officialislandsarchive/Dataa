Lunar Mooncake Dropped by Lunar Rabbit Effect Jump boost A Lunar mooncake is a food item that was dropped by the Lunar Rabbit during the Lunar New Year 2023 event.

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

The Lunar Rabbit is guaranteed to drop 3 - 5 Lunar mooncakes upon defeat.

Envelope 2023 [ ]

Opening either a red envelope 2023 or gold envelope 2023 has a 35% and 30% chance of rewarding a Lunar mooncake respectively.

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating a Lunar mooncake grants a +100% jump boost for 55 seconds.

Trivia [ ]

Mooncakes are a traditional Chinese delicacy.

History [ ]

January 20, 2023 Added Lunar mooncakes.
