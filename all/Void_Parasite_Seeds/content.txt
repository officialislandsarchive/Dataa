Void Parasite Seeds Planted on Farmland Void parasite seeds are seeds used to grow void parasites .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a void parasite plant on player islands will give 1 - 3 void parasites and 1 void seed, while harvesting a naturally-spawning void parasite in the Void Isles has a 3% chance of rewarding a void seed.

Serpent eggs [ ]

Opening a serpent egg has a 2% chance of rewarding void parasite seeds, having a rarity of 'rare'.

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Void parasite seeds can be planted on farmland be grown into void parasites.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of void parasites can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer .
