Wizard Portal Shard Dropped by Wizard Lizard Wizard Boss Description Rare elemental item used to craft portals. The Wizard Portal Shard is an item used to craft the Wizard Portal .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

The wizard portal shard is obtained by killing Wizard Lizards with a drop chance of 0.02%. It can also be dropped by the Wizard Boss with a 0.4% chance.
