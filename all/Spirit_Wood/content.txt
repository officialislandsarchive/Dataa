Spirit Wood Type of block Solid block Sell price 34 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent Spirit wood is a block that can be obtained by cutting down spirit trees .

Obtaining [ ]

Cutting [ ]

Cutting down a spirit tree will drop 3 - 4 spirit wood along with a spirit sapling .

Fueling [ ]

Spirit wood can be used to fuel campfires , small furnaces , or desert furnaces . The fuel lasts for about 20 seconds.

Selling [ ]

Spirit wood can be sold to John for 34 coins each.
