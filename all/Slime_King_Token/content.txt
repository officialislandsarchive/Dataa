Slime King Token Dropped by Green Slime Blue Slime Pink Slime Description A token obtained from the Slime Island! The Slime King token is an item used to bypass the summon cooldown of the Slime King .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Green slimes , blue slimes , and pink slimes are guaranteed to drop Slime King tokens upon defeat, dropping 5, 10, and 25 tokens respectively.

Dungeon Chest [ ]

Opening dungeon chests have a 20% of rewarding ?? - ?? Slime Queen tokens, having a rarity of 'common'.

Usage [ ]

Summoning [ ]

At least 500 Slime King tokens can be used to bypass the summon cooldown needed to summon the Slime King .

History [ ]

September 18, 2021 Added Slime King tokens. February 7, 2022 Slime King tokens can now be dropped, traded , and put into vending machines and chests .
