May 12, 2023 Basic Information Day released May 12, 2023 Version number 122 Version Navigator Previous version Next version April 28, 2023 May 26, 2023 The May 12, 2023 update, also known as the Hardcore update part I, is a major update in Islands that introduced Hardcore mode to solo save slots .

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Hardcore mode . Added void crop eaters . Added new Achievements . Added Master of Hardcore Medal . Added moth glider pet spawn eggs .

Modifications [ ]

Players can now receive a notification if they cannot follow other players. Following other players now uses loading screens Players can now receive a notification if they are banned from other player's islands instead of getting kicked on arrival. Increased the sell price of wheat from 7 coins to 12 coins. Decreased the sell price of watermelons from 80 coins to 75 coins.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed bug causing players unable to see travel merchant shops. Fixed bug causing players unable to breed vultures .

May 16, 2023 [ ]

Bug fixes [ ]

Removed seaweed seeds from experience tree. Removed anniversary quests. Fixed bug causing non-hardcore profiles getting hardcore achievements and items . Fixed damage conversion in hardcore.

Modifications [ ]

Void Serpent [ ]

Decreased knockbacks of the Void Serpent 's attacks. Decreased the amount of void hounds spawned by the Void Serpent. Increased movement speed of underground state of the Void Serpent.

Fhanhorn [ ]

Decreased the hardness of Fhanhorn 's healing tree from 83 to 55. Increased Fhanhorn's healing time from 5 seconds to 7 seconds.

Infernal Dragon [ ]

Decreased the Infernal Dragon 's health from 17,000 HP to 13,000 HP.

Hardcore [ ]

Added fruit tree saplings to travel merchant. Losing coins is now 5%-8% of player's total coins. Crop eaters no longer target totems .

May 23, 2023 [ ]

Decreased the maximum player count from 30 to 20 for private servers and the Player's Island servers. The Hub and other islands remain the same maximum player count at 30.
