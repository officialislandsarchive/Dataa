Cornucopia Bed Type of block Solid block Buy cost 10,000 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent The cornucopia bed is a furniture block that can be purchased from Bailey .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Cornucopia beds can be purchased from Bailey for 10,000 coins each.

Treasure chest [ ]

Opening a Maple Isles treasure chest has a 7.5% chance of rewarding a cornucopia bed, having a rarity of 'legendary'.
