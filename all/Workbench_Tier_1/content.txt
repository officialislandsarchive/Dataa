Upgrading [ ]

The workbench tier 1 can be upgraded into a workbench tier 2 with 50 oak wood , 50 stone , and 50 iron ingots . Upgrading the crafting station will replace the workbench tier 1 with a workbench tier 2.

History [ ]

April 10, 2020 Added the basic workbench. July 17, 2020 The basic workbench will no longer spawn on new player islands , being replaced by the workbench . July 23, 2021 Renamed the basic workbench to "Workbench Tier 1".

Trivia [ ]

It is the only block that is able to be crafted in 4 different crafting stations . It is the only block that can be crafted using iron ore .
