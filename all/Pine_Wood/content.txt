Fueling [ ]

Pine wood can be used as fuel for campfires , small furnaces , and desert furnace . Each log fuels the heating devices for 20 seconds.

Selling [ ]

Pine wood can be sold to John for 4 coins .

Trivia [ ]

Before the July 4, 2020 update, pine wood wasn't a placeable block ; it was just an item .
