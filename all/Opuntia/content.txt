Opuntia Seed cost 4,200 coins Planted on Farmland Sell price 42 coins Edible An opuntia is a crop that grows on farmland from opuntia seeds .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a opuntia yields 1 - 3 opuntia, 1 opuntia seed , and 12 Farming XP (48 XP with the 4x daily bonus).

Growth [ ]

The growth time of opuntias can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler or fertilized with basic fertilizer . If watered and fertilized, the growth time will be shortened by 30%.
