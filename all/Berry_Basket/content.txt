Berry Basket Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe 60 Maple Wood Crafted with Maple Workbench Best tool Luminous Transparent The berry basket is a block used for storing berries .

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

The berry basket's recipe will automatically be unlocked upon completing Emma 's second quest in Maple Isles .

Storage [ ]

A berry basket has 3 slots that can fit up to 4 items per slot, adding up to a total of 12 items per basket. Turkeys will deposit harvested berries from berry bushes into the basket.
