Obtaining [ ]

Cooking [ ]

Baking dough in a campfire , small furnace , desert furnace , or industrial oven will create bread.

Input Output 1 Dough 1 Bread

Eating [ ]

Eating bread restores 20 HP.

Selling [ ]

Bread can be sold to Petur for 20 - 30 coins each, or 30 - 45 coins if bread is a hot offer.

Duck [ ]

Holding a bread will attract nearby ducks to the player. They are also the duck's favorite food and can be used to feed any duck.
