Vine Stem Seeds Planted on Farmland Buy cost 3,800 coins Vine stem seeds are used to grow Vine Stems .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Vine stem seeds can be purchased from Maria for 3,800 coins .

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Vine stem seeds can be planted on farmland to be grown into vine stems.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of vine stems can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer . If watered and fertilized, the growth time will be shortened by 30%.
