Pink Sticky Gear Dropped by Pink Slime Slime King Slime Queen Description Valuable advanced crafting component A pink sticky gear is a crafting ingredient uncommonly dropped by pink slimes , the Slime King , and the Slime Queen .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Pink slimes and the Slime King have a 10% chance of dropping a pink sticky gear upon defeat.

The Slime Queen drops 0 - 1 ( 50% ) pink sticky gears upon defeat.

Draven dropped 3 - 5 pink sticky gears upon defeat.

Treasure chest [ ]

Opening a Slime Island treasure chest has a 1% chance of rewarding a pink sticky gear, having a rarity of 'Mythic'.
