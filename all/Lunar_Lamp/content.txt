Lunar Lamp Type of block Solid block; Luminous Buy cost 3,000 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent The lunar lamp is a luminous furniture block that can be purchased from Yuzi .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

The lunar lamp can be purchased from Yuzi for 3,000 coins .

Red envelope [ ]

Opening a red envelope has a 7.14% chance of rewarding a lunar lamp.
