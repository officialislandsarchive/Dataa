September 3, 2022 , also known as the Butterfly Festival update, is a major update in Islands that added the Butterfly Festival , butterflies , new decor blocks, and more.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added butterflies . Added butterfly jars . Added the Butterfly Festival . Added the Butterfly Festival Island . Added golden nets . Added butterfly tickets . Added butterfly event archways . Added rabbit topiaries . Added frog topiaries . Added butterfly event benches . Added the Butterfly Festival trophy .

Modifications [ ]

Buffed the damage from the Slime Queen's scepter health drain attack from 10 HP to 20 HP. Increased the sell price of plums from 50 coins to 56 coins. Decreased the sell price of kiwis from 24 coins to 20 coins.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed misaligned seating on benches.
