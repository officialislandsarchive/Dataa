Diamond Diamond Rock Spawns When Kor is killed or naturally spawning throughout the Diamond Mines Description Excavated from the Diamond Mines A diamond is an item obtained by breaking its respective rock . It is used to craft various items and blocks .

Obtaining [ ]

Mining [ ]

Diamond rocks naturally generate in the Diamond Mines and Emerald Mines . Mining a diamond rock yields 1 diamond after each stage and 57 Mining XP (225 with the 4x daily bonus or 6 when maxed out), resulting in a total of 2 diamonds and 225 Mining XP (450 with the 4x daily bonus or 12 when maxed out) per rock.

Each diamond rock has a 0.25% - 0.5% chance of spawning as an enchanted diamond rock .

Mob drop [ ]

Four diamond rocks spawn upon killing Kor . Each rock that spawns has a 2% chance of being an enchanted diamond rock.
