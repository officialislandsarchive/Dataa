Dodo Fossil Statue Buy cost 55 Ruby Skorp Scales The dodo fossil statue is one of the fossil statues that can be bought from Azur .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

It can be purchased from Azur in The Azarathian Desert for 55 ruby skorp scales .

Breaking [ ]

Unknown...

Fossil Parts [ ]

The Dodo Bird fossil parts to complete the statue are obtained by breaking special Fossil Rocks found throughout the different islands.
The locations for the different parts are listed when right-clikcing on the fossil statue. (When placed down, not in inventory)
