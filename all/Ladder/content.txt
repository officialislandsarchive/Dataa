These blocks can float, meaning they can be placed without blocks underneath them or even behind them.

Usage [ ]

Climbing [ ]

Players are able to climb up ladders by walking up to it and moving forward to go up and backwards to go down. Players can also  stay still on a ladder by not pressing any movement keys.

Trivia [ ]

The ladder does not have a white box around it to show its hitbox when a cursor hovers over it with a pickaxe or an axe .
