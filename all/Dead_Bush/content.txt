Dead Bush Type of block Solid block Best tool Luminous Transparent A dead bush is a decorative block that naturally spawns around Desert Island .

Obtaining [ ]

Natural generation [ ]

Wild dead bushes spawn naturally around Desert Island . They can be harvested and placed on player islands .

Usage [ ]

Quests [ ]

Azur has a quest that involves breaking 15 dead bushes. When a player has completed this quest, they will be rewarded with 4 sand dollars.

Quest Reward Break 15 wild dead bushes. 4 Sand Dollars

History [ ]

August 9, 2020 Added dead bushes. April 24, 2021 Dead bushes no longer spawn on Desert Island . May 8, 2021 Dead bushes spawn on Desert Island again.

Trivia [ ]

The April 24, 2021 update revamped Desert Island, and in doing so, dead bushes accidently stopped spawning. This was later fixed on May 8, 2021 .
