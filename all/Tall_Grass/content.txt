Tall Grass Type of block Non-solid block Spawns Player's Island Best tool Luminous Transparent Tall grass is a foliage block that randomly spawns on top of unoccupied grass blocks on player islands .

Obtaining [ ]

Natural generation [ ]

There is a random chance for tall grass to spawn on grass blocks on player islands that aren't occupied by other blocks. They can be broken and will drop a single tall grass. The exact spawn chance is unknown.

Tall grass can only be placed on grass for decoration. It can also be used in some crafting recipes.

Trivia [ ]

Seasons change the color of some natural blocks like grass and trees , but not tall grass. Rabbits may eat from a tall grass, similar to cows , sheep , pigs , chickens , turkeys , ducks , yaks , and horses eating from a grass block . Rabbits do not break the non-soild block while eating tall grass.
