Armored Skeleton Health 220 HP Behavior Hostile Attack strength 8 HP Drops Chocolate Bar Orange Candy Lollipop Jukebox Disk Location Witch Island An armored skeleton is a hostile mob that spawned during the Halloween 2020 event.

Spawning [ ]

Witch Island [ ]

Armored skeletons naturally spawned on Witch Island , which could be accessed through the portal in the Hub .

Grand High Witch [ ]

4 armored skeletons spawned during phase 3 and 4 of the Grand High Witch fight.

Trivia [ ]

Before the October 19, 2020 patch , if this mob was hit by the spellbook or tidal spellbook , the armored skeleton will be rag-dolled and would not be able to move.
