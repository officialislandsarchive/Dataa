Multi Medal Display Stand Type of block Solid block; Interactable Best tool Luminous Transparent A multi medal display stand is an unimplemented block in Islands .

Information [ ]

Multi medal display stands were added on February 3, 2023 along with single medal display stands , but were left unused. It was likely used to display multiple medals obtained from maxing out certain achievements .
