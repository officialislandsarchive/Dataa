The August 27, 2022 update is a minor update in Islands that added void stone variants.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added void stone stairs . Added void stone slabs . Added void stone bricks . Added void stone brick stairs . Added void stone brick slabs . Added void stone cobble blocks. Added void stone cobble stairs . Added void stone cobble slabs . Added void stone polished blocks. Added void stone polished stairs . Added void stone polished slabs . Added void stone tiled . Added void stone tiled stairs . Added void stone tiled slab . Added void stone carved blocks. Added void stone carved stairs . Added void stone carved slabs .

Modifications [ ]

Differentiated all seeds in the inventory . Increased the sell price of wheat from 7 coins to 14 coins. Decreased the sell price of onions from 30 coins to 24 coins. Teleporter pads on mob islands no longer replace their respective orb with their respective boss orb.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed void parasite crops not dropping void parasite seeds in the Void Isles . Fixed teleporter pads not displaying the proper name of an orb .
