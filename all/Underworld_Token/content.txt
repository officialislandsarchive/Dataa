Underworld Token Dropped by Magma Blob Magma Golem Description A token obtained from the Underworld! The underworld token is an item used to summon the Infernal Dragon in the Underworld .

Obtaining [ ]

Mining [ ]

Underworld tokens can be obtained by mining opal rocks in the Underworld . 1 - 5 tokens is guaranteed to drop alongside opal when the rock is fully broken.

Mob drop [ ]

Magma blobs and magma golems are guaranteed to drop 5 underworld tokens upon defeat.

Dragon eggs [ ]

Opening an infernal dragon egg has a 23% chance of rewarding 300 - 1,000 underworld tokens, having a rarity of 'common'.

Usage [ ]

Summoning [ ]

At least 750 underworld tokens are required to summon the Infernal Dragon in its spawn area in the Underworld .

History [ ]

September 11, 2021 Added underworld tokens. February 7, 2022 Underworld tokens can now be dropped, traded , and put into vending machines and chests . July 23, 2022 Underworld tokens can now be dropped from defeating magma golems .
