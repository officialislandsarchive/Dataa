November 13, 2021 Basic Information Day released November 13, 2021 Version number 62 Version Navigator Previous version Next version October 18, 2021 November 27, 2021 The November 13, 2021 update, also known as the sushi update, is an update in Islands that added new food and crops .

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added status effects to 16 types of food Added two new crops Rice Seaweed Added Tuna Added the Pond Planter to plant seaweed and rice Added 7 new foods to the cooking table Truffle Pizza Truffle Avocado Toast Sushi Roll Tuna Roll Dragon Roll Philadelphia Roll Tai Nigiri Added the Cutting Table Added cut variants of fish Cut Tuna Cut Eel Cut Red Snapper Cut Salmon Added Quest Log Added quests for Taliyah and Cletus

Modifications [ ]

Improved potion UI's Potion effects now save when switching servers Fixed an issue with wooden swords swinging very slowly Fixed an issue where mobs won't play on-hit sounds Treasure Map issues now fixed Chili pepper's speed effect reduced from 1 minute 30 seconds to 1 minute

Other [ ]

Removed Halloween content. Day/Night cycles can now happen in the Hub and Slime Island

Bug fixes (November 16, 2021) [ ]

Fixed UI issues causing pets to not show up & quest log to show incorrect quests (or not show any quests) Fixed issue where Bhaa would launch you into space Fixed large lag spikes when dropping frogs & rabbits (or storing them in chests ) Fixed issue where hub can turn to night & fixed lighting on the edges of the pirate island
