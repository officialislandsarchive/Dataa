Industrial Sawmill Recipe 8 Steel Rod 3 Blue Sticky Gear Crafted with Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4 Required skill Level 15 Forging Description Converts wood into wood planks. Industrial sawmills are industrial machines that can be used to automatically turn any wood into its respective plank . It can be used for semi-automatic plank farms, when coupled with an industrial lumbermill .

Usage [ ]

When powered by coal (one coal will power the machine for about 50 seconds), it will turn any kind of wood into its respective plank type ( e.g. pine wood into pine plank ), via the interface or by dropping them onto a conveyor input of the machine. It takes 15 seconds to fully transform a block .
