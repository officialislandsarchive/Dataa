Nutcracker Red Green Blue Type of block Solid block Best tool Luminous Transparent The nutcracker is a furniture block that has a chance to be obtained from Christmas present 2021s . It comes in three color variants: red, green, and blue.

Obtaining [ ]

Christmas presents [ ]

Opening a Christmas present 2021 has an 18% chance of rewarding a red, green, or blue nutcracker.
