Fire Chalice Small Tall Type of block Solid block Buy cost Small : 1,250 coins Tall: 1,500 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent A fire chalice is a luminous furniture block that could have been purchased from Kontos during the Halloween 2022 event. It comes in two sizes: small and tall.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Small and tall fire chalices could have been purchased for 1,250 coins and 1,500 coins each respectively from Kontos during the Halloween 2022 event.
