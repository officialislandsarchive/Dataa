Purchasing [ ]

Long crossbow bolts could have been purchased for 1,000 coins each from Kontos during the Halloween 2022 event.

Long crossbow bolts can now be purchased from Ivon for 1,000 coins .

Usage [ ]

Ammunition [ ]

Long crossbow bolts are used as ammunition in crossbows and at least one is required to be in the player's inventory in order for them to fire a crossbow. Shooting a long crossbow bolt deals an extra 25 - 34 HP to mobs .

Long crossbow bolts are a one-time use item provided that a mob is hit, meaning they cannot be retrieved after they have been shot as long as damage has been dealt. Unlike other arrows though, long crossbow bolts have a small chance to return upon defeating a mob.

History [ ]

October 15, 2022 Added long crossbow bolts. November 4, 2022 Long crossbow bolts can now be purchased from Ivon instead of Kontos .

Trivia [ ]

Unlike stone short arrows , the limit of long crossbow bolts is 2,000 instead of 20,000. This is the first and only crossbow bolt in Islands . Long crossbow bolts and stone short arrows are the only arrows that can be crafted without an Archery level requirement.
