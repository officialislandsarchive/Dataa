The obby leaderboard is a block used for obbies , built on Player Islands . It was added in the August 16, 2020 Obby update. It is used to display the top three players who beat an obby the fastest.

Usage [ ]

Obbies [ ]

When a player completes an obby by touching an obby finish the time it took them to get from the obby start to the finish is calculated and if it is less then one of the top three on the leaderboard it is added to the list. Gold is first place, Silver is second, and bronze third. To reset the leaderboard break the obby start, breaking the leaderboard will NOT reset it.

Trivia [ ]

Like all obby blocks, this can be used/placed by any player.
