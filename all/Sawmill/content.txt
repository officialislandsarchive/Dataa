Sawmill Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe 10 Iron Ingot 10 Oak Wood Crafted with Workbench Tier 1 Workbench Tier 2 Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4 Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Converts wood into wooden planks.

The Sawmill is a manual item transformer used to create wood planks . It is powered by coal , and it produces 4 wood planks per piece of oak wood in 15 seconds. When powered by coal , it will run for 60 seconds before needing to be re-fueled.

Trivia [ ]

The industrial sawmill is an automated sawmill, it is faster and more efficient then the sawmill. If there is coal in the sawmill, some coal will appear under its overworld sprite. This item uses the same crafting recipe as the shears .
