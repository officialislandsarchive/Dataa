Fish Festival Trophy 2021 Type of block Solid block Buy cost 450 Tickets Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Rare decoration prize from a fishing festival! The fish festival trophy 2021 is a trophy that was obtainable during the Fishing Festival 2021 event. It can no longer be obtained as the 2021 Fishing Festival event has ended.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

The fish festival trophy 2021 was purchasable from Oscar for 450 tickets during the Fishing Festival 2021 event. Tickets were obtained by competing in fishing contests on Fishing Festival Island .
