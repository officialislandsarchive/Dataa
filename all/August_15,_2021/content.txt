August 15, 2021 Basic Information Day released August 15, 2021 Version number 53 Version Navigator Previous version Next version August 8, 2021 August 21, 2021 The August 15, 2021 update, also known as the Factory update part 2, is a major update adding the workbench tier 4 , various new automated blocks, and other oil -related blocks .

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Workbench Tier 4 Added Oil Deposits Added Oil Added Pumpjack Added Pipes Added Pipe Junctions Added Oil Refineries Added Oil Tanks Added Petroleum Tanks Added Petroleum Petrifiers Added Fuel Barrel Extractors and Fuel Barrel Fillers Added Doug , a villager found in the Hub Added Automated Troughs Added Fruit Tree Shakers Added Crate Packer Tier 2 Added Sapling Automatic Planter Added Tier 2 Conveyors Added Merger

Bug Fixes [ ]

Fully fixed the issue causing Davey to visit the player's island multiple times per day.

August 16, 2021 [ ]

Additions [ ]

Oil Barrels and Petroleum Barrels can be sold to Doug .

Modifications [ ]

Modified Doug's dialogue.
