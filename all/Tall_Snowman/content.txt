The tall snowman is a decorative block that could be purchased from Santa during the Christmas 2020 event.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Tall snowmen were purchasable from Santa for 80 Snowflakes, a special event currency. They were made unobtainable when the 2020 Christmas event ended on January 4, 2021 .
