Birch Tree Type Birch Sapling Cost 500 Coins Growth Duration 90 seconds The birch tree is a tree added in the May 10, 2020 update along with the maple tree .

Obtaining [ ]

Birch trees are grown from birch saplings , which can be purchased from Cletus for 500 coins in the Hub . The approximate growing time for the saplings are 90 seconds from planting. It must be at least 3 unoccupied blocks to be able to grow.

Drops [ ]

When chopped, it drops:

3 - 4 birch wood 1 birch sapling

When harvested by leaf clippers , it drops:

2 leaves
