Marble Marble Rock Spawns Buffalkor Island Best tool Luminous Transparent Marble is a block obtained by breaking marble rocks naturally found on Buffalkor Island .

Obtaining [ ]

Mining [ ]

Marble rocks naturally generate on Buffalkor Island in a pit next to the large crane . Mining a marble rock yields 1 - 3 marble after each stage and 6 Mining XP (24 with the 4x daily bonus), resulting in a total of 2 - 6 marble and 12 Mining XP (48 with the 4x daily bonus) per rock.

Totems [ ]

Marble totems can be purchased from Arius in the Hub for 22,000 coins after reaching level 22 Mining . A marble totem will spawn marble rocks around it when placed, or if placed next to a conveyor belt , the totem will drop marble directly onto the conveyor.

Transforming [ ]

Marble can be transformed into marble bricks in a stonecutter or an industrial stonecutter or smooth marble in a polishing station or an industrial polishing station .
