Truffle Pizza Recipe 1 Pizza 1 Black Truffle Crafted with Cooking Table Food Processor Effect +25% logging drop yield Required skill Level 66 Cooking Description Consume for +25% logging drop yield. A truffle pizza is a food item that grants +25% logging drop yield when consumed.

It currently cannot be sold to Petur .

Eating [ ]

Eating a truffle pizza grants +25% logging drop yield for 15 minutes, giving the chance of obtaining an extra log when a tree is chopped down.
