Opal Sword Hilt Description The hilt for an opal sword , crafted on the anvil . An opal sword hilt is an item used to craft the dragonslayer .

Obtaining [ ]

Dragon eggs [ ]

Opening an infernal dragon egg has a 3.5% chance of rewarding an opal sword hilt, having a rarity of 'mythic'.
