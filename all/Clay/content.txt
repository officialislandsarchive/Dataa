Obtaining [ ]

Mining [ ]

Clay rocks naturally generate underwater in the Hub Lake . Mining a clay rock yields 1 - 2 clay after each stage and 3 Mining XP (9 with the 4x daily bonus), resulting in a total of 2 - 4 clay and 9 Mining XP (18 with the 4x daily bonus) per rock.

Totems [ ]

Clay totems can be purchased from Arius for 10,000 coins each. A clay totem will spawn clay rocks around it when placed, or if placed next to a conveyor belt , the totem will drop clay directly onto the conveyor.
