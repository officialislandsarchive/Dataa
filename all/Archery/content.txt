Archery Class Combat Base Stats +0.2 Bow DMG Unlocks Lowest Unlock LVL 10 Highest Unlock LVL 40 Archery is a skill that boosts bow damage and unlocks crafting recipes of bows. It can be leveled up by defeating mobs with a wooden shortbow , iron shortbow , ancient longbow , golden shortbow , azarathian longbow , or the reaper's crossbow . Every level-up increases bow damage by 0.2 HP.
