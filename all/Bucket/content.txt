Bucket Recipe 3 Iron Ingot Crafted with Animal Bench Workbench Tier 1 Workbench Tier 2 Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4 A bucket is a tool used to carry water , milk , yak milk , or lava .

Usage [ ]

Collecting water and lava [ ]

Players can collect water by clicking on any water source block or body of water , and collect lava by clicking on any lava source block or lava pool in the Underworld , both while holding a bucket. The bucket will turn into a water bucket or lava bucket after collecting water or lava respectively.

Milking [ ]

After feeding a cow or yak , players can milk the animal by holding a bucket or inserting empty buckets into an industrial milker . The bucket will be dispensed as a milk bucket or a yak milk bucket respectively.

History [ ]

Trivia [ ]

Before the September 18, 2021 update, using an empty bucket on a lava pool would fill the bucket with water instead of lava . Despite having a crafting recipe of iron ingots, exclusively, the Bucket cannot be crafted via the Anvil.
