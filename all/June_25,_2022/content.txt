June 25, 2022 Basic Information Day released June 25, 2022 Version number 87 Version Navigator Previous version Next version June 18, 2022 July 2, 2022 The June 25, 2022 update, also known as the ruby sword update, is an update in Islands that added ruby swords , more rock mimic variants, and other quality of life improvements.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Ruby Swords . Added Rock Mimic variants. Added Iron Rock mimics. Added Gold Rock mimics. Added custom sounds to rock mimics. An indicator is now shown to villagers , crafting recipes, and ore rocks during quests . Added an option to reset time to continue the time of day in the Day/Night cycle menu.

Modifications [ ]

Players can now cancel quests after starting them Quests now regularly refresh Improved the Day/Night cycle menu. Personal orbs can now be renamed after being named. Changed the recipe of the industrial merchant teleseller to require 200 red bronze ingots , 10 steel plates , and 1 pink sticky gear instead of 500 red bronze ingots. Reduced tax rate from the industrial merchant teleseller from 30% to 10%. Decreased income time for owners of the pro gamepass from 30 minutes to 20 minutes. Renamed wood to oak wood. Increased sell price of tomatoes from 9 coins to 14 coins. Decreased sell price of wheat from 7 coins to 5 coins.

June 28, 2022 [ ]

Adjusted island portal destinations. Level 40 Magic is now required to craft the spirit spellbook . Rock mimic variants now drop their respective mineral when defeated. Randomized boss spawn locations. Randomized spirit spawn locations. Increased the amount of coins given when completing the "Catching Fireflies" quest. Removed the item cap on cactus seeds . Fixed a bug that caused crate packers to not save current crate selection. Fixed a bug that didn't allow item transformers to function properly. Fixed a bug that caused personal orbs to not be renamed. Limited how many quests can be active at a time.
