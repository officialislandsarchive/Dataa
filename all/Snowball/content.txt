Snowball Recipe 1 Snow Crafted with Snowball Machine Description Perfect for throwing! Snowballs are a projectile item made by inserting snow into a snowball machine .

Obtaining [ ]

Transforming [ ]

Putting one snow into a snowball machine makes 3 snowballs. It requires the snowball machine gamepass to use.

Input Item Transformer Output 1 Snow ➡ ➡ 3 Snowball

Usage [ ]

Throwing [ ]

Snowballs can be thrown at other players or mobs hitting a mob does 6 damage. They do deal some knockback, but only about a tenth of a block.
