Attacking [ ]

At level 1 Heavy Melee , the obsidian greatsword deals a base damage of 24 HP and can be swung every 0.5 seconds. It has the ability to damage multiple mobs in one swing and deals moderate knockback. There's also a small chance of landing a critical hit when using this weapon, meaning the attack will deal an extra 12 HP of damage. Its damage can be increased by leveling up the Heavy Melee skill and it is in the slashing class.
