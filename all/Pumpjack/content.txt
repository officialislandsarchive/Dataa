Usage [ ]

Extracting oil [ ]

Pumpjacks are used to extract oil from oil deposits and is powered by coal or petrified petroleum . Coal/petrified petroleum can be inputted through the conveyor belt at one side of the pumpjack to power it for approximately one minute per item. It takes 5 seconds by default to extract one unit of oil from the deposit, and after being extracted, the oil is then outputted through the pipe at the other side of the machine.
