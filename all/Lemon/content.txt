Lemon Heals 10 HP Sell price 27 coins Lemons are a fruit crop .

Obtaining [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Lemons grow every season on lemon trees and can be obtained by harvesting the trees. There can be up to three lemons on a tree at a time. When harvested, the player will obtain 1 lemon, 6 Farming XP (24 with the 4x daily bonus), and a 3% chance for an additional lemon tree sapling .

It takes 4 minutes and 30 seconds for a lemon to grow and 13 minutes and 30 seconds for the three lemons to grow.

Eating [ ]

Lemons can be eaten to restore 10 HP.

Selling [ ]

Lemons can be sold to Tom for 26 coins each.

Animals [ ]

Lemons can be a cow's , sheep's , or pig's favorite food.
