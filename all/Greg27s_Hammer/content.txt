Greg's Hammer Type Tool Recipe 2 Enchanted Diamond 30 Steel Plate 20 Pine Wood Crafted with Workbench Tier 4 A Greg's hammer is a tool that breaks blocks in a 3x3 grid. It cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

To get its crafting recipe, players need to complete Master Breaker achievement .

Name Recipe Crafting station Required skill Greg's Hammer 2 Enchanted Diamond 30 Steel Plate 20 Pine Wood Workbench Tier 4 None

Usage [ ]

Breaking blocks [ ]

The Greg's hammer can be used to break blocks in a 3x3 grid.

Trivia [ ]
