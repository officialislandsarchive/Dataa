Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Undyed glass blocks can be purchased from John for 60 coins each.

Smelting [ ]

Sand can be smelted into a glass block in a campfire , small furnace , or desert furnace .

Input Output 1 Sand 1 Glass Block

History [ ]

May 17, 2020 Added normal, blue, red, and yellow glass blocks. May 8, 2021 Changed the textures of glass blocks. August 15, 2021 Glass blocks can be used to craft pipes , pipe junctions , petroleum tanks , and oil tanks . September 18, 2021 Added black, orange, light green, dark green, cyan, purple, and pink glass blocks. December 10, 2021 Changed the texture of glass blocks. March 12, 2022 Glass blocks can now be used to craft hourglasses and medieval green vanities . April 9, 2022 Glass blocks can now be used to craft Slime Island orbs , Island orbs , Hub Island orbs , Pirate Island orbs , Maple Isles orbs , Spirit Island orbs , Buffalkor Island orbs , Diamond Mines orbs , Wizard Island orbs , Desert Island orbs , and Underworld orbs . April 10, 2022 5 glass blocks are now required to craft Slime Island orbs, down from 10. April 16, 2022 Glass blocks can now be used to craft black personal orbs , green personal orbs , yellow personal orbs , blue personal orbs , lime personal orbs , purple personal orbs , pink personal orbs , cyan personal orbs , orange personal orbs , red personal orbs , Slime King orbs , Fhanhorn orbs , Kor orbs , Wizard Boss orbs , Bhaa orbs , and Infernal Dragon orbs . July 2, 2022 Glass blocks can now be used to craft Slime Queen orbs . July 9, 2022 Glass blocks can now be used to craft chrome glass blocks . August 13, 2022 Glass blocks can now be used to craft Void Isles orbs and Void Serpent orbs . October 28, 2022 Glass blocks can now be used craft table tops and short table tops . November 4, 2022 Glass blocks can now be used to craft pet egg stands .
