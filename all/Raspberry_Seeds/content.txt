Raspberry Seeds Planted on Grass Buy cost 1,000 coins Raspberry seeds are seeds used to grow raspberries .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Raspberry seeds can be bought from Emma for 1,000 coins each.

Quests [ ]

Emma will reward the player 3 raspberry seeds after completing her second quest.

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Raspberry seeds can be planted on grass to be grown into a raspberry bush.

Growth [ ]

A raspberry bush grows through three stages after it is planted. Its first growth stage is a small seed. It becomes a bush in its second stage, and produces berries in its third growth stage. Its growth time is not affected by water nor basic fertilizer .
