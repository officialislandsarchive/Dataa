August 20, 2022 Basic Information Day released August 20, 2022 Version number 95 Version Navigator Previous version Next version August 13, 2022 August 27, 2022 The August 20, 2022 update, or the Void Isles Update II, is an update in Islands that added serpent eggs , void mattocks , void parasites , and other new content.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added serpent eggs . Added void mattocks . Added void mattock hilts . Added void parasite seeds . Added void parasites . Added void fish . Added Void Serpent pet spawn eggs . Added Void Serpent pets . Added privacy setting in the visitor permissions menu .

Modifications [ ]

Larry now hosts a shop. Plowing and replowing void grass now turns it back into a normal grass block . Changed the quest dialogue UI to use the default dialogue system used elsewhere. Increased the Void Serpent 's damage reduction per compound in the arena. Increased the sell price of rice from 24 coins to 30 coins. Increased the sell price of oranges from 16 coins to 20 coins. Decreased the sell price of dragon fruits from 50 coins to 45 coins. Decreased the sell price of lemons from 20 coins to 15 coins.

Bug fixes [ ]

The serpent's hook's exclusive spinning attack is no longer applied to every tool .

August 21, 2022 [ ]

Modifications [ ]

Fixed migrating data issues. Increased void parasite spawn locations on the Void Isles. Increased the chance of getting void parasite seeds from 0.5% to 3%.
