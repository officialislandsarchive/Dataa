October 1, 2022 Basic Information Day released October 1, 2022 Version number 101 Version Navigator Previous version Next version September 24, 2022 October 8, 2022 The October 1, 2022 update, also known as the weapon remodel update, is a minor update in Islands that added new quests , re-added auctions , and remodeled many weapons .

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added 5 new quests .

Modifications [ ]

Changed the model of the aquamarine sword , obsidian greatsword , obsidian hilt , antler hammer , rageblade , the captain's rapier , spellbook , and the tidal spellbook . Quinn now hosts auctions again. Increased the bee limit on player islands from six to ten. Increased the sell price of pineapples from 92 coins to 96 coins. Decreased the sell price of tomatoes from 9 coins to 4 coins.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed a bug that prevented the number of arrows available to update. Fixed a bug that prevented the bow button on mobile from disappearing. Fixed a few weapon renders in the hotbar . Fixed an issue where quests would not count as completed after completion. Quests now correctly display their complete timer.

October 5, 2022 [ ]

Modifications [ ]

Temporarily disabled trading .
