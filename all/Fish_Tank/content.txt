Medium Fish Tank: A medium fish tank. Fish tanks are blocks that can hold fish and fish tank decorations . There are two sizes, small and medium, and both can hold up to three items despite one being larger in size.

Displaying [ ]

Each fish tank can hold up to three fish or fish tank decorations, but they cannot hold eels . To insert an item , the player must interact with the fish tank and select the item in their inventory .
