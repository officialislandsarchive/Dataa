Kong Badge Obtained when Completing the second quest offered by Bosco Description Complete all quests on Kong's Island. The Kong Badge was obtained by completing all of the quests offered by Bosco during the Godzilla vs. Kong event.

Obtaining [ ]

To obtain the badge, the player must have completed both of Bosco 's quests. The badge was awarded when the second quest was finished along with Kong's axe and the Kong trophy .
