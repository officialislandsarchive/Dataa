Guitar Recipe 10 Iron Ingot 10 Oak Wood Crafted with Instrument Workbench The guitar is an instrument .

Obtaining [ ]

Crafting [ ]

The instrument workbench gamepass is required to craft the guitar.

Name Recipe Crafting station Gamepass required Guitar 10 Iron Ingot 10 Oak Wood Instrument Workbench

Usage [ ]

Owners of the instrument workbench gamepass can play a song by using the guitar; players without the gamepass will be prompted to purchase the gamepass if they attempt to play it.
