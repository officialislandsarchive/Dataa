Page Blue Green Red Dropped by Wizard Lizard Wizard Boss Description Foreign writing covers the page Pages are items dropped by wizard lizards and the Wizard Boss . There are 3 different colors: blue, green, and red. They are used to craft the spellbook .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Wizard lizards have a 0.5% chance of dropping any one of the three pages while the Wizard Boss has a 7.5% chance of dropping one. More than one type of page can be dropped per defeat.
