Shipwreck Podium Type of block Solid block Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Gift from Taliyah at a fishing festival! The shipwreck podium is a block obtained by talking to Taliyah during the Fishing Festival 2021 event. It could only be obtained once by each player.

Obtaining [ ]

Fishing Festival [ ]

To obtain the item, the player must first go to Fishing Festival Island . During the Fishing Festival 2021 , they must talk with Taliyah . Then, the block will be given to the player.
