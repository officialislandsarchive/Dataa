Bhutan Butter Tea Recipe 2 Yak Butter 1 Yak Milk Bucket 1 Water Bucket Crafted with Cooking Table Food Processor Heals 25 HP Effect +20% break speed (Currently cannot be stacked with Tomato Soup ) Required skill Level 49 Cooking Description Consume for +20% break speed. A Bhutan butter tea is a food item that heals 25 HP and grants +20% breaking speed when consumed.

The breaking speed of Bhutan butter tea currently cannot be stacked with tomato soup 's breaking speed.

It currently cannot be sold to Petur .
