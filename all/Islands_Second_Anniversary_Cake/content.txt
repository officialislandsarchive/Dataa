Islands Second Anniversary Cake Type of block Solid block Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Celebrate two years of Islands with a delicious birthday cake! The Islands second anniversary cake is a decorative block given out to players from April 9, 2022 - April 16, 2022 .

Obtaining [ ]

Taliyah [ ]

From April 9, 2022 - April 16, 2022 , Taliyah visited player islands during Islands' 2 year anniversary . Interacting with her on the player's own island will reward them the cake.

History [ ]

April 9, 2022 Added Islands second anniversary cakes. April 16, 2022 Taliyah no longer arrives on player islands to grant an Islands second anniversary cake. April 14, 2023 Islands second anniversary cakes can now be traded , dropped, placed on another player's island , and put into vending machines and chests .

Trivia [ ]

Like the Islands anniversary cake , the Islands second anniversary cake is not edible; it is purely meant for decoration.
