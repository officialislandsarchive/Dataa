Healing Causes Heals nearby players when mobs is attacked with ethereal weapons Source Weapons whose magical class is ethereal Type Attack

Healing is an effect that affects hostile mobs when attacked by weapons whose magical class is ethereal .

Source [ ]

Healing will come into effect when attacking hostile mobs with the Slime Queen's scepters , jolly daggers , cursed hammers , and cursed grimoires

Causes [ ]

Healing will heal nearby players. Green essence surrounds the player while players are being healed.
