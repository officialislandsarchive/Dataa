Avocado Tree Type of block Solid block Best tool Drops 3 - 4 Oak Wood 1 Avocado Tree Sapling Luminous Transparent The avocado tree is a fruit tree added in the June 26, 2020 update.

The canopies of avocado trees change color based on the current in-game season ; dark green in spring , light green in summer , dark red in fall , and white in winter , but its sapling always has light green leaves on its canopy.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Avocado trees are grown from avocado tree saplings , which can be purchased from Cletus for 8,000 coins in the Hub after reaching level 12 in the Woodcutting skill. It takes approximately 2 minutes and 50 seconds for an avocado tree sapling to grow into an avocado tree.

Harvesting [ ]

Avocado tree saplings have a 3% chance of dropping whenever an avocado is harvested from the tree. It can be planted on grass to be grown into an avocado tree.

Usage [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Avocados grow every season on avocado trees and can be obtained by harvesting them from the tree. There can be up to three avocados on a tree at a time, and whenever an avocado is harvested, there is a 3% chance of getting an additional avocado tree sapling. Harvesting an avocado yields 6 Farming XP (24 XP with the 4x daily bonus). It takes 1 minute and 30 seconds for an avocado tree to grow an avocado, adding up to a total of 4 minutes and 30 seconds to grow three avocados.

Avocado trees can be harvested with leaf clippers to drop 2 leaves .

History [ ]

June 26, 2020 Added avocado trees.

Trivia [ ]

If an avocado tree is chopped down while having avocados on it, it will only drop the sapling and not any of the avocados. The avocado tree sapling was the most expensive sapling, costing 8,000 coins , before the October 9, 2021 update introduced the plum tree sapling , costing 14,000 coins.
