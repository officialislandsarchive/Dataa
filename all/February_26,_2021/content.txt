February 26, 2021 Basic Information Day released February 26, 2021 Version number 36 Version Navigator Previous version Next version February 19, 2021 March 5, 2021 The February 26, 2021 update is an update in Islands. It added Chickens, the Fishing Festival, Fishing Revamps and many small other changes.

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Chickens [ ]

Added Chickens Added a Tuxedo Chicken skin for 399 Robux Added Nest . Added Chicken Spawn Egg . Added Eggs .

Food [ ]

Added Mayonnaise Jar . Added Deviled Eggs .

Other [ ]

Added scheduled Fishing Festival event, which was set for 11am PST. Added a countdown board in the Hub . Added a portal to the Fishing Festival Island which replaces the countdown board when it hits 0. Added Iron Fishing Rod . Added Metal Trough . Added Mayonnaise Spinner .

Modifications [ ]

Price changes [ ]

Fish Old Price New Price Red Snapper 10 coins ➡ 20 coins Blue Tang 10 coins ➡ 20 coins Clownfish 15 coins ➡ 25 coins Tilapia 30 coins ➡ 80 coins Grouper 30 coins ➡ 60 coins Eel 140 coins ➡ 140 coins Swordfish 500 coins ➡ 800 coins

Other [ ]

Revamped the Hub , including Taliyah's barn, a fishing shack, and a totem tower. Gave all the villagers names. An Iron Fishing Rod is now required to catch high-tier fish. Players are able to click-and-view locked merchant offers. Enabled the new Roblox bubble chat . Removed the old quest system from all villagers . Furniture blueprints are made unobtainable to obtain through normal gameplay. The Hub Portal text will no longer render through blocks. Fixed a bug that prevented animals to produce products when eating from troughs. Pigs now count as small animals instead of large animals. Removed the Oily Crops villager . Changed the model for Potato Salad . Removed the "Gameplay Paused" UI. Removed "Birthday Sheep" skin from the shop.
