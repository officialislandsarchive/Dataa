Fhanhorn's Flower Dropped by Fhanhorn Fhanhorn's flower is a mob drop that is used to craft Fhanhorn's pancakes .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Fhanhorn is guaranteed to drop 2 Fhanhorn's flowers upon defeat and has a 50% chance of dropping 3.
