Combat Class Combat Base Stats +2 Max Health Unlocks Lowest Unlock LVL 7 Highest Unlock LVL 72 Combat is a skill that can be levelled up by defeating mobs or bosses earning XP from defeating them. Leveling up is required for players to access mob islands other than the Hub , Slime Island , Pirate Island , Spirit Island , and the Maple Isles .

Every level-up in Combat will increase the playerâ€™s maximum health by 2. This goes until 302. Once a player reaches level 100 in combat, they will not earn daily bonuses from defeating a mob.
