Grape Seeds Planted on Trellis Buy cost 3,000 coins Required skill Level 35 Farming Description Placed on a trellis instead of soil Grape seeds are seeds that can be grown into grapes .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Grape seeds can be purchased from Cletus in the Hub for 3,000 coins each after reaching level 35 Farming .

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a grape crop will give 2 - 3 grapes and 1 grape seed, with a 5% chance of getting an additional seed. This chance is increased to owners of the pro gamepass .

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Grape seeds can be planted in trellises to be grown into grapes .

Growth [ ]

The growth time of grapes can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer . If watered and fertilized, the growth time will be shortened by 30%.
