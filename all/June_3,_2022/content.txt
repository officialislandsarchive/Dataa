June 3, 2022 Basic Information Day released June 3, 2022 Version number 84 Version Navigator Previous version Next version May 27, 2022 June 10, 2022 The June 3, 2022 update, also known as the horse update, is a major update that added horses and revamped the entire quest system.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Horses Added Horse Spawn Eggs Added Spirit Jars Added Green Spirit Essences Added Copper Resource Deeds Added Gold Resource Deeds Added white and pink spirits . Added new quests Added Jade

Modifications [ ]

Removed Tom Overhauled quest system. Cletus now buys harvested crops . Changed the chandelier model. The cactus spike no longer requires a blueprint to craft. The cactus spike now requires 100 cactus to craft. Visitor Permissions UI now shows display names. Copper ore rocks now spawn in Buffalkor Island and in the Diamond Mines . Increased the sell price of kiwis from 24 coins to 32 coins. Increased the sell price of pineapples from 85 coins to 100 coins. Decreased the sell price of avocadoes from 36 coins to 32 coins. Removed quests from Fulco , Azur , Catherine , Terri , Odlum , Ruby , and Evans . Azur no longer hosts a shop. Fertile red daffodils and fertile yellow daffodils can now be bought for 5,000 coins. Removed orange and red spirits. Changed the color of blue spirits. Resource islands no longer naturally generate.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed banned user list not scrolling properly and being cut off. Fixed lemon puff's model falling apart when dropped.

Other [ ]

An arfing sound is now played when a player types "arf" in the chat .

June 4, 2022 [ ]

Fixed audio issues. Corrected the spirit jar's model. Fixed a bug causing spirit jars to break the inventory . Green spirit essences no longer drop from catching spirits.
