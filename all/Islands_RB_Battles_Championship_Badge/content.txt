Islands RB Battles Championship Badge Obtained when Completing the Roblox Battles event Description Earned by beating the RB Battles event in Islands! The Islands RB Battles Championship Badge was obtained by completing the Roblox Battles event. The player would also receive the Roblox Battles Trophy with the badge when completing the event.
