Plates are decorative blocks used for the player to display any type of food . There are three variants: wood , clay , and golden .

Usage [ ]

Displaying [ ]

Players can display any type of food or potion on plates. Each plate can hold up to one item .

Trivia [ ]

Golden plates have a shiny effect on them that periodically plays. There is a bug that causes lollipops , honey toast and lemon puffs to appear invisible on plates. The blueberries on blueberry cookies will not show when displayed on plates. The liquid in the regen potion disappears when displayed on plates.
