Frost Causes The affected mob will move slower. Source Frost Sword Frost Hammer Candy Cane Scepter Type Weapon-inflicted

Frost is an effect that causes the mob attacked by frost swords , frost hammers , and candy cane scepters to take extra damage occasionally.

Causes [ ]

Frost is inflicted by all weapons of the frost magic class, such as frost swords, frost hammers, and candy cane scepters.

Information [ ]

Frost is applied by frost weapons which when inflicts will cause you to occasionally deal extra damage, dealing 50% of the attacks initial damage once. Despite claiming to inflict slowness, it does not.
