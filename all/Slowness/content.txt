Slowness Causes Slows damaged enemies Source Weapons whose magical class is quake Type Attack

Slowness was an effect that affects hostile mobs when attacked by weapons whose magical class was quake until December 23, 2022 .

Source [ ]

Slowness will come into effect when attacking mobs with weapons that has the quake magical class such staff of godzilla and the ruby staff .

Causes [ ]

Slowness adds a slowness effect that slows the attacked mob by 50%. The enemy is also surrounded by dust when with this effect.

History [ ]

October 8, 2022 Added Slowness. December 23, 2022 Removed Slowness.
