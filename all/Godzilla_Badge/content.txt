Godzilla Badge Obtained when Completing the second quest offered by Holt Description Complete all quests on Godzilla's Island. The Godzilla Badge was obtained by completing all of the quests offered by Holt during the Godzilla vs. Kong event.

Obtaining [ ]

To obtain the badge, the player must have completed both of Holt 's quests . The badge was awarded when the second quest was finished along with the staff of Godzilla and the Godzilla trophy .
