Mayonnaise Jar Recipe 1 Egg Crafted with Churner Sell price 240 coins Required skill Level 18 Animal Care (to sell) The mayonnaise jar is an item made by spinning eggs .

Obtaining [ ]

Transforming [ ]

Eggs can be transformed into mayonnaise jars in a churner . It takes a total of 3 minutes and 25 seconds to fully transform an egg into a mayonnaise jar.

Input Item transformer Output 1 Egg ➡ Churner ➡ 1 Mayonnaise Jar

Selling [ ]

Mayonnaise jars can be sold to Taliyah for 240 coins each after reaching level 18 Animal Care .

Trivia [ ]

The jar holding the mayonnaise does not need to be crafted. This trait is shared with other jars , such as bee jars , firefly jars , and frog jars .
