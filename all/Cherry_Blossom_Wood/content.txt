Cherry Blossom Wood Type of block Solid block Sell price 40 coins Best tool Luminous Transparent Cherry blossom wood is a block that can be obtained by cutting down cherry blossom trees .

Obtaining [ ]

Cutting [ ]

Cutting down a cherry blossom trees will drop 3 - 4 cherry blossom wood along with a cherry blossom sapling .

Fueling [ ]

Cherry blossom wood can be used to fuel campfires , small furnaces , or desert furnaces . The fuel lasts for about 20 seconds.

Selling [ ]

Cherry blossom wood can be sold to John for 40 coins each.
