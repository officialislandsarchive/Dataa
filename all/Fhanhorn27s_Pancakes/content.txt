Fhanhorn's Pancakes Recipe 1 Berry Pancakes 3 Fhanhorn's Flower Crafted with Cooking Table Food Processor Effect Might of Fhanhorn Required skill Level 75 Cooking Description Consume for Might of Fhanhorn, granting small chance [sic] of finding antler weapons in combat. Fhanhorn's pancakes are a food item that grants the Might of Fhanhorn when consumed.

Usage [ ]

Eating [ ]

Eating Fhanhorn's pancakes grants the Might of Fhanhorn for 15 minutes, which grants a 0.3% chance for any mob to drop an antler shield or antler hammer when defeated.
