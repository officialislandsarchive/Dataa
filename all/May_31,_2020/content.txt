May 31, 2020 Basic Information Day released May 31st, 2020 Version number 10 Version Navigator Previous version Next version May 26, 2020 June 15, 2020 The May 31st, 2020 update is the ninth major update in Islands. This update introduces vending machines , new merchant quests , and furniture .

Changes [ ]

Added [ ]

Vending Machine

Quests [ ]

Merchants now have quests Collect blueprints and rewards by leveling up the merchant by doing quests.

Blocks [ ]

Carpentry Workbench is now craftable, allowing you to craft the following furniture: Wooden Chair Wooden Stool Wooden Table Large Red Bed King's Blue Bed '70s Single Bed '70s Rug Red Sofa Red Cloth Table Barbecue Outdoor Table Picnic Table Outdoor Bench Fireplace Umbrella '70s Lime Table '70s Bed White Rug Flat Red Bed '70s Chair Outdoor Stool Red Lounge Chair

Modified [ ]

Recipes [ ]

Umbrella 's crafting recipe has been fixed.

Bug fixes [ ]

Minor bug fixes Xbox users aren't able to eat food Breaking a Vending Machine wouldn't give you its contents
