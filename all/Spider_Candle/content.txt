Spider Candle Health 90 Behavior Hostile Attack strength 12 Drops Zombie Cletus Token Jukebox Disk Location Hub A spider candle was a hostile mob that spawned in the Hub during the Halloween 2022 event.

Spawning [ ]

Hub [ ]

Spider candles spawned in the Hub during the Halloween 2022 event.

Zombie Cletus [ ]

Zombie Cletus may summon 1 - 7 spider candles to aid him in battle, with the amount summoned in proportion to the number of players fighting him.

Behavior [ ]

Spider candles are small mobs who have a moderate attack speed. They deal 12 damage per hit. Spider candles have a relatively small size which limits the feasibility in using melee weapons .

Drops [ ]

A spider candle rewards 5 Combat XP (18 XP with the 4x daily bonus) and 5 Light Melee , Heavy Melee , Magic , or Archery XP (18 with the 4x daily bonus) depending on the weapon the player used to defeat it.
