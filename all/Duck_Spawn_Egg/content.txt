Duck Spawn Egg Buy cost Tier 1: 15,000 coins Required skill Tier 1: Level 40 Animal Care (to buy) Description Spawn a duck with tier 1/2/3 trait maximums. Eats bread. The duck spawn egg is a spawn egg used to spawn ducks on player islands . There are 3 tiers of the egg; one can be bought while the other two can only be obtained through breeding ducks.

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Tier 1 duck spawn eggs can be purchased from Taliyah for 15,000 coins each after reaching level 40 Animal Care .

Breeding [ ]

Any tier of this egg can be obtained by breeding two ducks. To breed ducks, the player must feed them each a potion of fertility during the day (in-game). The traits of the two animals will determine the tier of the spawn egg.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

Players can only spawn ducks if they have not reached the small animal limit and are the owner of the island they are on. While holding the egg, they can click/tap anywhere on their screen to spawn a duck.

History [ ]

Trivia [ ]

Duck spawn eggs are the third-most expensive item the player can buy from Taliyah , the most being yak spawn eggs . The turkey spawn egg tier 2 used to share the same model as duck spawn eggs before April 16, 2022 .
