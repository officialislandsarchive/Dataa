The June 26, 2020 update or more commonly known as the Fruits and Cooks update was the 11th consecutive update and a major update. This update introduced many new features, including fruits, and auto-cooking food via the Legacy Food Processor , which was a theoretical item thought to have been obtained by glitching prior to the update.
