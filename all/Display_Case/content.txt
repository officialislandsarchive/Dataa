Display Case Small Large Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe Small Display Case: 15 Oak Wood 5 Glass Block Large Display Case: 10 Glass Block 30 Oak Wood Crafted with Furniture Workbench Best tool Luminous Transparent Display cases are blocks used to store and display fireflies . Large display cases can hold 6 firefly jars , while small display cases can only hold 3.
