Truffle Barrel Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe 75 Maple Wood 25 Pine Wood Crafted with Animal Bench Required skill Level 34 Animal Care Best tool Luminous Transparent The truffle barrel is a block used to automate collecting truffles from pigs .

Usage [ ]

Collecting truffle [ ]

If a pig digs up a truffle it will bring it to the barrel. A truffle barrel can hold up to 3 truffles at a time.
