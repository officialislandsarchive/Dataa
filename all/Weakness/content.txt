Weakness Causes Weakens enemies Source Weapons whose magical class is void Type Attack

Weakness is an effect that affects hostile mobs when attacked by weapons whose magical class is void .

Source [ ]

Weakness will come in effect when attacking hostile mobs with a weapon whose magical class is void such as the spirit spellbook , serpent's hook , and the reaper's scythe .

Causes [ ]

Weakness adds a weakened effect that reduces enemy damage by 20% while under the effect, which can stack. The enemy is also surrounded by purple gas when under the effect.
