Opal Pickaxe Hilt Description The hilt for an opal pickaxe, crafted on the anvil. An opal pickaxe hilt is an item used to craft the opal pickaxe .

Obtaining [ ]

Dragon eggs [ ]

Opening an infernal dragon egg has a 9% chance of rewarding an opal pickaxe hilt, having a rarity of 'rare'.
