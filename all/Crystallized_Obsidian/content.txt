Crystallized Obsidian Dropped by Bandit Description Used to craft Obsidian Weaponry on the anvil Crystallized obsidian is the crystallized version of obsidian .

Obtaining [ ]

Mining [ ]

Crystallized obsidian has a 1.5% chance of dropping when breaking a obsidian rock . It can only drop by fully breaking an obsidian rock, and can never be dropped by breaking it to its halfway point.

Mob drop [ ]

Bandits have a 1% of dropping a crystallized obsidian upon defeat.

Purchasing [ ]

Crystallized obsidian can be bought from Evans for 10 doubloons .

Treasure chest [ ]

Opening a treasure chest has a 10% chance of rewarding crystallized obsidian, having a rarity of 'uncommon.'
