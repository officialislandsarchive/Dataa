Blue Sticky Gear Dropped by Blue Slime Slime King Description Valuable advanced crafting component The blue sticky gear is a rare mob drop from blue slimes and an uncommon drop from the Slime King . It is one of the three variants of sticky gears , the other two being the green sticky gears and pink sticky gears .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Blue slimes have a 10% chance of dropping a blue sticky gear and a 50% drop chance from the Slime King.
