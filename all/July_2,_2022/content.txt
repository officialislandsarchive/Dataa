July 2, 2022 Basic Information Day released July 2, 2022 Version number 88 Version Navigator Previous version Next version June 25, 2022 July 9, 2022 The July 2, 2022 update, also known as the the Slime Queen update, is an update in Islands that added the Slime Queen and other related items.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added the Slime Queen . Added Slime Queen Pets . Added Slime Queen Pet Spawn Eggs . Added Slime Queen Tokens . Added Slime Queen Orbs . Added Slime Queen Crystals . Added Void Blocks . Added Trash Cans . Added icons for villagers that appear above them in the Hub . Added decors next to villagers.

Modifications [ ]

Adjusted various locations of the Hub . Removed the Art Museum building; however Quinn and the leaderboard are still in the game. Expanded Slime Island to include the Slime Queen spawn area . Updated Thomas ', Jack 's, and Arius ' model. Reduced the range of randomized spawn boss locations. Increased sell price of wheat from 5 coins to 12 coins. Decreased sell price of potatoes from 11 coins to 9 coins. Improved the UI with the new fonts, and revamped the dialogue UI. Added icons to the season text in the inventory .

July 3, 2022 [ ]

Added more decor to the Hub. Fixed the positions of Doug , the Slime Island portal, and Ivon .
