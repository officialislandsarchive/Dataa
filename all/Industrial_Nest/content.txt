An industrial nest is an industrial machine that automates the egg collecting process on chickens and ducks . It can also be used to automate the feather collecting process on vultures .

Usage [ ]

Eggs [ ]

The industrial nest can be used to automatically collect eggs from chickens and ducks when ready. It has 3 slots able to hold 3 eggs each, and when a chicken or duck has laid eggs, it will automatically go towards the industrial nest to collect eggs, provided that its maximum capacity has not yet been reached. The player will receive 15 Animal Care XP (60 with the 4x daily bonus).
