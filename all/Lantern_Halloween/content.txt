Lantern Halloween Type of block Solid block; Luminous Best tool Luminous Transparent The lantern Halloween is a furniture block that has a chance to be obtained from cauldrons .

Obtaining [ ]

Cauldron [ ]

Opening a cauldron has a 7% chance of rewarding a lantern Halloween, having a rarity of 'uncommon'.
