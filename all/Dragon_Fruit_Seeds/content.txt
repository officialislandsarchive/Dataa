Dragon Fruit Seeds Planted on Trellis Buy cost 5,000 coins Required skill Level 41 Farming Description Plant in a trellis to grow dragon fruit. Dragon fruit seeds are seeds that can be grown into dragon fruit .

Obtaining [ ]

Purchasing [ ]

Dragon fruit seeds can be purchased from Tom for 5,000 coins each after reaching level 41 Farming .

Harvesting [ ]

Harvesting a dragon fruit crop will give 1 - 4 dragon fruits and 1 dragon fruit seed.

Usage [ ]

Planting [ ]

Dragon fruit seeds can be planted on trellises to be grown into dragon fruits.

Growth [ ]

The growth time of dragon fruits can be shortened by 20% when watered with a watering can or basic sprinkler , or fertilized with basic fertilizer . If watered and fertilized, the growth time will be shortened by 30%.
