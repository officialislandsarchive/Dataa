Crab Pet Spawn Egg The crab pet spawn egg is a spawn egg used to spawn a pet crab . It cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Treasure chests [ ]

Opening a treasure chest has a 0.25% chance of rewarding a crab pet spawn egg, having a rarity of 'mythic'.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

To spawn a crab , the player must be the owner of the island they are currently on. While holding the egg, clicking or tapping anywhere on their screen will spawn a crab.

History [ ]

July 17, 2021 Added crab pet spawn eggs.

Trivia [ ]

The crab pet spawn egg was the first pet spawn egg added into the game.
