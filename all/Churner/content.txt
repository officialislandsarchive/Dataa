Usage [ ]

Transforming [ ]

Churners can be used to transform eggs into mayonnaise jars and yak milk buckets into yak butter . It takes 3 minutes and 30 seconds to fully transform each item.

Input Output 1 Egg ➡ 1 Mayonnaise Jar 1 Yak Milk Bucket ➡ 1 Yak Butter

History [ ]

February 26, 2021 Added mayonnaise spinners. March 25, 2022 Renamed the mayonnaise spinner to "Churner." Changed the description from "Spin your eggs into mayonnaise." to "Churner used to process a bunch of raw ingredients." Churners can now be used to transform yak milk buckets into yak butter . April 1, 2022 10 items can be placed inside of the churner instead of 1.

Trivia [ ]

Despite being unlocked in the Animal Care skill, it is not crafted in the animal bench unlike most items in this skill level.
