February 19, 2022 Basic Information Day released February 19, 2022 Version number 71 Version Navigator Previous version Next version February 7, 2022 February 27, 2022 The February 19, 2022 update, also known as the duck update, is an update in Islands that introduced ducks .

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added Ducks Added Duck Spawn Eggs Added Duck Eggs Added four new food items: Poached Duck Egg Duck Eggs Benedict Scrambled Duck Eggs Potato and Duck Egg Scramble Added new smooth blocks: Smooth Stone Smooth Slate Smooth Marble Added new carved blocks: Stone Carved Aquamarine Carved Slate Carved Marble Carved Added new tiled blocks: Stone Tiles Aquamarine Tiles Slate Tiles Marble Tiles Added new auction items : Athlete Statue Capitalist Statue Formal Statue Gentleman Statue Proud Statue

Modifications [ ]

Changed wheat sell price from 9 coins to 7 coins Changed radish sell price from 64 coins to 70 coins Changed smooth aquamarine's texture. Removed Yuzi Lunar New Year-themed items can no longer be purchased Reduced speed effect of chili peppers from +50% to +25% speed Renamed the Chicken Nest to "Nest". Adjusted lighting settings Reduced entity lag Other bug fixes

Other [ ]

Auctions are now held every Wednesday and on the weekends
