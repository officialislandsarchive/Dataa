Islands Third Anniversary Cake Type of block Solid block Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Celebrate three years of Islands with a delicious birthday cake! The Islands third anniversary cake is a decorative block given out to players from April 14, 2023 - April 28, 2023 .

Obtaining [ ]

Taliyah [ ]

From April 14, 2023 - April 28, 2023 , Taliyah visited player islands during Islands' 3 year anniversary . Interacting with her on the player's own island will reward them the cake.

History [ ]

April 14, 2023 Added Islands third anniversary cakes. April 28, 2023 Islands third anniversary cakes are now unobtainable.

Trivia [ ]

Like the Islands anniversary cake and Islands second anniversary cake , the Islands third anniversary cake is not edible; it is purely meant for decoration.
