Diamond Mines Portal Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe 1 Diamond Mines Portal Shard 15 Enchanted Diamond 150 Stone Brick Crafted with Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4 Required skill Level 46 Combat (To use) Best tool Luminous Transparent Description A placeable portal that allows quick-travel to Diamond Mines. The diamond mines portal is a placeable portal that allow players to directly travel to the Diamond Mines through the player's island .

Usage [ ]

Traveling [ ]

When a player touches the purple part of the portal, they will be teleported to the Diamond Mines . If their Combat level is below 46, a UI element will pop-up telling them they do not have a high enough Combat level to enter, and it won't teleport the player.
