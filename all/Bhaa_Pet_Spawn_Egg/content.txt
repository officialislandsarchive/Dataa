Bhaa Pet Spawn Egg Dropped by Bhaa The Bhaa pet spawn egg is a spawn egg used to spawn a Bhaa pet . It cannot be dropped, traded , or put in vending machines and chests .

Obtaining [ ]

Mob drop [ ]

Bhaa has a 0.33% chance of dropping a Bhaa pet spawn egg upon defeat.

Usage [ ]

Spawning [ ]

To spawn a Bhaa pet , the player must be the owner of the island they are currently on. While holding the egg, clicking or tapping anywhere on their screen will spawn a Bhaa pet.
