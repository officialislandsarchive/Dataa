March 13, 2021 Basic Information Day released March 13, 2021 Version number 38 Version Navigator Previous version Next version March 5, 2021 March 19, 2021

The March 13, 2021 update, also known as the Slime King and Seasons update, added a new boss mob as well as a new crop and a recurring crop. When seasons were introduced, the first one to take place was Spring .

Changes [ ]

Additions [ ]

Seasons [ ]

Added 4 Seasons . Seasons alternate every 24 hours. Spring Summer Fall Winter Cletus visit the Player's Island during Spring and Fall to sell seasonal seeds. The colors of trees and grass change during each seasons. Added wind .

Radish [ ]

Added the Radish crop, which can only grow during spring. Added Radish Seeds .

Other [ ]

Added the Slime King . Added a Settings Menu which allows players to control volume, notifications, and shadows in-game. Removed villager animations in the Hub .

Modifications [ ]

Petur now offers to buy all food items, rather than making 5 offers per day. Pumpkins were re-introduced and can now only grow during the Fall . Cletus visits the Player's Island and offers to sell Pumpkin Seeds and buy Pumpkins . Opening the inventory shows the current season above the search bar. Additions to Slime Island were made to house the Slime King boss. Blocks can now cast a shadow, until turned off in the Settings. Friend notifications and joining can be disabled in the Settings. Item tooltips are now visible in the animal inspect menu.
