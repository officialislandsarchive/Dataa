Usage [ ]

Transporting fuel [ ]

Pipes can be connected to any machine or tank with a pipe input or output, pipes will only appear full if they are connected to something on both sides. To split a pipe two ways, use a pipe junction , otherwise pipes snap to other pipes and turn automatically.
