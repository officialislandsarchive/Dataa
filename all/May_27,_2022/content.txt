May 27, 2022 Basic Information Day released May 27, 2022 Version number 83 Version Navigator Previous version Next version May 20, 2022 June 3, 2022 The May 27, 2022 update is a minor update in Islands that reworked magic weapons and visitor permissions .

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Revamped the Visitor Permissions UI . Added an option to teleport players. Added the ability to ban and unban players. Added a search bar to search for players. A warning now shows up when changing the permissions of a player. A confirmation now shows up when resetting a join code.

Modifications [ ]

Charging spellbooks , tidal spellbooks , and ruby staffs will now increase its damage. Improved particle effects on magic weapons. 3 gilded steel rods are now required to craft gilded steel pickaxes and gilded steel axes , down from 5. Increased chances of fishing eels , seaweed seeds , and propellers when fishing . Changed the skill requirements of certain items : Catfish now requires level 12 Combat to sell. Groupers and eels now require level 20 Combat to sell. Tilapia and swordfish now require level 36 Combat to sell. Cactus spikes now require level 36 Combat to craft. Infernal fish now require level 55 Combat to sell. The dragonslayer now requires level 55 Combat to craft. Rageblades now require level 60 Combat to craft. Tidal spellbooks now require level 20 Magic to craft. Ruby staffs now require level 36 Magic to craft. Conveyor sensors now require level 36 Forging to craft, down from 50. Input/output chests now require level 40 Forging to craft, down from 62. Opal pickaxes now require level 55 Mining to craft. Opal axes now require level 44 Woodcutting to craft. Iron sickles now require level 20 Farming to craft. Gilded steel sickles now require level 36 Farming to craft. Diamond sickles now require level 50 Farming to craft. Vending Machine Tier 2 now require level 70 Economy to craft. Increased the sell price of avocados from 32 coins to 36 coins. Reduced the sell price kiwis from 32 coins to 24 coins.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed an issue where join codes didn't reset immediately. Fixed the fireball offset when using magic weapons. Fixed an issue were certain furniture blocks were still locked after using its blueprint . Fixed an issue where the progress bar would get stuck when fishing.
