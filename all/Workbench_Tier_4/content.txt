Workbench Tier 4 Type of block Solid block; Interactable Recipe 250 Red Bronze Ingot 250 Hickory Wood Crafted with Workbench Tier 3 Workbench Tier 4 Best tool Luminous Transparent Description Used to craft advanced industrial island craftables. The workbench tier 4 is a crafting station used to craft some of the most advanced items . Like the other workbenches, it has more crafting recipes than its predecessor, the workbench tier 3 , and is currently the highest tiered workbench.

Once placed, players may spawn oil resource islands using oil resource deeds .

Upgrading [ ]

The workbench tier 3 can be upgraded into a workbench tier 4 with 250 red bronze ingots and 250 hickory wood . Upgrading the crafting station will replace the workbench tier 3 with a workbench tier 4.

History [ ]

August 15, 2021 Added workbench tier 4s. November 4, 2022 Workbench tier 4s can craft pet egg stands , large diamond chests , and expanded diamond chests . November 11, 2022 Workbench tier 4s can craft sewing stations. December 16, 2022 Workbench tier 4s can craft ATMs .

Trivia [ ]

This crafting station has more recipes than any other crafting station in the game. It is also the only crafting station that emits light.
