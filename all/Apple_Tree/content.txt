Apple Tree Sapling Spring Summer Fall Winter Type of block Solid block Spawns Player's Island Best tool Drops 3 - 4 Oak Wood 1 Apple Tree Sapling Luminous Transparent The apple tree is a fruit tree added in the June 26, 2020 update.

The canopies of apple trees change color based on the current in-game season ; dark green in spring , light green in summer , dark red in fall , and white in winter , but its sapling always has light green leaves on its canopy.

Obtaining [ ]

Natural generation [ ]

Players have a 25% chance of starting with an apple tree on their island upon island creation.

Harvesting [ ]

Apple tree saplings have a 3% chance of dropping whenever an apple is harvested from the tree. It can be planted on grass to be grown into an apple tree. It takes 1 minute and 50 seconds for an apple tree sapling to grow into an apple tree.

Usage [ ]

Harvesting [ ]

Apples grow every season on apple trees and can be obtained by harvesting them from the tree. There can be up to three apples on a tree at a time, and whenever an apple is harvested, there is a 3% chance of getting an additional apple tree sapling. Harvesting an apple yields 6 Farming XP (24 XP with the 4x daily bonus). It takes x minutes for an apple tree to grow an apple, adding up to a total of xx minutes to grow three apples.

Apple trees can be harvested with leaf clippers to drop 2 leaves .

History [ ]

June 26, 2020 Added apple trees.

Trivia [ ]

If an apple tree is chopped down while having apples on it, it will only drop the sapling and not any of the apples.
