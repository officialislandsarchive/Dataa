Usage [ ]

Merging wood tubes [ ]

Wood tube mergers can be used to merge two wood tube pathways into one output. The output tube is the center tube perpendicular to the two input tubes.

Trivia [ ]

There is a visual bug where fluid within wood tube mergers will appear black instead of their usual colour when one wood tube merger is connected to another.
