March 3, 2023 Basic Information Day released March 3, 2023 Version number 117 Version Navigator Previous version Next version February 17, 2023 March 17, 2023 The March 3, 2023 update, also known as the farming tools update, is an update in Islands that added a new farming tool, reworked Desert Island , bug fixes, and other changes.

Content [ ]

Additions [ ]

Added the Master Harvester achievement . Added crystalline plow . Added crystalline ivy seeds . Added crystallines . Added industrial nests . Added new villagers : Added Rick . Added Maria .

Modifications [ ]

Increased the sell price of onions from 30 coins to 37 coins. Decreased the sell price of watermelons from 80 coins to 74 coins. Added a new toggle in the Settings for highlighting entities on player islands . The Hide Icons setting is now on by default. Added a Host tag for island owners when using chat on Published islands.

Bug fixes [ ]

Fixed a bug preventing rageblades from damaging mobs . Fixed a bug causing animals to jitter when near players. Fixed a hold seed placement for PC. Fixed a bug causing block placement not to register when looking into the void or buildings underneath players. Fixed inconsistent trellis seed planting placement. Fixed a bug causing players to be unable to place blocks underneath properly, causing some blocks to be placed on the opposite side of another block.
